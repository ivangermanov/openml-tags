**Meta Album Sports Actions Dataset (Micro)**

The Sports Actions dataset is a collection of sports images covering various sports. Images are in .jpg format and were gathered from internet searches. The images were scanned with a duplicate image detector program and all duplicate images were removed. For Meta Album, the dataset is preprocessed and images are resized into 128x128 pixels using Open-CV.
