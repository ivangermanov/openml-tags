The Train Dataset, tagged under Machine Learning and Social Media testing, comprises various features that describe characteristics of passengers. The dataset includes information such as Passenger Identification, Survival Status, Passenger Class, Full Name, Gender, Age, Number of Siblings or Spouses on board, Number of Parents or Children on board, Ticket Details, Travel Fare, Cabin Information, and Port of Embarkation.
