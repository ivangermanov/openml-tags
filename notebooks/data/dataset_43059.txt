Real Ad Exchange Two Tags: Online Advertisement Clicking Rates, where the metrics are Cost Per Click and Cost Per Thousand Impressions. Features: Time Stamp, Cost Per Click, Cost Per Thousand Impressions.

This dataset focuses on the realm of online advertisement clicking rates, providing valuable insights into the performance of advertisements based on two primary metrics: Cost Per Click, which measures the cost of each individual click on an ad, and Cost Per Thousand Impressions, which calculates the cost of displaying an ad to one thousand users. The dataset includes a time stamp feature, allowing for analysis of how these metrics change over time.

Keywords: Online Advertisement, Clicking Rates, Cost Per Click, Cost Per Thousand Impressions, Time Stamp.
