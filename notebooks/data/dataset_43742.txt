Content
The dataset contains a wealth of information, including the route taken, embarkation and disembarkation ports, cabin type, duration of the cruise, region of the cruise, and the number of weeks prior to booking and cancellation. Additionally, it includes the embarkation and debarkation regions, price paid, and the number of people on the booking.
