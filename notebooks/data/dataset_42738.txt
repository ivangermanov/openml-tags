Open Payments

Tags: Payments given by Healthcare Manufacturing Companies to Medical Doctors or Hospitals

Features: 
Applicable Manufacturer or Company Making Payment Name, 
Dispute Status for Publication, 
Name of Associated Covered Medical Device or Supply, 
Name of Associated Covered Drug or Biological, 
Physician Specialty, 
Status

This dataset provides information on payments made by healthcare manufacturing companies to medical doctors or hospitals. The features of this dataset include the name of the applicable manufacturer or company making the payment, the dispute status for publication, the name of the associated covered medical device or supply, the name of the associated covered drug or biological, the physician specialty, and the status.

Keywords: Healthcare, Payments, Manufacturing Companies, Medical Doctors, Hospitals
