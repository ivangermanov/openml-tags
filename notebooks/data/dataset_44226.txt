Newspaper Churn Tags

This dataset includes records of individuals who are or were subscribers to this newspaper. The dataset contains demographic information such as household income, home ownership status, whether the subscriber has children, ethnicity, year of residence, age range, language spoken, and Nielsen Prizm information. Additionally, the dataset includes geographic information like address, state, city, county, and zip code. The dataset also provides information on the delivery period chosen by the subscriber and the weekly charge associated with it. Furthermore, the dataset includes the number of rewards subscribers have used and the source channel through which they were initially recruited. Finally, the dataset indicates whether the customer is still an active subscriber or not.

Features: Subscription Identification, Household Income, Home Ownership Status, Ethnicity, Presence of Children, Year of Residence, Age Range, Language Spoken, Address, State, City, County, Zip Code, Weekly Fee, Delivery Period, Nielsen Prizm Information, Reward Program, Source Channel, Subscription Status

Keywords: Newspaper Subscription, Demographic Data, Geographic Information, Customer Retention, Reward Programs
