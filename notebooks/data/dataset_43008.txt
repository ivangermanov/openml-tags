Heart Failure Dataset

Tags: test

Features:

* Age of the patient in years
* Presence of anemia, a decrease of red blood cells or hemoglobin
* Level of creatine phosphokinase enzyme in the blood
* Presence of diabetes
* Ejection fraction, the percentage of blood leaving the heart at each contraction
* Presence of high blood pressure
* Platelet count in the blood
* Sex of the patient, either woman or man
* Level of serum creatinine in the blood
* Level of serum sodium in the blood
* Presence of smoking habit
* Follow-up period in days
* Death event, whether the patient died during the follow-up period

Dataset Information

A detailed description of the dataset can be found in the dataset section of the paper.

Scraped Data

The dataset has no missing values.

Additional Variable Information

The dataset consists of thirteen clinical features that are used to predict the survival of patients with heart failure.
