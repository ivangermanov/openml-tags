Wine 

Tags: Computer Systems, Mathematics 

Dataset used in the Tabular Data Benchmark, transformed in the same way, belonging to the Classification on Numerical Features benchmark.

Original Source: Wine Quality Archive

This dataset consists of various features, including Acidity Levels, Sugar Content, Sulfur Dioxide Levels, Density, PH Levels, Sulphate Content, and Alcohol Content, which are used to determine the overall Quality of the wine.

Please give credit to the original Wine Quality Archive source if you use this dataset.
