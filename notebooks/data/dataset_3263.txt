The database was created with records of Absenteeism at Work from July two thousand seven to July two thousand ten at a courier company in Brazil. The data set allows for several new combinations of attributes and attribute exclusions, or the modification of the attribute type, categorical, integer, or real, depending on the purpose of the research.

### Attribute Information:

One. Individual Identification. 
Two. Reason for Absence, which includes twenty-one categories, such as Certain Infectious and Parasitic Diseases, Neoplasms, Diseases of the Blood and Blood-Forming Organs, and so forth.
Three. Month of Absence. 
Four. Day of the Week, including Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday. 
Five. Seasons, consisting of Summer, Autumn, Winter, and Spring. 
Six. Transportation Expense. 
Seven. Distance from Residence to Work, measured in kilometers. 
Eight. Service Time. 
Nine. Age. 
Ten. Work Load Average per Day. 
Eleven. Hit Target. 
Twelve. Disciplinary Failure, which is either yes or no. 
Thirteen. Education Level, categorized as High School, Graduate, Postgraduate, Master, and Doctor. 
Fourteen. Number of Children. 
Fifteen. Social Drinker, which is either yes or no. 
Sixteen. Social Smoker, which is either yes or no. 
Seventeen. Number of Pets. 
Eighteen. Weight. 
Nineteen. Height. 
Twenty. Body Mass Index. 
Twenty-One. Absenteeism Time in Hours, which is the target.
