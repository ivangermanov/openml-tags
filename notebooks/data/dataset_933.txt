Systematic Determination of Genetic Network Architecture 

The Yeast Gene dataset is categorized under Chemistry and Life Science. This dataset consists of seventeen instances and one thousand eight hundred eighty-four genes. The data was originally published in the article "Systematic Determination of Genetic Network Architecture" in Nature Genetics and later reused in "Biclustering of Expression Data". 

The dataset is available in ARFF format without labeled classes. It includes various gene features, including those related to yeast chromosome one left arm, yeast chromosome one right arm, yeast chromosome two, and many others.

Keywords: Yeast Gene, Genetic Network Architecture, Chemistry, Life Science, Gene Features, ARFF Format.
