The ACSPublicCoverage dataset is a collection of data related to computer systems and machine learning. This dataset was reproduced from a research paper that explores various aspects of public coverage. The dataset comprises several features, including Age, School Completion, Marital Status, Sex, Disability, English Speaking Proficiency, Citizenship, Migration, Military Service, Ancestry, Nativity, Hearing Impairment, Eye Impairment, Remittance, Personal Income, Employment Status, State, Fertility, Race, and Public Coverage.

Keywords: ACSPublicCoverage, Computer Systems, Machine Learning, Public Coverage, Demographic Data, Socioeconomic Features
