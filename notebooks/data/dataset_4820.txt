The Credit dataset is categorized under Human Activities and Sociology, and is part of the classification on numerical features benchmark. It was originally sourced from the Give Me Some Credit competition overview.

This dataset features various attributes, including revolving utilization of unsecured lines, age, number of times thirty to fifty nine days past due but not worse, debt ratio, monthly income, number of open credit lines and loans, number of times ninety days late, number of real estate loans or lines, number of times sixty to eighty nine days past due but not worse, and number of dependents. The primary target variable is serious delinquency in two years.
