The Credit dataset, categorized under Health and Social Media, originates from the Tabular Data Benchmark and has undergone transformation in the same manner. This dataset falls within the classification on numerical features benchmark category.

The original source of this dataset is Give Me Some Credit, a competition hosted on Kaggle. It is essential to acknowledge the original source when utilizing this dataset.

The features included in this dataset are Revolving Utilization of Unsecured Lines, Age, Number of Times Thirty to Fifty-Nine Days Past Due Not Worse, Debt Ratio, Monthly Income, Number of Open Credit Lines and Loans, Number of Times Ninety Days Late, Number of Real Estate Loans or Lines, Number of Times Sixty to Eighty-Nine Days Past Due Not Worse, and Number of Dependents.

Keywords: Credit, Classification, Numerical Features, Health, Social Media, Tabular Data, Benchmark, Kaggle.
