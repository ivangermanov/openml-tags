The Train Dataset, categorized under Meteorology and Transportation, contains various features related to passenger information.

This dataset consists of attributes such as Passenger Identity, Survival Status, Passenger Class, Full Name, Gender, Age, Number of Siblings or Spouses Aboard, Number of Parents or Children Aboard, Ticket Details, Travel Fare, Cabin Information, and Port of Embarkation.

Keywords: Train, Meteorology, Transportation, Passenger, Survival, Travel
