Data Science YouTube Channels Video Metadata

This dataset contains metadata of various Data Science YouTube channel videos. 

Acknowledgements
The data was scraped from the Digital Methods Initiative Tool Database.

Cover Photo
The cover photo is courtesy of Rachit Tank on Unsplash.

Motivation
This dataset was inspired by the need to provide a comprehensive collection of Data Science YouTube channel video metadata. 

Possible uses for this dataset include conducting sentiment analysis or categorizing YouTube videos based on their comments and statistics. The dataset can also be used to train machine learning algorithms, such as recurrent neural networks, to generate their own YouTube descriptions. Additionally, the dataset can be used to identify the most popular Data Science YouTube channels based on total likes, dislikes, and view counts. Furthermore, statistical analysis can be performed on different channels to gain insights into their performance.

Features
The dataset includes the following features: channel ID, channel title, video ID, publication date, video title, video description, video category ID, video category label, duration, dimension, definition, caption, thumbnail, licensed content, view count, like count, dislike count, favorite count, and comment count.
