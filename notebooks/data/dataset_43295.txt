Pair0001: DWD Data

The dataset consists of climate data from the German Weather Service (Deutscher Wetterdienst) collected at 349 stations. The data was obtained from the German Weather Service's website, which provides free access to climate data for Germany.

The dataset contains information on altitude and temperature, with the temperature values being averages over the period from 1961 to 1990.
