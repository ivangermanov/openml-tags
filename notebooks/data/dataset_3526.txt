The receptor is involved in various biological processes, including signal transduction, phagocytosis, and smooth muscle cell migration. 

The dataset includes information about the expression of this receptor in different tissues and its role in various diseases. Additionally, it provides information about the ligands that bind to this receptor and their effects on its activity.
