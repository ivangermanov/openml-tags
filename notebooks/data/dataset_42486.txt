Name: Hate News Dataset 
Tags: Manufacturing, Social Media 

Features: Hate Speech, Transgender Issues 

This dataset focuses on the manufacturing of hate speech and its dissemination through social media platforms, particularly when it comes to transgender issues. The dataset aims to explore how hate speech is created, spread, and consumed online, and how it affects marginalized communities such as the transgender community.

Keywords: Hate Speech, Social Media, Transgender Issues, Online Harassment, Marginalized Communities
