The BNG Wine Dataset 

Tags: Artificial, BNG, Chemistry, Life Science 

Features: This wine class contains various chemical properties, including alcohol levels, malic acid, ash content, and alkalinity of ash. Additionally, it comprises mineral levels such as magnesium, as well as total phenols, flavanoids, non-flavanoid phenols, and proanthocyanins. The color intensity and hue of the wine are also included, along with the OD280/OD315 of diluted wines and proline levels.

Keywords: Wine, Chemical Properties, Class, Phenols, Minerals, Color Intensity
