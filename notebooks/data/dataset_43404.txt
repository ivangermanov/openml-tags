The Three Gorges Dam Water Data Tags: The Three Gorges Dam is a hydroelectric gravity dam located on the Yangtze River, near the city of Yichang, in the Hubei province of China. It has a total water storage capacity of thirty-nine point three billion cubic meters. The dam is situated at the geographic coordinates thirty degrees forty-nine minutes north and one hundred eleven degrees east.

This dataset contains the measurements of water storage and discharge from the Three Gorges Dam, from April seventeenth, two thousand eleven, up to August thirty-first, two thousand twenty. The content includes the following:

Measurement Date: the date of the reservoir water measurement
Upstream Water Level: the depth of water upstream in meters
Downstream Water Level: the depth of water downstream in meters
Inflow Rate: the reservoir water inflow rate in cubic meters per second
Outflow Rate: the reservoir water outflow rate in cubic meters per second
