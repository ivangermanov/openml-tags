Austin Weather Tags: Context

This dataset is meant to complement the Austin Bike Sharing Dataset. The content contains the date, high temperature in Fahrenheit, average temperature in Fahrenheit, low temperature in Fahrenheit, high dew point in Fahrenheit, average dew point in Fahrenheit, low dew point in Fahrenheit, high humidity as a percentage, average humidity as a percentage, low humidity as a percentage, high sea level pressure in inches, average sea level pressure in inches, low sea level pressure in inches, high visibility in miles, average visibility in miles, low visibility in miles, high wind speed in miles per hour, average wind speed in miles per hour, highest wind speed gust in miles per hour, total precipitation in inches, and adverse weather events if any.

This dataset contains data for every date from the start of the data collection period to the end of the data collection period. 

Acknowledgements: This dataset was obtained from Weather Underground at the Austin KATT station.

Inspiration: Can we use this dataset to explain some of the variation in the Austin Bike Sharing Dataset?

Features: Date, High Temperature, Average Temperature, Low Temperature, High Dew Point, Average Dew Point, Low Dew Point, High Humidity, Average Humidity, Low Humidity, High Sea Level Pressure, Average Sea Level Pressure, Low Sea Level Pressure, High Visibility, Average Visibility, Low Visibility, High Wind Speed, Average Wind Speed, Highest Wind Speed Gust, Total Precipitation, Adverse Weather Events

Keywords: Weather, Austin, Bike Sharing, Temperature, Humidity, Pressure, Visibility, Wind Speed, Precipitation.
