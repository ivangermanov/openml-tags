The Balanced Dataset of the DOA BWIn Dataset represents a fifty-fifty split between the classes of the dependent variable. This dataset has been balanced using the SMOTE algorithm in the R Package Themis. 

The dataset features a range of variables, including Country Identification, Gender, Age Relative to Date, and several metrics related to betting behavior, such as total stakes, total bets, and total days active. Additionally, the dataset includes variables related to betting patterns, including the slope of stakes and total active days. The dependent variable is also included.

Keywords: Balanced Dataset, DOA BWIn, SMOTE Algorithm, Betting Behavior, Dependent Variable
