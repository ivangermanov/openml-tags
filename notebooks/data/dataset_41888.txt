**FOREX aud/chf - Hour - Close**

Tags: Computer Systems, Economics, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Hour

This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex Australian Dollar/Swiss Franc currency pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one hour-long candlestick. The entire dataset spans from January to December and excludes weekends, as Forex markets are closed during these periods. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean close price in the subsequent hour compared to the current hour's mean close price. In other words, the class is true if the mean close price rises in the next hour and false if it falls or remains stagnant.

Attributes:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.
* Bid Open: The opening bid price for this time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Close: The closing bid price for this time interval.
* Bid Volume: The frequency of changes in the bid price within this time interval.
* Ask Open: The opening ask price for this time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Close: The closing ask price for this time interval.
* Ask Volume: The frequency of changes in the ask price within this time interval.
* Class: Whether the average price will increase during the next interval.
