2019 Ironman World Championship Results

Tags: Context Data set

This dataset contains results from the 2019 Ironman World Championship in Kona, Hawaii, providing valuable insights into which countries produce the fastest overall finish times, as well as the fastest swim, bike, and run times.

The dataset comprises the name of each male and female competitor, the country they represent, their category (whether they are a professional or age group athlete), their overall placing, finish time, and detailed splits for the swim, transition one, bike, transition two, and run segments.

The dataset features include: Bib Number, Athlete Name, Country, Gender, Division, Swim Time, Bike Time, Run Time, Overall Time, Division Rank, Gender Rank, and Overall Rank.

Keywords: Ironman, World Championship, Triathlon, Kona, Hawaii, Athlete Performance, Country Rankings.
