Ceres Discovery Data Tags: Observations of the Dwarf Planet Ceres

This dataset comprises twenty-two observations of the dwarf planet Ceres, which were published in the September edition of the Monthly Correspondence in eighteen hundred and one. These measurements were utilized to fit a model of the planet's orbit, ultimately leading to its recovery three months later.

Features: Tag, Mean Solar Time, Right Ascension in Time, Right Ascension in Degrees, Northern Declination, Geocentric Longitude, Geocentric Latitude, Position of the Sun with Twenty Arcseconds Aberration, Logarithm of the Distance

Keywords: Ceres, Dwarf Planet, Orbital Model, Astronomical Observations, Geocentric Coordinates
