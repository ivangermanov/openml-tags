Stock Information 
Tags: Context 

The Stock Information dataset provides context information about public companies tradable on the Robinhood website. This dataset contains useful information about public companies being traded in the US stock market. It offers valuable insights into various aspects of these companies, including their size, market capitalization, price-to-earnings ratio, listing date, and more.

The dataset includes essential details about each company, such as its simple name, full name, stock symbol, country of origin, and initial margin ratio. It also provides trading-related information, including the day trade ratio, maintenance ratio, and default collar fraction. Additionally, the dataset includes financial metrics, such as the opening, high, and low prices, trading volume, and average trading volume over two weeks and overall.

Furthermore, the dataset provides important statistics, including the fifty-two-week high and low prices, market capitalization, price-to-book ratio, price-to-earnings ratio, and dividend yield. It also includes information about the company's float, shares outstanding, and a brief description of its business activities. 

Other relevant details include the company's CEO, headquarters location, sector, industry, number of employees, and founding year.
