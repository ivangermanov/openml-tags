The Titanic Dataset for Survival Prediction in Machine Learning and Sociology Studies

This dataset is focused on predicting the survival of passengers on the ill-fated ship, RMS Titanic, using various features related to the passengers' demographics and travel information.

The primary goal of this analysis is to explore how different factors contributed to the survival rates of individuals on board, taking into account their social class, gender, age, ticket fare, port of embarkation, and family relationships.

The key features examined in this study include the passenger's survival status, social class, sex, age, ticket fare, embarkation location, and number of relatives accompanying them, as well as their title or occupation.

Keywords: Titanic, machine learning, sociology, survival prediction, passenger demographics, travel information.
