Titanic 
Tags: Titanic Survival Prediction 

The dataset is focused on predicting the survival of passengers on the Titanic, a British passenger liner that sank in the North Atlantic Ocean in nineteen twelve. 

Features of the dataset include whether the passenger survived, their social class, gender, age, the cost of their ticket, the port at which they boarded the ship, and the number of relatives they were traveling with, as well as their title, such as Mr, Mrs, or Miss.
