**FOREX aud sgd Day Close**

Tags: Economics, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Day, Machine Learning

**Source:** Dukascopy Historical Data Feed

This dataset contains the historical price data of the Forex Australian Dollar/Singapore Dollar from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one day. The entire dataset spans a specific period of time and does not include weekends, as the Forex market is not traded on weekends.

The timezone of the feature Timestamp is Europe/Amsterdam. The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean of the Close Bid and the Close Ask of the following day, relative to the Close Bid and Close Ask mean of the current minute. In other words, the class attribute is true when the mean Close price is expected to increase the following day, and false when the mean Close price is expected to decrease or remain unchanged the following day.

**Attributes:**

* **Timestamp:** The time of the current data point in the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.
* **Bid Open:** The bid price at the start of this time interval.
* **Bid High:** The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Low:** The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Close:** The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* **Bid Volume:** The number of times the Bid Price changed within this time interval.
* **Ask Open:** The ask price at the start of this time interval.
* **Ask High:** The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Low:** The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Close:** The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* **Ask Volume:** The number of times the Ask Price changed within this time interval.
* **Class:** Whether the average price will increase during the next interval.

Keywords: Forex, Australian Dollar, Singapore Dollar, Historical Data, Candlestick, Machine Learning
