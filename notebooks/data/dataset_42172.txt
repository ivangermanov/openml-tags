This dataset focuses on the Sex, Ethnic Origin, Age, Weight, Height, and eating habits of individuals. The features of this dataset include the consumption of bread, cereals, and biscuits, as well as rice, pasta, semolina, and potatoes. It also explores the intake of legumes, both dry and fresh, fruits, dairy products, meat or eggs, fish, ready-to-eat meals, sugary products, appetizer biscuits, and dietary supplements. Additionally, the dataset examines the consumption of sweetened beverages and alcoholic drinks. The presence of Overweight is also a feature of this dataset.

Keywords: Diet, Nutrition, Eating Habits, Health, Food Consumption
