Open Payments Dataset 

Tags: Astronomy, Images 

This dataset contains information about payments given by healthcare manufacturing companies to medical doctors or hospitals. The data provides insights into the financial relationships between these companies and medical professionals.

Features:

Applicable Manufacturer or Group Purchasing Organization Making Payment Name 
Dispute Status for Publication 
Name of Associated Covered Device or Medical Supply 
Physician Specialty 
Status 

Keywords: Open Payments, Healthcare Manufacturing, Medical Doctors, Hospitals, Financial Relationships, Physicians
