Abalone 

Tags: Chemistry, Derived, Life Science 

The Abalone dataset is a three-class version of the original dataset. 

Sources: 
The original owners of the database are the Marine Resources Division, Marine Research Laboratories, located in Taroona, Department of Primary Industry and Fisheries, Tasmania. 
The donor of the database is the Department of Computer Science, University of Tasmania.

This dataset consists of various physical measurements of abalones, including their characteristics. 
The characteristics of the abalones were measured and recorded for further analysis. 
The purpose of this dataset is to provide a comprehensive understanding of abalones through their physical measurements.
