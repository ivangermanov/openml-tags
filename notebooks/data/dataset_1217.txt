This dataset, known as Click Prediction Small, is a derived dataset used for study purposes. It represents a reduced sample of an earlier version, focusing on click prediction in online advertising. The dataset includes various features related to user interactions, ad placements, and content identifiers. These features encompass click and impression data, URL hashes, advertisement and advertiser identifiers, as well as positional information such as depth and position. Additionally, it incorporates query, keyword, title, and description identifiers, along with user identifiers to track individual user behavior.
