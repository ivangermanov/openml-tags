This dataset, Detailed Analysis on Campus Recruitment, consists of placement data of students in a campus. It includes secondary school percentage and board, as well as higher secondary school percentage, board, and stream. Additionally, it includes degree percentage, type, and specialisation, as well as work experience and salary offers to the placed students. We will analyse what factors are playing a major role in order to select a candidate for job recruitment.

The dataset features include gender, secondary school percentage, secondary school board, higher secondary school percentage, higher secondary school board, higher secondary school stream, degree percentage, degree type, work experience, employment test percentage, specialisation, Master of Business Administration percentage, status, and salary.

Keywords: campus recruitment, placement data, student analysis, job selection, salary offers.
