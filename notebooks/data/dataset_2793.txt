**FOREX eur hkd day Close**

Tags: Astronomy, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Day

This is the historical price data of the Forex Euro Hong Kong Dollar from Dukascopy. Each instance, or row, represents one candlestick of one day. The entire dataset spans a specific date range, excluding weekends, as the Forex market is not active on these days. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute is determined by the direction of the mean close price of the following day, relative to the mean close price of the current day. Specifically, the class attribute is true when the mean close price increases in the following day, and false when the mean close price decreases or remains the same.

The attributes included in this dataset are:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in the Europe Amsterdam time zone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the start of the time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during the time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during the time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of the time interval.
* Bid Volume: The frequency of changes to the bid price within the time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the start of the time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during the time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during the time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of the time interval.
* Ask Volume: The frequency of changes to the ask price within the time interval.
* Class: The direction of the mean close price change in the following day.
