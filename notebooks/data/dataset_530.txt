Tags: Chemistry, Life Science, Myth Busting, Stat Lib, Study One, Study Fifteen, Study Twenty, Study Forty One 

This dataset contains one hundred ten data sets from the book The Statistical Sleuth. The book was published by Duxbury Press in nineteen ninety-seven.

Class Type: Numeric 
Class Index: None Specific 

Features: Soil Value, Age Group, Tree Length, Air Exposure, Water Type, Body Height, Hair Length, Flower Type, Tree Trunk, Skin Tone, Knee Length
