The Debutanizer Column is part of a Desulfuring and Naphtha Splitter Plant. 

Top Temperature, Top Pressure, Reflux Flow, Flow to Next Process, Sixth Tray Temperature, Bottom Temperature, and Bottom Temperature are all important variables in the Debutanizer Column. 

The Butane Content in the Debutanizer Bottoms, which is also the Naphtha Splitter Feed, is a key output of the process. 

For more information, see Appendix A.3 of Fortuna.
