This dataset reflects incidents of crime in the City of Los Angeles dating back to the year two thousand and ten. This data is transcribed from original crime reports that are typed on paper and therefore there may be some inaccuracies within the data. The address fields are only provided to the nearest hundred block in order to maintain privacy. This data is as accurate as the data in the database. Please note any questions or concerns in the comments.

The dataset includes various features such as the crime report number, the date the crime was reported, the date the crime occurred, the time the crime occurred, the area identification number, the area name, the reporting district, the crime code, a description of the crime code, the modus operandi codes, the age of the victim, the sex of the victim, the descent of the victim, the premise code, a description of the premise, the weapon used code, a description of the weapon, the status code, a description of the status, and the location of the crime.

The address of the crime is also provided, but only to the nearest hundred block, along with the cross street. This is done to maintain the privacy of those involved.

Keywords: Los Angeles, crime, incidents, reports, privacy, database, crime code, victim, weapon, location.
