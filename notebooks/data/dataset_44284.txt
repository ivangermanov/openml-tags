**Meta Album Sports Actions Dataset (Mini)**

The one hundred sports dataset is a collection of sports images covering seventy-three different sports. Images are in jpg format and were gathered from internet searches. The images were scanned with a duplicate image detector program and all duplicate images were removed. For Meta Album, the dataset is preprocessed and images are resized into one twenty-eight by one twenty-eight pixels using Open-CV.
