Weather Izmir Tags: 

The Weather Izmir dataset contains the weather information of Izmir from January first, nineteen ninety-four to December thirty-first, nineteen ninety-seven. From the given features, the goal is to predict the mean temperature. 

This dataset provides a comprehensive overview of the weather patterns in Izmir during this period, with a range of attributes that capture various aspects of the weather. 

Attributes Information 

The dataset includes the following attributes: maximum temperature, minimum temperature, dew point, precipitation, sea level pressure, standard pressure, visibility, wind speed, maximum wind speed, and mean temperature. The mean temperature is the target attribute that we aim to predict based on the other attributes. 

Keywords: weather, Izmir, temperature, precipitation, pressure, visibility, wind speed.
