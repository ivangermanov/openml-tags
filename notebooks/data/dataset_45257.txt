Pigs Bayesian Network Sample Five

This Bayesian network is part of the bnlearn repository, specifically listed under the discrete-verylarge category. The network consists of four hundred forty-one nodes and five hundred ninety-two arcs, resulting in a total of five thousand six hundred eighteen parameters. The average Markov blanket size is approximately three point six six, and the average degree is two point six eight. The maximum in-degree of the network is two.

The network features a wide range of variables, including but not limited to: pig characteristics, health indicators, and environmental factors.
