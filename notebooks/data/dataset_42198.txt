This dataset contains, for each Premier League match, the probabilities generated with the Learning to Forecast models, as well as match odds. The dataset provides a comprehensive overview of each match, including the identities of the home and away teams, the likelihood of each possible outcome, and the number of goals scored by each team. Additionally, the dataset includes the odds of each outcome as provided by the bookmaker Bet Three Six Five.

Features: 
Home Team, Away Team, Probability of Away Team Winning, Probability of a Draw, Probability of Home Team Winning, Match Index, Home Team Goals, Away Team Goals, Bet Three Six Five Home Win Odds, Bet Three Six Five Draw Odds, Bet Three Six Five Away Win Odds

Keywords: Premier League, Football, Match Odds, Learning to Forecast, Bookmaker
