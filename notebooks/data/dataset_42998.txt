Metro Interstate Traffic

Tags: Computer Systems, Human Activities

Hourly Interstate Ninety Four Westbound traffic volume for the Minnesota Department of Transportation Automated Traffic Recorder station three hundred one, roughly midway between Minneapolis and Saint Paul, Minnesota. Hourly weather features and holidays are included for their impacts on traffic volume.

This dataset contains hourly Interstate Ninety Four Westbound traffic volume for the Minnesota Department of Transportation Automated Traffic Recorder station three hundred one, which is located roughly midway between Minneapolis and Saint Paul, Minnesota. In addition to the traffic volume, this dataset also includes hourly weather features and holidays, which can have significant impacts on traffic volume.

The dataset does not contain any missing values.

Additional variable information:

Holiday - This categorical variable indicates whether a particular day is a United States national holiday or a regional holiday, including the Minnesota State Fair.
Temperature - This numeric variable represents the average temperature in Kelvin.
Rain One Hour - This numeric variable measures the amount of rainfall in millimeters that occurred during the hour.
Snow One Hour - This numeric variable measures the amount of snowfall in millimeters that occurred during the hour.
Clouds All - This numeric variable represents the percentage of cloud cover.
Weather Main - This categorical variable provides a brief textual description of the current weather conditions.
Weather Description - This categorical variable provides a longer textual description of the current weather conditions.
Date Time - This datetime variable represents the hour of the data collection in local Central Standard Time.
Traffic Volume - This numeric variable represents the hourly Interstate Ninety Four Westbound traffic volume reported by the Minnesota Department of Transportation Automated Traffic Recorder station three hundred one.
