Timing Attack Dataset 

Tags: Chemistry, Life Science 

Bleichenbacher Timing Attack: A dataset created to study and analyze timing attacks, specifically focusing on a thirty-five microsecond delay.

Attribute Descriptions:

The dataset consists of various attributes related to three types of messages: Change Cipher Spec, TLS Alert, and TCP Disconnect.

For the Change Cipher Spec message, there are attributes describing the source and destination ports, sequence numbers, acknowledgment numbers, header lengths, flags, window sizes, checksum status, urgent pointers, options, and timestamps.

The TLS Alert message has similar attributes, including content type, length, alert level, and description.

The TCP Disconnect message also shares some common attributes with the other two messages, including source and destination ports, sequence numbers, acknowledgment numbers, header lengths, flags, window sizes, checksum status, urgent pointers, options, and timestamps.
