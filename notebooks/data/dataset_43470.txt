ANTM JK Stock Market Dataset from Two Thousand Five to January Two Thousand Twenty-One

Tags: Overview

This dataset contains historical daily prices for all tickers currently trading on ANTM JK since the twenty-ninth of September two thousand five until the third of February two thousand twenty-one. PT Aneka Tambang Tbk, colloquially known as Antam or ANTM JK in the Stock Market, is an Indonesian mining company. The company primarily produces gold and nickel, and is the largest producer of nickel in Indonesia.

The dataset was retrieved from Finance Yahoo - PT Aneka Tambang Tbk.

Background

There were news reports stating that the increase in ANTM Stock was due to the impact of public sentiment on the battery holding industry. Moreover, the electric vehicle manufacturer from the United States, Tesla, plans to build a battery factory in Indonesia, and the increasing nickel price and nickel ore sales volume in the domestic market are factors that influence Antam's stock trend. In addition, the impact of influencers from Indonesia who have followed stock movements in the last few months has encouraged people to invest in various sectors, one of which is stock, and that is Antam's Stock.

Data Structure

The column meanings in the dataset are as follows: Date specifies the trading date; Open is the price at which a stock started trading when the opening bell rang; High is the highest price at which a stock traded during a period; Low is the lowest price at which a stock traded during a period; Close is the price of an individual stock when the stock exchange closed shop for the end of period; Adjusted Close is the adjusted close price adjusted for both dividends and splits; Volume is the number of shares that changed hands during a given period.

Features: Date, Open, High, Low, Close, Adjusted Close, Volume
