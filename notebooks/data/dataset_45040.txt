This synthetic dataset, known as Intersectional Bias Assessment, contains demographic and clinical data used to train a classifier to predict a diagnosis of either schizophrenia or depression, and assess the model performance for intersectional bias. This dataset is used in the tutorial An Intersectional Approach to Model Construction and Evaluation in Mental Health Care. The dataset features include diagnosis, sex, race, housing status, delay, anhedonia, depressed mood, sleep patterns, tiredness, appetite, rumination, concentration, psychomotor activity, delusion, suspiciousness, withdrawal, passive behavior, tension, and unusual thought patterns.

Keywords: mental health, diagnosis, intersectional bias, demographic data, clinical data, model performance.
