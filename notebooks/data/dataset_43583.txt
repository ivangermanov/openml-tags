Water Capture by Method

Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning

Context:
Although it doesn't rain frequently in Los Angeles, the city employs various methods to capture rainfall, thereby increasing our local water supply. This dataset displays the cumulative amount of water captured this season as well as today.

Content:
Each row in this dataset corresponds to a specific date and time at which a measurement was taken. The measurements encompass water captured in Rain Barrels and Cisterns, Incidental Capture, Green Infrastructure Capture, and so forth. For further details, please refer to the Data tab of this dataset.

Methods:
This dataset was created utilizing Kaggle's API from the City of Los Angeles' Open Data Portal.

Inspiration:
How much precipitation occurs in Los Angeles? What sources contribute the most to rain capture?

Features: Rain Barrels and Cisterns Capture, Green Infrastructure Capture, Incidental Capture, Rain In, Spreading Capture, Timestamp, Total Capture

Keywords: Water Capture, Rainfall, Los Angeles, Local Water Supply, Measurement, Green Infrastructure.
