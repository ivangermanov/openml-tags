BNG (Anneal)

Tags: Artificial, BNG, Chemistry, Life Science

This BNG product features a strong family of steel alloys, characterized by their high carbon content, exceptional hardness, and ability to undergo temper rolling. The resultant condition provides excellent formability, strength, and resistance to ageing.

The surface finish and quality of this alloy are superior, making it ideal for enamelability. It also exhibits excellent properties such as BC, BF, BT, BW/ME, BL, M, Chrom, Phos, CBond, Marvi, Exptl, Ferro, Corr, Blue/Bright/Varn/Clean, Lustre, JuroFM, S, P.

Available in various shapes, thicknesses, widths, and lengths, this alloy can be supplied with an oil coating and specialized packaging. Additionally, it can be bored to specific dimensions, and its class rating ensures reliability and consistency.
