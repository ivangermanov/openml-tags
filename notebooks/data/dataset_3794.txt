Mental Health in Tech Survey 
Tags: Life Science, Machine Learning 

Dataset Information 
This dataset is from a survey that measures attitudes towards mental health and frequency of mental health disorders in the tech workplace. It is highly recommended to analyze data from the ongoing survey as well.

Content 
This dataset contains the following data:

* Timestamp
* Age
* Gender
* Country
* State: If you live in the United States, which state or territory do you live in?
* Self-Employed Status: Are you self-employed?
* Family History: Do you have a family history of mental illness?
* Treatment Status: Have you sought treatment for a mental health condition?
* Work Interference: If you have a mental health condition, do you feel that it interferes with your work?
* Number of Employees: How many employees does your company or organization have?
* Remote Work Status: Do you work remotely (outside of an office) at least half of the time?
* Type of Company: Is your employer primarily a tech company or organization?
* Benefits Provided: Does your employer provide mental health benefits?
* Care Options Knowledge: Do you know the options for mental health care your employer provides?
* Wellness Program Discussion: Has your employer ever discussed mental health as part of an employee wellness program?
* Help Resources: Does your employer provide resources to learn more about mental health issues and how to seek help?
* Anonymity Protection: Is your anonymity protected if you choose to take advantage of mental health or substance abuse treatment resources?
* Medical Leave Ease: How easy is it for you to take medical leave for a mental health condition?
* Perception of Consequences: Do you think that discussing a mental health issue with your employer would have negative consequences?
* Physical Health Consequence Perception: Do you think that discussing a physical health issue with your employer would have negative consequences?
* Coworker Discussion Willingness: Would you be willing to discuss a mental health issue with your coworkers?
* Supervisor Discussion Willingness: Would you be willing to discuss a mental health issue with your direct supervisor(s)?
* Interview Discussion Willingness: Would you bring up a mental health issue with a potential employer in an interview?
* Comparison of Seriousness: Do you feel that your employer takes mental health as seriously as physical health?
* Observed Consequences: Have you heard of or observed negative consequences for coworkers with mental health conditions in your workplace?
* Additional Comments: Any additional notes or comments

Inspiration 
Some questions worth exploring include how the frequency of mental health illness and attitudes towards mental health vary by geographic location, and what are the strongest predictors of mental health illness or certain attitudes towards mental health in the workplace?
