The dataset freMTPL Two Frequency contains risk features for motor third-party liability policies, which were observed mostly over a period of one year. For more information, please refer to the GitHub repository CASdatasets. This dataset is associated with the book Computational Actuarial Science with R. 

The dataset provides information on various policy features, including the policy identifier, the number of claims, exposure, area, vehicle power, vehicle age, driver age, bonus-malus, vehicle brand, vehicle gas, density, and region.

Keywords: motor insurance, liability policies, risk features, actuarial science
