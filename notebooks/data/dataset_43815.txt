Data on the Geographic Distribution of COVID-19 Cases Worldwide

Tags: Data on the geographic distribution of COVID-19 cases worldwide

The dataset includes the reported number of diagnosed cases and deaths each day in every country around the world. The main fields include the date, the number of reported cases, the number of reported deaths, and the country. This dataset is a direct transformation of the original Excel file provided by the European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control. The European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control is the provider of the data, and the dataset is posted here to increase visibility and allow easier access to the community.

Features: Date of Report, Day, Month, Year, Cases, Deaths, Countries and Territories, Country Territory Code, Population Data

Keywords: COVID-19, Geographic Distribution, Cases, Deaths, Countries, European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control
