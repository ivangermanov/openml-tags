**FOREX chfjpy-hour-Close**

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Hour, Life Science, Machine Learning

This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex CHF/JPY currency pair obtained from Dukascopy Historical Data Feed.

Each instance represents one candlestick of one hour duration. The entire dataset spans from January to December, excluding weekends since Forex markets are closed during these days. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.

The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean close price of the following hour relative to the mean close price of the current minute. Specifically, it is true if the mean close price increases during the next hour and false if it decreases or remains unchanged.

Attributes:

* **Timestamp**: The time of the current data point in Europe/Amsterdam timezone.
* **Bid Open**: The bid price at the start of this time interval.
* **Bid High**: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Low**: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Close**: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* **Bid Volume**: The number of times the bid price changed within this time interval.
* **Ask Open**: The ask price at the start of this time interval.
* **Ask High**: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Low**: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Close**: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* **Ask Volume**: The number of times the ask price changed within this time interval.
* **Class**: Whether the average price will increase during the next interval.
