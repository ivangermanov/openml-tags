Nutritional Values for Common Foods and Products
Tags: Agriculture, Machine Learning

I came across this data and couldn't resist sharing it with others who might be interested in food nutrition values.

This dataset contains nutritional information for approximately eight thousand types of food products. The feature names are self-explanatory, so I won't provide a detailed description.
