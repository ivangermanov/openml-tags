Stack Overflow Polarity Train 

Tags: Machine Learning, Sentiment Analysis, Statistics 

A gold standard dataset consisting of approximately four thousand four hundred questions, answers, and comments from Stack Overflow, which have been manually annotated for their polarity. This dataset has been utilized for the development of the Emotional Manipulation Toolkit, also known as EMTk, which specializes in detecting polarity from technical texts. 

The dataset features include an identification number, the actual polarity, and the corresponding text.

Keywords: sentiment analysis, machine learning, statistics, polarity detection, technical texts
