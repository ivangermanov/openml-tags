Airlines Departure Delay Prediction Dataset 

Tags: Manufacturing, Social Media 

This dataset is focused on predicting airline departure delays. The original data can be accessed at the Bureau of Transportation Statistics website. This particular version has been processed to forecast departure delays in seconds.

The full dataset spans from nineteen eighty-seven to two thousand thirteen and can be downloaded as a comma-separated values file. This specific version includes only the first ten million rows, along with a selection of the most relevant columns, formatted in Attribute-Relation File Format.

The key features used for prediction include the month of travel, day of the month, day of the week, scheduled departure time, scheduled arrival time, carrier information, origin airport, destination airport, and flight distance.
