The Echo Months dataset has been binarized, converting its numeric target feature into a two-class nominal target feature. This process involves calculating the mean and subsequently categorizing all instances with a lower target value as Positive and all others as Negative.

This dataset is relevant to the fields of Chemistry and Life Science, and is particularly useful for myth-busting and studying various phenomena.

The dataset includes several features, including whether an individual was still alive, their age, and various heart-related metrics.
