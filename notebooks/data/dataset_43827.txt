City Quality of Life Dataset
Tags: Context

Teleport.org provides data related to the quality of life for the most creative cities around the world, which enables users to find suitable cities for living and working according to their personal preferences. This dataset was obtained from Teleport API endpoints and pre-processed before being uploaded to Kaggle.

The dataset features a range of indicators that capture various aspects of city living, including the quality of housing, cost of living, startup ecosystem, venture capital investment, travel connectivity, commute time, business freedom, safety, healthcare, education, environmental quality, economy, taxation, internet access, leisure and culture, tolerance, and outdoor activities.

Keywords: city quality of life, creative cities, living preferences, startup ecosystem, travel connectivity, business freedom, safety, healthcare, education, environmental quality.
