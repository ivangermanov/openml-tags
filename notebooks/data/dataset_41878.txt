**FOREX aud/usd Hourly Close**

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Hour, Life Science, Machine Learning

**Source:** Dukascopy Historical Data Feed

**Data Description**

This dataset comprises historical price data of the Forex Australian Dollar vs United States Dollar (AUD/USD) from Dukascopy. Each instance, represented by a single row, corresponds to one hourly candlestick. The entire dataset spans from January to December, excluding weekends when the Forex market is closed.

The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam time zone. The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean close price movement over the subsequent hour relative to the current hourly mean close price. Specifically, the class attribute is assigned a value of true if the mean close price is expected to rise over the next hour and false if it remains stagnant or declines.

**Attributes**

* **Timestamp**: The time stamp of each data point in the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.
* **Bid Open**: The opening bid price at the commencement of each hourly interval.
* **Bid High**: The highest bid price recorded during each hourly interval.
* **Bid Low**: The lowest bid price observed during each hourly interval.
* **Bid Close**: The closing bid price at the conclusion of each hourly interval.
* **Bid Volume**: The frequency of bid price changes occurring within each hourly interval.
* **Ask Open**: The opening ask price at the beginning of each hourly interval.
* **Ask High**: The highest ask price reached during each hourly interval.
* **Ask Low**: The lowest ask price recorded during each hourly interval.
* **Ask Close**: The closing ask price at the end of each hourly interval.
* **Ask Volume**: The number of times the ask price changed within each hourly interval.
* **Class**: A binary indicator signifying whether the mean close price is anticipated to increase or decrease over the subsequent hour.
