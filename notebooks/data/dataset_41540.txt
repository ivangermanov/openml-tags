Name: Black Friday
Tags: OpenML Reg Customer Purchases

This dataset contains information about customer purchases on Black Friday. The features of this dataset include the customer's gender, age, occupation, city category, number of years they have stayed in their current city, marital status, and the categories of products they have purchased, including the first, second, and third product categories. The purchase amount is also included in the dataset.

Keywords: Black Friday, customer purchases, demographics, product categories, purchase amount
