Student Performance Dataset Tags: Student Performance Features

This dataset explores the relationship between various factors and student performance. It examines how a student's gender, race and ethnicity, and parental level of education impact their academic achievement. Additionally, it considers the effects of lunch programs and test preparation courses on student outcomes. The dataset includes metrics such as math scores, reading scores, and writing scores to provide a comprehensive understanding of student performance.

Keywords: student performance, academic achievement, demographics, educational outcomes
