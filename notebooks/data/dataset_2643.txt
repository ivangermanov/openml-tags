**FOREX US Dollar Japanese Yen Day Close**

Tags: Culture, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Day, Politics

This dataset contains the historical price data of the US Dollar Japanese Yen currency pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one day. The entire dataset spans a specific period and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is not active on these days.

The timestamp feature is based on the Europe Amsterdam timezone. The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean close price of the following day relative to the current day's mean close price. In other words, if the mean close price is expected to rise the following day, the class attribute is true; otherwise, it is false.

**Attributes**

* **Timestamp**: The time of the current data point in the Europe Amsterdam timezone.
* **Bid Open**: The bid price at the start of this time interval.
* **Bid High**: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Low**: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Close**: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* **Bid Volume**: The number of times the bid price changed within this time interval.
* **Ask Open**: The ask price at the start of this time interval.
* **Ask High**: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Low**: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Close**: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* **Ask Volume**: The number of times the ask price changed within this time interval.
* **Class**: Whether the average price will increase during the next interval.
