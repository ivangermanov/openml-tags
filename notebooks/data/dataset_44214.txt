Reading Hydro Upstream Data Set

Tags: Upstream Data from Twin Archimedes Screw Hydro-Electric Generator on the River Thames at Caversham Weir, Reading, United Kingdom

This data set features a collection of readings taken from the upstream side of the twin Archimedes screw hydro-electric generator located on the River Thames at Caversham Weir in Reading, United Kingdom. The data includes a timestamp for each reading, which allows for the tracking of changes over time.

Keywords: hydroelectric, Archimedes screw, River Thames, Caversham Weir, Reading, United Kingdom
