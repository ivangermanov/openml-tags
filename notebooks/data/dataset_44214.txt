Name: Reading Hydro Upstream

Tags: Upstream data from the Twin Archimedes Screw Hydro-Electric Generator on the River Thames at Caversham Weir, Reading, United Kingdom.

This dataset features data collected from the upstream section of the Twin Archimedes Screw Hydro-Electric Generator, a unique and innovative system located on the River Thames at Caversham Weir in Reading, United Kingdom. The dataset provides valuable insights into the performance and operation of this hydro-electric generator, which is an important source of renewable energy.

Keywords: Hydro-Electric Generator, River Thames, Caversham Weir, Reading, Renewable Energy
