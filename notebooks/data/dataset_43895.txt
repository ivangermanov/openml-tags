IBM Employee Performance Tags: IBM Employee Attrition Data

The dataset used in the code pattern is supplied by Kaggle and contains Human Resources analytics data of employees who stay and leave. The types of data include metrics such as Education Level, Job Satisfaction, and Commute Distance. The dataset was obtained from the IBM Employee Attrition AIF360 repository.
