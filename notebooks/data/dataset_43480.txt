Toronto COVID-19 Cases 

Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning 

This data set contains demographic, geographic, and severity information for all confirmed and probable cases reported to and managed by Toronto Public Health since the beginning of the pandemic. This includes cases that are sporadic, occurring in the community, and outbreak-associated cases. The data are extracted from the provincial communicable disease reporting system and Toronto's custom COVID-19 case management system and combined for reporting purposes. The data in this spreadsheet are subject to change as public health investigations into reported cases and continuous quality improvement initiatives are ongoing, and additional cases continue to be reported.

The data set features include case identification, whether the case is associated with an outbreak, age group, neighbourhood name, forward sortation area, source of infection, classification, episode date, reported date, client gender, outcome, hospitalization status, intensive care unit status, intubation status, and history of hospitalization, intensive care unit admission, and intubation.

Keywords: COVID-19, Case Management, Public Health, Data Reporting, Demographic Data, Geographic Information, Severity Information.
