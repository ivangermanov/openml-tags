## **Meta-Album Omni Print MD Five Bis Dataset (Mini)**

The Omni Print MD Five Bis dataset consists of synthesized character images with a specific set of nuisance parameters. It contains twenty-eight thousand two hundred forty images, each measuring one hundred twenty-eight pixels by one hundred twenty-eight pixels, in RGB format, from seven hundred six categories.
