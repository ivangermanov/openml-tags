FOREX Eur/Aud Minute Close

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Social Media, Sociology

This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex Eur/Aud pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one minute. The entire dataset spans a specific period and does not include weekends, as the Forex market is not active during these days.

The timestamp feature is based on the Europe/Amsterdam time zone. The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean close price of the subsequent minute relative to the mean close price of the current minute. Specifically, the class attribute is true if the mean close price increases in the next minute, and false if it decreases or remains unchanged.

Attributes:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the beginning of this time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* Bid Volume: The frequency of changes in the bid price within this time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the beginning of this time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* Ask Volume: The frequency of changes in the ask price within this time interval.
* Class: The direction of the mean close price in the subsequent minute.
