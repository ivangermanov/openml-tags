EMNIST Balanced 

Tags: Earth Science, Meteorology, Vision 

The EMNIST Balanced dataset is a comprehensive collection of images featuring various earth science and meteorology-related concepts. This extensive dataset comprises numerous image samples, each represented by a set of pixel values.

Features:

Pixels 

This dataset is particularly useful for computer vision tasks, allowing researchers to develop and train models capable of recognizing and interpreting visual data related to earth science and meteorology.
