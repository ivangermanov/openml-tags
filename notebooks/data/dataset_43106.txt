Chronic Kidney Disease Dataset

Tags: Chronic Kidney Disease, Machine Learning, ML, Survival Analysis

We use the following representation to collect the dataset:

* Age: age in years
* Blood Pressure: blood pressure in millimeters of mercury
* Specific Gravity: specific gravity (1.005, 1.010, 1.015, 1.020, 1.025)
* Albumin: albumin (0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5)
* Sugar: sugar (0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5)
* Red Blood Cells: red blood cells (normal, abnormal)
* Pus Cell: pus cell (normal, abnormal)
* Pus Cell Clumps: pus cell clumps (present, not present)
* Bacteria: bacteria (present, not present)
* Blood Glucose Random: blood glucose random in milligrams per deciliter
* Blood Urea: blood urea in milligrams per deciliter
* Serum Creatinine: serum creatinine in milligrams per deciliter
* Sodium: sodium in milliequivalents per liter
* Potassium: potassium in milliequivalents per liter
* Hemoglobin: hemoglobin in grams
* Packed Cell Volume: packed cell volume
* White Blood Cell Count: white blood cell count in cells per cubic millimeter
* Red Blood Cell Count: red blood cell count in millions per cubic millimeter
* Hypertension: hypertension (yes, no)
* Diabetes Mellitus: diabetes mellitus (yes, no)
* Coronary Artery Disease: coronary artery disease (yes, no)
* Appetite: appetite (good, poor)
* Pedal Edema: pedal edema (yes, no)
* Anemia: anemia (yes, no)
* Class: class (chronic kidney disease, not chronic kidney disease)

Attribute Information:

The dataset consists of 24 attributes and a class attribute, making a total of 25 attributes. There are 11 numeric attributes and 14 nominal attributes.
