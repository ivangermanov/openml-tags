**FOREX eur/dkk - Minute - Close**
Tags: Demographics, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Geography

**Data Description**
This dataset contains the historical price data of the Forex Euro-Danish Krone currency pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one minute. The entire dataset spans a specific time period, excluding weekends, since the Forex market is not traded on weekends.

The timezone of the feature Timestamp is set to Europe/Amsterdam. The class attribute is determined by the direction of the mean of the Close Bid and the Close Ask prices of the following minute, relative to the Close Bid and Close Ask mean of the current minute. In other words, the class attribute is true when the mean Close price increases in the following minute, and false when the mean Close price decreases or remains the same in the following minute.

**Attributes**

* **Timestamp**: The time of the current data point, specified in the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.
* **Bid Open**: The bid price at the start of this time interval.
* **Bid High**: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Low**: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Close**: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* **Bid Volume**: The number of times the bid price changed within this time interval.
* **Ask Open**: The ask price at the start of this time interval.
* **Ask High**: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Low**: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Close**: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* **Ask Volume**: The number of times the ask price changed within this time interval.
* **Class**: A binary indicator of whether the average price will increase or decrease in the next interval.
