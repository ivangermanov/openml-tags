**Buenos Aires Airbnb Data**

Tags: Machine Learning, Statistics

Context:
For over ten years, Airbnb has become a preferred personalized accommodation option for travelers globally. This dataset provides comprehensive details of Airbnb listings in Buenos Aires.

Content:
This dataset encompasses all relevant information about hosts, geographical availability, and essential metrics required to make predictions and conduct analysis.

Acknowledgments:
This publicly available dataset was published by Airbnb.

Inspiration:
What insights can be gained about various hosts? Which areas in Buenos Aires experience high demand? Who are the busiest hosts and what factors contribute to their popularity?

Features:
Identification, Name, Host Identification, Host Name, Neighborhood, Latitude, Longitude, Room Type, Price, Minimum Nights Required, Number of Reviews, Last Review Date, Monthly Review Rate, Total Host Listings Count, Annual Availability
