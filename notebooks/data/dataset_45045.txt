Original Description: The Zurich Public Transport Delay Data set consists of data collected between October thirty, three thirty AM, Central European Time, and November twenty seventh, one twenty AM, Central European Time. The data was cleaned and prepared during the Open Data Day event.

The dataset provides information about various factors affecting delays in public transportation, including the type of vehicle, direction of travel, day of the week, temperature, wind speed, precipitation, dew point, humidity, time of day, and minute of the day. The target variable is the delay itself.
