World Top Chess Players (August)

Tags: Context

The International Chess Federation, a governing body responsible for overseeing international chess competitions, utilizes the Elo rating system to calculate the relative skill levels of chess players. This system provides an objective measure of a player's performance.

This comprehensive dataset contains detailed information about all chess players worldwide, ranked according to their Standard FIDE Rating in descending order, as published by the International Chess Federation in August. The dataset encompasses both active and inactive players, which can be distinguished by the Inactive Flag column. It is essential to note that all ratings have been updated based on the official publication by the International Chess Federation in August.
