Arrhythmia Dataset:

This dataset is a binarized version of the original dataset, where the multi-class target feature has been converted into a two-class nominal target feature. The majority class is labeled as Positive, while all other classes are labeled as Negative.

Features:

The dataset includes various physiological and demographic characteristics, including Age, Sex, Height, Weight, Heart Rate, and several ECG-related measurements such as QRS Duration, PR Interval, QT Interval, and T Interval. Additionally, the dataset includes numerous features related to ECG waveforms, including the presence and characteristics of different types of waves, such as Q Waves, R Waves, S Waves, and RP Waves, among others.

Keywords: Arrhythmia, ECG, Heart Rate, Physiological Characteristics, Demographic Data
