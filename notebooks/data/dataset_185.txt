This comprehensive baseball database contains detailed statistics for players, encompassing various aspects of their performance on the field. The data includes offensive metrics such as hits, runs, and home runs, as well as defensive statistics like fielding average. It also tracks career longevity through the number of seasons played and total games participated in. Advanced statistical measures like on base percentage and slugging percentage provide deeper insights into player effectiveness. The database also notes whether a player has been inducted into the Hall of Fame, offering a measure of career achievement and recognition.
