BNG (Primary Tumor)

Tags: Artificial, BNG, Chemistry, Life Science

This dataset features information about various aspects of primary tumors, including the age and sex of patients, as well as the histologic type of tumor present. The degree of differentiation of the tumor cells is also included, providing insight into the aggressiveness of the cancer.

The dataset also explores the presence of tumors in various bodily locations, such as the bone, bone marrow, lung, pleura, peritoneum, liver, brain, skin, neck, supraclavicular region, axillary region, mediastinum, and abdominal cavity. Furthermore, it examines the classification of these tumors based on their characteristics.

Keywords: Primary Tumor, Histologic Type, Degree of Differentiation, Bodily Locations, Classification.
