The Midwest Survey dataset contains individual responses from surveys about regional identification conducted by SurveyMonkey. 

Features of this dataset include responses to the question "In your own words, what would you call the part of the country where you live?", as well as questions about personal identification as a Midwesterner. 

Additionally, the dataset includes questions about whether certain states are considered part of the Midwest, including Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Michigan, Minnesota, Missouri, Nebraska, North Dakota, Ohio, South Dakota, and Wisconsin. 

It also includes questions about whether Arkansas, Colorado, Kentucky, Oklahoma, Pennsylvania, West Virginia, Montana, and Wyoming are considered part of the Midwest. 

Other features of the dataset include the respondent's ZIP Code, gender, age, household income, education, and location by Census Region.

Keywords: Midwest, regional identification, SurveyMonkey, Midwesterner, states, demographics.
