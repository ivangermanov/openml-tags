Name: Colorectal Cancer Dataset
Tags: Dataset from R Package RelSurv

This dataset is known as the Colorectal Cancer Dataset and is part of the RelSurv package in R. It contains various features that are relevant to the study of colorectal cancer. These features include the patient's sex, age at diagnosis, diagnosis details, time of diagnosis, status of the patient, stage of cancer, and site of the tumor.
