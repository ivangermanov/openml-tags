Airlines Departure Delay Dataset

Tags: Dataset used in the tabular data benchmark, transformed in the same way. This dataset belongs to the "regression on both numerical and categorical features" benchmark.

Original Link: OpenML

Original Description: This dataset is designed to predict departure delay in seconds. The dataset is a processed version of the original data, obtained from the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, Airline Service Quality Performance. The data was sourced from the website of the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, Airline Service Quality Performance, and is a subset of the columns from the original dataset, which covers the years from nineteen eighty-seven to two thousand thirteen.

The dataset is a subset of a larger CSV file, which can be found online. This subset includes the first one million rows of the original dataset. The dataset is in ARFF format.

The dataset features include the month of the year, the day of the month, the scheduled departure time, the scheduled arrival time, the distance of the flight, and the departure delay.
