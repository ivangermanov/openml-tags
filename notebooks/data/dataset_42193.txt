Name: COMPAS Two Years

Tags: Study

Original data from the COMPAS Analysis dataset. The data was subsequently preprocessed and reduced to relevant features for classification. The target variable is Recidivism Within Two Years, which indicates whether an individual will reoffend within a two-year period.

Features: Gender, Age, Juvenile Felony Count, Juvenile Misdemeanor Count, Juvenile Other Count, Prior Offenses Count, Age Category Twenty-Five to Forty-Five, Age Category Greater than Forty-Five, Age Category Less than Twenty-Five, African American Race, Caucasian Race, Felony Charge Degree, Misdemeanor Charge Degree

Keywords: COMPAS, Recidivism, Classification, Preprocessing, Features, Target Variable
