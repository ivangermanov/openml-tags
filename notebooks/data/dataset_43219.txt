The Productivity Prediction dataset is categorized under Machine Learning and Mathematics tags.

This dataset features various attributes, including the Date, Quarter, Department, Day, Team, Targeted Productivity, Work in Progress, Overtime, Incentive, Idle Time, Number of Workers Who Are Idle, Number of Style Changes, and Total Number of Workers, as well as Actual Productivity.
