TUPRAS Boiler Data 
Tags: Computer Systems, Education 

This dataset contains sensor data measurements of one boiler, collected over a period of time. The data includes water input and steam output parameters, such as flow rate, temperature, and pressure, recorded every minute. 

The dataset features include date and time, water flow rate, water temperature, water pressure, steam flow rate, steam temperature, steam pressure, and an additional parameter labeled as X.

Please cite: Sensor data measurements of one boiler.
