Stock Market Prediction

This dataset contains information about HDFC Bank equity share information.

The dataset contains a total of seven columns. The Date column is in datetime64 format. The Open, High, Low, Close, and Adjusted Close columns are in float64 format, representing the stock prices. The Volume column is also in float64 format, representing the number of shares traded.

The dataset features include the date, open price, high price, low price, previous close price, last traded price, close price, volume-weighted average price, fifty-two week high, fifty-two week low, volume, value, and number of trades.

Keywords: stock market, HDFC Bank, equity shares, stock prices, trading volume.
