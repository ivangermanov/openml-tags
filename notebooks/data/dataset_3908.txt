League of Legends - Life Science, Medicine Context Dataset

As an enthusiast of the Russian League of Legends Championship Series, I have created a comprehensive dataset dedicated to capturing the essence of this esteemed competition.

This dataset comprises match data documenting all regular season games of the split, including tiebreakers, while excluding playoff matches and covering both spring and summer splits.

The data was meticulously collected from Gamepedia, ensuring accuracy and reliability.

The dataset features include:

* Team One
* Team Two
* Winning Team
* Coordinated Universal Time
* Blue Side
* Red Side
* Most Valuable Player

Keywords: League of Legends, Championship Series, Match Data, Regular Season, Tiebreakers, Playoffs, Spring Split, Summer Split, Gamepedia.
