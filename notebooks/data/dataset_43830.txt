**Indian Card Payment Data Set**
**Tags: Context**

The card payments data is published by the Reserve Bank of India on a monthly basis, providing valuable insights into the methods of payment used in retail transactions and ATM transactions in India. This comprehensive dataset constitutes payments via debit cards, credit cards, ATMs, and other payment methods, enabling users to analyze the trend of card-based payments in India.

**Content**

The dataset contains monthly statistics of the following information:

* The number of ATMs deployed on-site by the bank
* The number of ATMs deployed off-site by the bank
* The number of Point of Sale (POS) terminals deployed online by the bank
* The number of POS terminals deployed offline by the bank
* The total number of credit cards issued and outstanding, adjusted for withdrawn or cancelled cards
* The total number of financial transactions done by credit cards issued by the bank at ATMs
* The total number of financial transactions done by credit cards issued by the bank at POS terminals
* The total value of financial transactions done by credit cards issued by the bank at ATMs
* The total value of financial transactions done by credit cards issued by the bank at POS terminals
* The total number of debit cards issued and outstanding, adjusted for withdrawn or cancelled cards
* The total number of financial transactions done by debit cards issued by the bank at ATMs
* The total number of financial transactions done by debit cards issued by the bank at POS terminals
* The total value of financial transactions done by debit cards issued by the bank at ATMs
* The total value of financial transactions done by debit cards issued by the bank at POS terminals

**Acknowledgements**

The data is scraped from the Reserve Bank of India's monthly statistics.
