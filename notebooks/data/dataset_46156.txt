Name: Standard Dataset
Tags: Dataset from R package 'KMsurv'

This dataset, known as the Standard Dataset, is a collection of features related to various aspects of health and wellness. The features included in this dataset are: Race, Marital Status, Age, Years of Schooling, Infection Status, Number of Partners, Outcomes within Twelve Months, Outcomes within Thirty Days, Risk Status within Twelve Months, Risk Status within Thirty Days, Abdominal Pain, Discharge, Dysuria, Condom Use, Itching, Lesions, Rash, Lymph Node Status, Vaginal Health, Discharge Examination, Abnormal Node Status, and Survival Status, along with the corresponding Time variable.

Keywords: Health, Wellness, Infection, Risk Factors, Survival Rate
