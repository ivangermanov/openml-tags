The Bread Basket 

Tags: Machine Learning, Meteorology 

This dataset belongs to The Bread Basket, a charming bakery situated in the heart of Edinburgh. It comprises an extensive collection of twenty thousand plus entries, stemming from over nine thousand transactions, spread across four distinct columns. 

The dataset encompasses transactions of customers who placed orders for various delectable items from this esteemed bakery through their online platform. The time frame of the data spans from January twenty-sixth to December twenty-seventh. 

Features include transaction details, item descriptions, date and time information, and categorization based on day periods and weekdays versus weekends.

Keywords: Bakery, Transactions, Online Orders, Edinburgh, Date and Time, Weekdays
