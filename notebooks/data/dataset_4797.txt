**socmob**

This dataset provides an in-depth description of social mobility, which refers to the correlation between the occupations held by sons and those held by their fathers. Each instance represents the number of sons who have a specific occupation, given that their fathers hold a particular job, while also taking into account factors such as race and family structure.

The dataset was originally compiled for the comprehensive survey titled "Occupational Change in a Generation II".

**Attribute Description**

The attributes included in this dataset are:

Fathers Occupation
Sons Occupation
Family Structure, which can be classified as either intact or non-intact
Race, categorized as either black or white
Counts for Sons First Occupation
Counts for Sons Current Occupation, which serves as the target feature.

Keywords: Social Mobility, Occupational Change, Family Structure, Race, Fathers Occupation, Sons Occupation.
