Titanic Survival Prediction Dataset 

Tags: Machine Learning, Physical Sciences 

This dataset focuses on predicting the survival chances of passengers based on various characteristics. The key features used for this prediction include the Passenger Class, Gender, Age, Ticket Fare, Port of Embarkation, Number of Relatives Onboard, and the passenger's Title.

Keywords: Titanic, Survival, Machine Learning, Physical Sciences, Passenger Class, Gender, Age, Ticket Fare
