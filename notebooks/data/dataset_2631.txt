**FOREX eur hkd Minute High**

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex High, Forex Minute, Human Activities, Transportation

This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex EUR/HKD exchange rate, sourced from Dukascopy Historical Data Feed. Each instance represents a one-minute candlestick, and the entire dataset spans from January to December, excluding weekends when the Forex market is closed.

The timezone of the feature Timestamp is Europe/Amsterdam. The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean of the High Bid and the High Ask prices in the following minute, relative to the current minute. Specifically, the class attribute is true when the mean High price increases in the subsequent minute, and false when it decreases or remains unchanged.

Please note that this dataset is intended for scientific purposes only, and realistic trading strategies should only be applied to predictions based on Close attributes, which are also available.

The dataset includes the following attributes:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point, expressed in Europe/Amsterdam timezone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the start of the time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during the time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during the time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of the time interval.
* Bid Volume: The number of times the bid price changed within the time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the start of the time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during the time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during the time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of the time interval.
* Ask Volume: The number of times the ask price changed within the time interval.
* Class: Indicates whether the average price will increase in the next time interval.
