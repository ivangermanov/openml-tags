Shill Bidding Dataset

Tags: Shill Bidding

We scraped a large number of eBay auctions of a popular product. After preprocessing the auction data, we built the Shill Bidding dataset. The goal is to share the labelled Shill Bidding dataset with researchers.

Features: Record Identification, Auction Identification, Bidder Identification, Bidder Tendency, Bidding Ratio, Successive Outbidding, Last Bidding, Auction Bids, Starting Price Average, Early Bidding, Winning Ratio, Auction Duration, Class

Scraped Data: Dataset Information Has No Missing Values.

Keywords: Shill Bidding, eBay Auctions, Dataset, Research, Auction Data, Preprocessing.
