Online Shoppers Purchasing Intention

Tags: Machine Learning, Mathematics

This dataset includes various features related to online shoppers' purchasing intentions. The administrative feature provides insight into the duration spent on administrative tasks. Meanwhile, the informational feature highlights the time spent on gathering information. Additionally, product-related aspects are explored through their corresponding durations.

Other notable features include bounce rates, exit rates, page values, and special day indicators. Furthermore, the dataset takes into account monthly variations, operating systems used, browsers employed, regional differences, traffic types, visitor types, and whether the visit occurred during the weekend.

Revenue is also an essential aspect considered in this dataset.

Keywords: Online Shopping, Purchasing Intention, Machine Learning, Mathematics, E-commerce Analytics
