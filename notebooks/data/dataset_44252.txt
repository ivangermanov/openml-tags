**Meta-Album OmniPrint-MD-5-bis Dataset (Micro)**

The OmniPrint-MD-5-bis dataset consists of synthesized images from seven hundred six categories. The images are not processed further after synthesis with OmniPrint. The OmniPrint synthesis parameters are stated as follows: font size, image size, the strength of random perspective transformation, left, right, top, and bottom margins, the strength of pre-rasterization elastic transformation, random translation, image blending method, rotation, horizontal shear, foreground, and background.
