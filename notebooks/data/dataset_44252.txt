**Meta-Album OmniPrint-MD-5-bis Dataset (Micro)**

The OmniPrint-MD-5-bis dataset consists of synthesized character images with a specific set of nuisance parameters. It contains twenty-eight thousand two hundred forty images, each measuring one hundred twenty-eight by one hundred twenty-eight pixels, and featuring three color channels (RGB). These images are categorized into seven hundred six classes.
