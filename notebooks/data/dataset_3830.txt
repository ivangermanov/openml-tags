Weather in Szeged 2006-2016 
Machine Learning, Statistics 

This dataset is part of a larger project focused on analyzing and comparing real historical weather patterns with traditional weather folklore. 

The dataset provides a comprehensive hourly and daily summary of weather conditions in the Szeged, Hungary area, covering a ten-year period. 

The hourly data includes information on time, summary, precipitation type, temperature, apparent temperature, humidity, wind speed, wind direction, visibility, cloud cover, and atmospheric pressure.

Features include formatted date, summary, precipitation type, temperature, apparent temperature, humidity, wind speed, wind direction, visibility, cloud cover, and atmospheric pressure.

Keywords: Weather Forecasting, Historical Climate Data, Folklore Comparison, Atmospheric Conditions, Hungary Climate
