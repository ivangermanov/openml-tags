Indian Liver Patient Dataset
Tags: Data Set Information

This dataset contains liver patient records and non-liver patient records. The dataset was collected from test samples in the North East of Andhra Pradesh, India. The class label "is patient" is used to divide the records into two groups: liver patient or not.

The dataset contains male patient records and female patient records. Any patient whose age exceeded eighty-nine is listed as being of age "ninety".

Attribute Information:

* Age of the patient
* Gender of the patient
* Total Bilirubin
* Direct Bilirubin
* Alkaline Phosphotase
* Alamine Aminotransferase
* Aspartate Aminotransferase
* Total Proteins
* Albumin
* Albumin and Globulin Ratio
* Selector field used to split the data into two sets (labeled by the experts)

The dataset has been elicited from the UCI Machine Learning Repository.
