Web Series Ultimate Collection
Tags: Health, Machine Learning Content

This comprehensive dataset comprises every web series currently streaming globally, providing an extensive collection of information about each series. The inspiration behind creating this dataset lies in its ability to provide answers to various questions, including which streaming platforms feature a particular web series, its average IMDb rating and other ratings, its genre classification, a brief summary, the number of available seasons, and the production year.

The dataset offers a wide range of features, including:

Series Title, Year Released, Content Rating, IMDb Rating, Genre Classification, Description, Number of Seasons, and Streaming Platform.

Keywords: Web Series, Streaming Platforms, IMDb Ratings, Genre Classification, Synopsis, Production Year, Comprehensive Dataset.
