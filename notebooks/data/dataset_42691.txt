Weather 

Tags: Life Science, Transportation 

Sample Features: The Outlook, Temperature, Humidity, Windy Conditions, Playful Weather 

The Weather dataset provides information about the atmospheric conditions that affect our daily lives and transportation systems. It includes details about the general outlook, which helps us plan our daily activities. The temperature feature indicates whether it will be hot or cold outside, while the humidity level shows how much moisture is present in the air. Additionally, the windy conditions feature informs us about the speed and direction of the wind, which is essential for transportation systems like aviation and shipping. Finally, the playful weather feature suggests whether the weather is suitable for outdoor recreational activities or not.
