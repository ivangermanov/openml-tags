Anneal Dataset 

Tags: Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Study 

This dataset has undergone binarization of the original data set. The multi-class target feature has been converted into a two-class nominal target feature, where the majority class is labeled as Positive and all others as Negative. 

Features of this dataset include Family, Product Type, Steel, Carbon, Hardness, Temper Rolling, Condition, Formability, Strength, Non-Ageing, Surface Finish, Surface Quality, Enamel Ability, and various chemical properties.

Keywords: Anneal, Chemistry, Life Science, Binary Classification, Material Properties
