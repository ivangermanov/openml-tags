Relevant Information: This dataset is a subset of the National Indonesia Contraceptive Prevalence Survey, which focuses on married women who were either not pregnant or did not know if they were at the time of interview. The primary goal is to predict the current contraceptive method choice of a woman based on her demographic and socioeconomic characteristics.
