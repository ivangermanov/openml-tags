**Meta-Album RSICB Dataset (Mini)**

The RSICB dataset covers forty-five scene categories, assembling a total of thirty-six thousand images of resolution one hundred twenty-eight by one hundred twenty-eight pixels. The data authors select various locations around the world and follow China's land-use classification standard. This collection has a two-level label hierarchy with six super-categories: Agricultural Land, Construction Land and Facilities, Transportation and Facilities, Water and Water Conservancy Facilities, Woodland, and Other Lands.
