**Meta Album RSICB Dataset (Mini)**

The RSICB dataset covers forty-five scene categories, assembling a total of images of resolution one hundred twenty-eight by one hundred twenty-eight pixels. The data authors select various locations around the world and follow China's land use classification standard. This collection has a two-level label hierarchy with six super categories: agricultural land, construction land and facilities, transportation and facilities, water and water conservancy facilities, woodland, and other lands.
