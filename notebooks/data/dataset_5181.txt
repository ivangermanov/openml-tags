Churn Uplift Orange Dataset

This anonymized dataset originates from a 2020 marketing campaign conducted by Orange Belgium, focusing on churn and uplift modeling. The dataset comprises various features, including principal components, which are essential for understanding customer behavior and preferences.

The comprehensive set of features includes Principal Component One, Principal Component Two, and so forth, providing a detailed understanding of the variables influencing customer decisions. These components are crucial in identifying patterns and trends within the data, ultimately enabling the development of effective marketing strategies.

By analyzing this dataset, researchers and marketers can gain valuable insights into the factors contributing to customer churn and uplift, allowing them to refine their approaches and improve overall customer satisfaction.

Keywords: Churn Modeling, Uplift Modeling, Marketing Campaign, Customer Behavior, Principal Components
