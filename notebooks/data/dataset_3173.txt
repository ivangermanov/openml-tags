House Sales 

Tags: Life Science, Transportation 

This dataset contains house sale prices for King County, including Seattle. The data spans from May two thousand fourteen to May two thousand fifteen. It comprises nineteen house features in addition to the price and identification columns, accompanied by twenty-one thousand six hundred thirteen observations. This dataset is well-suited for evaluating simple regression models due to its comprehensive nature.

Features include Identification, Price, Number of Bedrooms, Number of Bathrooms, Square Footage of Living Area, Square Footage of Lot, Number of Floors, Waterfront Property Indicator, View Indicator, Condition of House, Grade of House, Square Footage of Above Ground Living Area, Square Footage of Basement, Year Built, Year Renovated, Zip Code, Latitude, Longitude, Average Square Footage of Living Area Fifteen, Average Square Footage of Lot Fifteen, Year Sold, Month Sold, Day Sold.

Keywords: house sales, king county, seattle, regression models, real estate
