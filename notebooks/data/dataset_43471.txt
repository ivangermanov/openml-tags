Context: Candy Crush Saga is a highly popular mobile game developed by King, a part of Activision Blizzard, that is enjoyed by millions of people worldwide. The game is structured as a series of levels where players need to match similar candies together to hopefully clear the level and keep progressing on the level map.

Content: Candy Crush has a vast collection of levels, with new ones being added on a regular basis. Maintaining the perfect level of difficulty is crucial, as it can make or break the gaming experience. If the levels are too easy, the game becomes monotonous, whereas if they are too challenging, players become frustrated and eventually quit playing.

Features: player identification, date, level, number of attempts, number of successes

Keywords: mobile game, level difficulty, gaming experience, player engagement, level progression
