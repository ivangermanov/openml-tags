Students Scores 

Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning 

This is a dataset about student performance in various academic areas. The dataset explores how different factors influence student outcomes. 

Features included in this dataset are student's gender, their racial and ethnic background, the level of education achieved by their parents, whether they receive free or reduced-price lunches, participation in test preparation courses, as well as scores in mathematics, reading, and writing.

Keywords: Student Performance, Academic Outcomes, Demographic Factors, Educational Background, Test Preparation
