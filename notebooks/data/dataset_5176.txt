The Web Questions dataset consists of question and answer pairs. The questions are designed to be answerable by Freebase, a large knowledge graph. Most of the questions are centered around a single named entity. They are popular questions asked on the web, at least at some point in time.

This dataset has a number of useful characteristics. For instance, each entry includes a URL, a question, and the corresponding answers. 

Keywords: Web Questions, Chemistry, Life Science, Freebase, Knowledge Graph
