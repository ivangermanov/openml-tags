Sick Dataset from the OpenCC18 with all textual binary variables label encoded.

This dataset features a range of attributes, including the patient's Age, Sex, and medical history, such as whether they are currently taking Thyroxine or have undergone Thyroid Surgery. It also includes information on whether the patient is Pregnant, has a Goitre, or has been treated with Lithium. Additionally, the dataset covers various thyroid-related measurements, including TSH, T3, TT4, T4U, FTI, and TBG levels, as well as the Referral Source of the patient.

The Class label is also included in the dataset.

Keywords: Thyroid, Medical History, Patient Attributes, Label Encoded, Binary Variables.
