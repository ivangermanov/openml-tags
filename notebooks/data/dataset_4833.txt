This synthetic dataset, referred to as Intersectional Bias Assessment, contains comprehensive demographic and clinical information utilized to train a classifier capable of predicting a diagnosis of either schizophrenia or depression. Furthermore, this dataset serves as an essential tool for assessing the model's performance regarding intersectional bias.

The dataset encompasses an array of features, including the patient's sex, race, housing situation, delay in seeking medical attention, symptoms such as anhedonia, depressed mood, sleep disturbances, fatigue, appetite changes, rumination, concentration difficulties, psychomotor agitation, delusions, suspicious behavior, withdrawal, passive behavior, tension, and unusual thought patterns.

Keywords: Intersectional Bias Assessment, mental health care, demographics, clinical data, diagnosis, schizophrenia, depression, model evaluation.
