NYC Hourly Temperature

Tags: Context Hourly weather data for New York City

This dataset contains hourly weather data for New York City, extracted from online web sources and cleaned for the purpose of calculating NYC Taxi ETA. The dataset is comprehensive, with no missing values, and features a range of weather-related variables.

The dataset includes features such as the date, time, temperature in Fahrenheit, dew point in Fahrenheit, humidity, wind speed in miles per hour, and weather conditions. The weather conditions feature includes information on various types of weather, such as snow, rain, and others.

This dataset was inspired by a similar dataset created for Boston, which was used in past research for weather data analysis. The cleaned version of the dataset is user-owned and has been made available for further research and analysis.

Keywords: New York City, hourly weather data, taxi ETA, weather conditions, temperature, humidity, wind speed
