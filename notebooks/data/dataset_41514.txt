Diabetes Dataset

The Diabetes dataset consists of ten baseline variables, including age, sex, body mass index, average blood pressure, and six blood serum measurements, obtained for each of the diabetes patients. The response of interest is a quantitative measure of disease progression one year after baseline.

Data Set Characteristics:

The dataset contains a total number of instances. Each instance has ten numeric predictive values, and the target is a quantitative measure of disease progression one year after baseline.

Attribute Information:

The dataset includes the following attributes: age, sex, body mass index, average blood pressure, and six blood serum measurements, namely S One, S Two, S Three, S Four, S Five, and S Six. Each of these ten feature variables has been mean centered and scaled by the standard deviation.

Source:

The dataset is available at the following URL: http://www4.stat.ncsu.edu/~boos/var.select/diabetes.html. For more information, refer to the Least Angle Regression article.

Keywords: Diabetes, disease progression, blood serum measurements, body mass index, blood pressure.
