Historical Data on the Trading of Cryptocurrencies

Tags: Context

This dataset comprises historical data on cryptocurrency trading, spanning from the beginning of January 2016 to the twenty-first of February 2021. If you find this dataset useful, please upvote it, as this will indicate its popularity and encourage me to update it regularly. Thank you for your support!

This dataset is particularly well-suited for data analysis, specifically for predicting the prices of digital cryptocurrencies. It encompasses a wide range of features, including the trade date, volume, price in US dollars, price in Bitcoin, market capitalization, and changes in capitalization and prices over a one-day period. Additionally, it provides information on the cryptocurrency's name, type, ticker, maximum supply, website URL, GitHub URL, minability, platform name, and industry name.

Keywords: Cryptocurrency Trading, Historical Data, Data Analysis, Price Prediction, Digital Currencies
