**Data Description**
The datasets provided include the players' data for the Career Mode from FIFA. It only includes male football players with known wages. The goal is to predict the wage of the player based on his attributes, which are the key characteristics that define a player's skills and abilities.

**Attribute Description**
The features describe self-explanatory properties and skills of the player, including his physical characteristics, technical abilities, and playing style. These features are essential in determining a player's value and wage in the football industry.

Features: 
Wage in Euros, Age, Height in Centimeters, Weight in Kilograms, Nationality, Overall Rating, Potential Rating, Attacking Skills, Defending Skills, Goalkeeping Skills, and Movement Abilities.

Keywords: FIFA, Football, Players, Wages, Attributes, Career Mode
