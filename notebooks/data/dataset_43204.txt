Student Time Management

Tags: Student Time Management

Features: Age, Gender, Nationality, Program, Course, English Language Proficiency, Academic Performance, Attendance Record

This dataset focuses on the time management skills of students, taking into account their age, gender, and nationality. It also considers the program and course they are enrolled in, as well as their English language proficiency. The academic performance and attendance record of the students are also important features of this dataset.

Keywords: Student, Time Management, Academic Performance, Attendance, English Language Proficiency
