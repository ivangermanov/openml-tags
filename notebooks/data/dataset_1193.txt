This dataset, known as BNG (Low Birth Weight), is tagged as artificial and BNG. It contains various features related to maternal and fetal health factors that may influence birth weight. These features include low birth weight indicator, maternal age, maternal weight, racial background, smoking status, history of preterm labor, maternal height, presence of urinary infections, whether it's a first-time visit, and a class designation.
