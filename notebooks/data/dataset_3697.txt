The Snapchat Google Play Store Reviews dataset contains reviews from the Google Play Store regarding Snapchat. By conducting sentiment analysis on these reviews, we can gauge users' adoption of the Android version of Snapchat, which the company claims has undergone significant improvements recently. Moreover, this analysis will also help us determine whether the app is indeed redefining the use of cameras and changing the way individuals interact with their loved ones.

Features: 
Date, 
Rating, 
Helpful, 
Review Text

Keywords: Sentiment Analysis, Android Version, Camera Redefinition, Social Interaction, User Adoption.
