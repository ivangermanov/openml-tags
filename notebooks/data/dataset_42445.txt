The Titanic Three Dataset 

Tags: Life Science, Transportation 

This dataset explores the survival possibilities on the ill-fated Titanic ship. The dataset is focused on predicting the chances of survival based on various factors. 

Features of this dataset include whether the passenger survived or not, their social class, gender, age, the fare they paid for their ticket, where they boarded the ship, how many family members were traveling with them, and their title such as Mr., Mrs., or Miss.

Keywords: Titanic, Survival, Prediction, Life Science, Transportation
