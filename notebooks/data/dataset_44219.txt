Reading Hydro Upstream

Tags: Upstream data from the Twin Archimedes Screw Hydro-Electric Generator on the River Thames at Caversham Weir, Reading, United Kingdom.

This dataset features data collected from the upstream side of the hydro-electric generator, including a timestamp and information about the downstream flow. The data is collected from the Twin Archimedes Screw Hydro-Electric Generator, a unique and innovative system located on the River Thames at Caversham Weir in Reading, United Kingdom.

Keywords: Hydro-Electric, Archimedes Screw, River Thames, Caversham Weir, Reading, United Kingdom.
