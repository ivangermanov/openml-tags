Insurance Tags: This dataset is intended for classification tasks and comprises both continuous and categorical variables. The features of this dataset include the Upper Age and Lower Age ranges, Recommended Policy Premium, City Code, type of Accommodation, Recommended Insurance Type, whether the individual is a Spouse, Health Indicator, duration of Holding Policy, and type of Holding Policy, all of which are used to predict the class or category of the insurance policy.

Keywords: Insurance, Classification, Policy, Premium, Age, City, Accommodation, Health, Duration
