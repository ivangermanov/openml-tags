This database, Heart Disease Prediction, contains fourteen attributes. The goal field refers to the presence of heart disease in the patient, which is indicated by an integer value of either zero or one. The features of this database include the patient's age, sex, chest pain type, resting blood pressure, cholesterol level, fasting blood sugar level, resting electrocardiogram results, maximum heart rate achieved, exercise induced angina, old peak, slope of the peak exercise, number of major vessels colored by fluoroscopy, and thalassemia diagnosis.

Keywords: heart disease, patient attributes, diagnosis prediction
