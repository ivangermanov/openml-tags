The Heart Disease Prediction Database is categorized under Machine Learning and Statistics. This comprehensive database encompasses fourteen distinct attributes. The primary objective is to predict the presence of heart disease in patients, which is denoted by an integer value of zero or one in the goal field.

The database comprises various features, including age, gender, chest pain type, resting blood pressure, cholesterol levels, fasting blood sugar, resting electrocardiogram results, maximum heart rate achieved during exercise, exercise-induced angina, ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest, the slope of the peak exercise ST segment, number of major vessels colored by fluoroscopy, and thallium stress test outcome.

Keywords: Heart Disease Prediction, Machine Learning, Statistics, Medical Diagnosis
