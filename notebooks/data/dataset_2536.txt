Students Dataset

Tags: Economics, Transportation

This dataset pertains to students and comprises various features related to their instructional settings, classroom environments, and academic performance. The dataset explores factors influencing students' learning outcomes, including instructor characteristics, classroom settings, repetition of lessons, attendance rates, and perceived difficulty levels.

The features encompass a range of aspects, including instructional methods, class settings, repeated lessons, attendance records, perceived difficulty levels, and responses to multiple questions assessing their understanding of economics and transportation concepts.

Keywords: students, economics, transportation, instruction, classroom, attendance, difficulty
