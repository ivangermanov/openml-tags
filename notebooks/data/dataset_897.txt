This dataset provides information about colleges and universities, including their salaries and compensation for various professorial ranks. It has been modified from its original form to create a binary classification based on the mean target value. The dataset includes details such as the institution's name, location, and type, as well as comprehensive salary and compensation information for different academic positions. It also provides counts of faculty members at each rank. This binarized version allows for simplified analysis and comparison of institutions based on their relative standing in terms of faculty compensation.
