The dataset is called BNG Autos, which is an Artificial dataset with Nominal attributes and a total of one million entries. 

This dataset has several key features, including Normalized Losses, Make, Fuel Type, Aspiration, Number of Doors, Body Style, Drive Wheels, Engine Location, Wheel Base, Length, Width, Height, Curb Weight, Engine Type, Number of Cylinders, Engine Size, Fuel System, Bore, Stroke, Compression Ratio, Horsepower, Peak RPM, City MPG, Highway MPG, Price, and Symboling.

Keywords: Artificial Dataset, Nominal Attributes, Automobiles, Vehicle Features, Engine Specifications
