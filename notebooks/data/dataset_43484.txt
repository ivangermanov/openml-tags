Context: Bus Breakdown and Delays

You can find the road where the traffic was heavy for the New York City Taxi Trip Duration playground.

The Bus Breakdown and Delay system collects information from school bus vendors operating out in the field in real-time. Bus staff who encounter delays during the route are instructed to radio the dispatcher at the bus vendor's central office. The bus vendor staff are then instructed to log into the Bus Breakdown and Delay system to record the event and notify the Office of Pupil Transportation (OPT). OPT customer service agents use this system to inform parents who call with questions regarding bus service.

The Bus Breakdown and Delay system is publicly accessible and contains real-time updates. All information in the system is entered by school bus vendor staff. You can find data for previous years.

Features: School Year, Bus Breakdown ID, Run Type, Bus Number, Route Number, Reason, Schools Serviced, Occurred On, Created On, Borough, Bus Company Name

The Bus Breakdown and Delay system provides a comprehensive platform for tracking and managing bus breakdowns and delays, ensuring that parents and schools are informed in a timely manner.
