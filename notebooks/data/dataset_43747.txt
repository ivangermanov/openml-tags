Minneapolis Air Quality Survey

Tags: Context Minneapolis air quality survey results

The content contained in the file consists of Minneapolis air quality survey results obtained during a specific period. The data set was obtained from the official website of the City of Minneapolis, which provides open data to the public.

The inspiration behind this data set is to visualize the quantities of air pollutants over the city of Minneapolis, which may provide evidence for the source of certain air pollutants. This could lead to a better understanding of the factors contributing to air pollution in the city.

The features of this data set include the location coordinates, date, sample identification, parameter, results, units, and other relevant information.
