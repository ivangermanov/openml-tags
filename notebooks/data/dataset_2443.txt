Churn 
Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning, OpenML-CC18 

This dataset relates various characteristics of telephony account features and usage to determine whether or not the customer churned. It was originally used in Discovering Knowledge in Data: An Introduction to Data Mining. 

The dataset includes information about the customer's state, account length, and area code, as well as details about their phone plan, such as whether they have an international plan or voice mail plan. It also includes data about the customer's calling habits, including the total number of minutes and calls made during different times of day, as well as the total charge for these calls. Additionally, the dataset includes information about the customer's interactions with customer service.

Source: PMLB, BigML
