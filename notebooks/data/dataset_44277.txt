**Meta Album RSD Micro Tags**

The Meta-Album RSD Dataset (Micro) is a collection of remote sensing images from Google Earth and Tianditu. This dataset contains forty-six scene categories, with a total of images. Each scene category has a varying number of images. The original resolution of the images is either two hundred fifty-six by two hundred fifty-six pixels or five hundred twelve by five hundred twelve pixels. We have created a preprocessed version of RSD for Meta-Album by resizing the original dataset to one hundred twenty-eight by one hundred twenty-eight pixels.
