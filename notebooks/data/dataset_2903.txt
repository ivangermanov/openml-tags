S1 Dataset - Computer Systems, Social Media 
Synthetic two-dimensional data with vectors and Gaussian clusters featuring varying degrees of cluster overlap.

This dataset is based on research published in the paper titled "Iterative Shrinking Method for Clustering Problems", which explores innovative approaches to clustering algorithms.
