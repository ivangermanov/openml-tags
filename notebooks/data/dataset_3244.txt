Stock Dataset: Label Ranking in Manufacturing and Transportation 

This dataset focuses on label ranking in two significant industries: manufacturing and transportation. The primary objective is to facilitate the process of assigning relevant labels to various products or items within these sectors. 

The dataset comprises several essential features, including those related to product characteristics, production processes, and logistical considerations. These features play a vital role in determining the most suitable labels for each item. 

Keywords: label ranking, manufacturing, transportation, product characteristics, production processes, logistical considerations.
