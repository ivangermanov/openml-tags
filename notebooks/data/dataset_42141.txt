SVHN Medium Dataset

This dataset is a fifty percent stratified subsample of the original Street View House Numbers dataset. It contains a large number of features, including pixel values, that are used to represent images of house numbers.

The dataset includes a wide range of pixel values, from pixel one to pixel one hundred, and many more, totaling over three thousand features. These features are used to capture the details and patterns in the images of house numbers.

Keywords: Street View House Numbers, Image Dataset, Pixel Values
