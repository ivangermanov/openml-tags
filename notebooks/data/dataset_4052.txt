Meta QSAR Project - September 2015

This dataset shows activity values of several compounds on drug target Fibroblast Growth Factor Receptor 2.

Fibroblast Growth Factor Receptor 2 is a single protein that belongs to the enzyme/kinase/protein kinase/tyrosine protein kinase group/FGFR family. It is involved in various biological processes, including angiogenesis, ureteric bud development, in utero embryonic development, epithelial to mesenchymal transition, and positive regulation of mesenchymal cell proliferation.

The dataset contains information about the protein, including its synonyms, organism, species group, protein target classification, and description.
