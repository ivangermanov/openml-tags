Simulated Video Transcoding Dataset 

Tags: Chemistry, Life Sciences 

See GitHub repository for a detailed description of this dataset.

This dataset features video transcoding attributes, including video duration, codec type, video resolution represented by width and height, bitrate, frame rate, as well as specific details about individual frames such as intra-coded, predicted, and bi-predictive frames, and their respective sizes. Additionally, the dataset provides information on output video characteristics, including codec, bitrate, frame rate, and resolution. 

Keywords: video transcoding, chemistry, life sciences, video attributes, codec, resolution, bitrate, frame rate.
