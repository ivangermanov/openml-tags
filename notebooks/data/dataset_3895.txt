Product Relevance Dataset

Tags: Chemistry, Life Science

The primary objective of this dataset is to accurately predict the relevance of each product to individual customers, thereby ensuring that future recommendations and sales are tailored to meet their specific needs.

This comprehensive database comprises various attributes commonly collected when a company sells a product to a customer. These attributes provide valuable insights into the customer's profile and preferences, enabling businesses to offer personalized services.

Key Attributes:

* User Information: age, gender, nationality, knowledge level, loyalty status, loan details, income, savings, properties owned, risk aversion, marital status, number of dependents, pension details
* Product Details: type, risk factor, term, yield
* Transaction Details: unique identifier, year, month
* Score

Keywords: customer relevance, product recommendation, personalized sales, customer profiling, data analysis.
