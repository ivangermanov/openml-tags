**Eleven Tumors Dataset**
Tags: Microarray

The Eleven Tumors dataset is a comprehensive collection of gene expression signatures used for molecular classification of human carcinomas. This dataset is a valuable resource for researchers and scientists working in the field of cancer research and genomics.

The dataset features a wide range of attributes, including gene expression levels, that can be used for classification and analysis of different types of tumors.
