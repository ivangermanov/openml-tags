**FOREX aud/jpy-minute-Close**

Tags: Computer Systems, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Social Media

This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex aud/jpy pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents a single candlestick of one-minute duration. The dataset spans from January first to December thirteenth and excludes weekends, as Forex markets do not operate on Saturdays and Sundays. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute is determined by the direction of the mean close price between the current and subsequent minutes. Specifically, it is true if the mean close price increases in the following minute and false if it decreases or remains unchanged.

The attributes included in this dataset are:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the beginning of the time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during the time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during the time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of the time interval.
* Bid Volume: The number of times the bid price changed within the time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the beginning of the time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during the time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during the time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of the time interval.
* Ask Volume: The number of times the ask price changed within the time interval.
* Class: Indicates whether the average price will increase during the next time interval.
