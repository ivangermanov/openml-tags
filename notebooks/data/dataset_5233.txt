The Balanced Dataset of DOA BWIn 

Tags: The Balanced Dataset of DOA BWIn 

This dataset represents a fifty-fifty split between the classes of the dependent variable. It was created using the Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique algorithm in the Themis package for R. 

Features include country identification, gender, respondent's date of birth, sum of stakes, total bets, daily sum of bets, interval days, betting per day, net results, betting per day in the last month, standard deviation of stakes in the last month, total active betting days in the last month, and stakes slope in the last month. The dependent variable is also included.

Keywords: Balanced Dataset, DOA BWIn, Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique, Themis Package, Dependent Variable, Betting Data.
