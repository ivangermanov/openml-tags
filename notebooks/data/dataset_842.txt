The IPUMS LA 98-Small dataset is a binarized version of the original data set. The multi-class target feature has been converted to a two-class nominal target feature by re-labeling the majority class as Positive and all others as Negative.

This dataset falls under the categories of Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Study, and more specifically, Study One, Study Fifteen, Study Twenty, and Study Forty-One.

The dataset originally consisted of various features, including Year, General Quality, Type of Group Quarters, Farm Status, Ownership Status of Home, Value of Home, Rent Paid, Total Family Income, Number of Families, Number of Couples, Number of Mothers, Number of Fathers, Mother's Location, Stepmother's Presence, Mother's Rule, Population Location, Stepparent's Presence, Population Rule, Spouse's Location, Spouse's Rule, Family Size, Number of Children, Number of Children Under Five, Family Unit, Elder Child, Youngest Child, Number of Siblings, Relationship to Household Head, Age, Sex, Race, Marital Status, Birth Status, School Enrollment, Educational Credentials, School Type, Employment Status, Labor Force Participation, Occupation Score, Socioeconomic Index, Class of Worker, Weeks Worked, Hours Worked, Years Worked, Total Income, Wage Income, Business Income, Farm Income, Social Security Income, Welfare Income, Other Income, Poverty Status, Migration Status, Move-In Year, Veteran Status, and Binary Class.
