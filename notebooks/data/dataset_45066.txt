This dataset is for classification tasks and has both continuous and categorical variables. The features include Case State, Age, Seating Position, Restraint System Use, Air Bag Availability and Deployment, Non Motorist Location, Alcohol Test Result, Drug Test Results, Related Factor at the Person Level, Race, Sex, Person Type, Ejection, Ejection Path, Extrication, Police Reported Alcohol Involvement, Method of Alcohol Determination, Alcohol Test Type, Police Reported Drug Involvement, Method of Drug Determination, Drug Test Type, Hispanic Origin, Taken to Hospital, and a class label.

Keywords: Classification, Traffic Accident, Demographics, Substance Abuse, Vehicle Safety
