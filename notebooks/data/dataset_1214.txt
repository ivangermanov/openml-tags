Japanese Vowels Dataset

Tags: Artificial, Japanese Vowels, Chemistry, Life Science, Study

Features: Speaker, Utterance, Frame, Coefficients for Acoustic Analysis

This dataset focuses on the study of artificial Japanese vowels, delving into the realm of chemistry and life science. The coefficients provided are utilized for an in-depth acoustic analysis, allowing researchers to examine the intricacies of spoken language.
