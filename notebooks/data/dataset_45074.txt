An international e-commerce company specializing in electronic products wants to uncover key insights from its extensive customer database by leveraging advanced machine learning techniques to thoroughly study its customers.

The company aims to analyze various aspects of customer behavior, including the frequency of customer care calls, overall customer ratings, prior purchase history, discounts offered, product weights, warehouse block allocations, modes of shipment, product importance, and customer demographics such as gender, all of which will help classify customers into distinct categories.
