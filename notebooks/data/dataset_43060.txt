Real Ad Exchange Dataset

Tags: Online Advertisement Clicking Rates

This dataset focuses on online advertisement clicking rates, where the key metrics are cost per click and cost per thousand impressions. The features of this dataset include a timestamp, which provides a record of when the advertisement was displayed, as well as the cost per click and cost per thousand impressions, which are essential metrics for evaluating the effectiveness of an online advertising campaign.

Keywords: Online Advertising, Clicking Rates, Cost Per Click, Cost Per Thousand Impressions
