The Churn dataset relates characteristics of telephony account features and usage to whether or not the customer churned. This dataset was originally used in data mining research.

Source: https://www.secs.ac.in/wp-content/CSE_PORTAL/Data-Mining-Dataset.pdf

Features of this dataset include the following: State, Account Length, Phone Number, Number of Voicemail Messages, Total Day Minutes, Total Day Calls, Total Day Charge, Total Evening Minutes, Total Evening Calls, Total Evening Charge, Total Night Minutes, Total Night Calls, Total Night Charge, Total International Minutes, Total International Calls, Total International Charge, Area Code, International Plan, Voice Mail Plan, and Number of Customer Service Calls.

This dataset also includes a classification feature indicating whether or not the customer churned.

Keywords: Churn, Telephony, Account Features, Customer Behavior, Data Mining, Classification.
