The Heloc dataset is a classification benchmark focused on numerical features, belonging to the Tabular Data Benchmark. This dataset originates from the Kaggle platform, where it was shared under the title "Home Equity Line of Credit". 

The dataset includes various features that are relevant for predicting risk performance in home equity lines of credit. These features encompass external risk estimates, trade history, delinquency information, credit inquiries, and burden metrics. Specifically, the features included are average months since oldest trade open, months since most recent trade open, average months in file, number of satisfactory trades, and other metrics related to trade history and credit behavior.
