The Black Friday dataset is a collection of customer purchase information, categorized under the regression on categorical and numerical features benchmark. This dataset examines various factors influencing customer purchases, including demographic characteristics, lifestyle, and product preferences.

The dataset contains several features, including gender, age, occupation, city category, length of stay in current city years, marital status, and categories of purchased products. These features provide insights into the complexities of consumer behavior and purchasing decisions.

Keywords: Black Friday, customer purchases, demographic characteristics, lifestyle, product preferences, consumer behavior, purchasing decisions.
