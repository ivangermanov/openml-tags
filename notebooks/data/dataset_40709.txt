This dataset is focused on the theme of happiness, exploring the various factors that contribute to an individual's overall sense of well-being and satisfaction with life. The features included in this dataset provide a comprehensive look at the relationship between happiness and demographic characteristics, such as years of education and number of siblings. Finally, the social class feature provides insight into how happiness may vary across different socioeconomic backgrounds.
