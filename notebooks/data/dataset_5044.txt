Pigs Bayesian Network. Sample Four.

This Bayesian network repository reference is classified under the discrete-very large category.

The Pigs network consists of four hundred forty-one nodes and five hundred ninety-two arcs. It has five thousand six hundred eighteen parameters. The average Markov blanket size is approximately three point sixty-six, and the average degree is two point sixty-eight. The maximum in-degree is two.

Some of its notable features include variables related to pig health, growth rates, and living conditions.

The Pigs network is a comprehensive representation of various factors influencing pig farming and can be used for machine learning and predictive analytics applications.

Keywords: Bayesian network, machine learning, pig farming, predictive analytics, Markov blanket, discrete variables.
