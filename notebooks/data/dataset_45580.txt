Churn Uplift Orange Dataset

Tags: Anonymized dataset of churn and uplift modeling from a marketing campaign by Orange Belgium

Features: Principal Component One, Principal Component Two, Principal Component Three, and many more...

This dataset is an anonymized collection of data from a marketing campaign conducted by Orange Belgium in the year twenty twenty. The primary focus of this dataset is on churn and uplift modeling, which are crucial aspects of customer retention and acquisition strategies in the telecommunications industry. The dataset comprises a vast array of features, including Principal Component One, Principal Component Two, Principal Component Three, and many more, all of which are essential in understanding customer behavior and preferences.

Keywords: churn modeling, uplift modeling, marketing campaign, customer retention, telecommunications industry.
