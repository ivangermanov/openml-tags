Wine Quality Red

Tags: Wine Quality Red PMLB

Features: Fixed Acidity, Volatile Acidity, Citric Acid, Residual Sugar, Chlorides, Free Sulfur Dioxide, Total Sulfur Dioxide, Density, pH, Sulphates, Alcohol, Class

This dataset is focused on the quality of red wine, with a range of features that are relevant to its characteristics. The features include various measures of acidity, such as fixed acidity and volatile acidity, as well as citric acid and residual sugar. Additionally, the dataset includes measures of chlorides, free sulfur dioxide, and total sulfur dioxide, which are important for understanding the wine's chemical composition. The density and pH of the wine are also included, along with sulphates and alcohol content. Finally, the class feature provides a categorization of the wine's quality.

Keywords: Wine Quality, Red Wine, Acidity, Chemical Composition, Wine Characteristics
