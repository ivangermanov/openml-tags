Parking Statistics in North America

About

This dataset identifies areas within a city where drivers are experiencing difficulty searching for parking. Cities can utilize this data to identify problem areas, adjust signage, and implement other solutions to alleviate parking congestion. Only cities with a significant population are included in this dataset.

Data

Some variables to highlight include:

Average Time to Park: The average time taken to search for parking in minutes
Average Time to Park Ratio: The ratio between the average time taken to search for parking and those not searching for parking in the current geographic area
Total Searching: The number of drivers searching for parking
Percentage Searching: The percentage of drivers that were searching for parking
Average Unique Geographic Areas: The average number of unique geographic areas at a specific level that were driven in among vehicles that searched for parking
Average Total Geographic Areas: The average number of all geographic areas at a specific level that were driven in among vehicles that searched for parking
Circling Distribution: A JSON object representing the neighboring geographic areas at a specific level whereby vehicles searching for parking tend to spend their time
Hourly Distribution: A JSON object representing the average prevalence of searching for parking by hour of day
Searching by Hour: A JSON object representing the average percentage of vehicles searching for parking within the hour
Percentage of Vehicle Types: The percentage of vehicles with parking issues that were cars, multi-purpose vehicles, light duty trucks, medium duty trucks, heavy duty trucks, or unknown classification

Content

This dataset is aggregated over the previous six months and is updated monthly. This data is publicly available from a reputable source.

Inspiration

Some potential questions to explore with this dataset include: Which cities are the hardest to find parking in? Is there a relationship between a region's population and the time it takes to find parking? Is there a correlation between a region's economic indicators, such as GDP, and parking times? What about average household income?

Features:

Location, City, County, State, Country, Average Time to Park, Average Time to Park Ratio, Total Searching, Percentage Searching, Average Unique Geographic Areas, Average Total Geographic Areas, Circling Distribution, Hourly Distribution, Searching by Hour, Percentage of Vehicle Types, Update Date, Version

Keywords: parking statistics, city planning, traffic congestion, urban development, transportation infrastructure
