**FOREX EUR GBP Minute Close**

Tags: Demographics, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Transportation

This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex EUR GBP from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one minute. The entire dataset covers a period from January first to December thirteenth and excludes weekends, as Forex trading does not take place on weekends. The timestamp feature is based on the Europe Amsterdam timezone.

The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean close price in relation to the subsequent minute. Specifically, the class attribute is true if the mean close price increases in the subsequent minute, and false if the mean close price decreases or remains the same.

The attributes comprise:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in the Europe Amsterdam timezone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the beginning of this time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the conclusion of this time interval.
* Bid Volume: The frequency of changes in the bid price within this time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the beginning of this time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the conclusion of this time interval.
* Ask Volume: The frequency of changes in the ask price within this time interval.
* Class: Indicates whether the average price will increase or decrease in the subsequent interval.
