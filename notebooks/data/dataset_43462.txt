The Riga Real Estate Dataset

Tags: Content

This dataset contains a comprehensive collection of real estate objects in Riga. The dataset provides detailed information about each property, including its offer type, location, and physical characteristics.

The offer type column specifies whether the property is for rent, for sale, or available for other purposes. The district and street columns provide the exact location of the property. The number of rooms and living area of each property are also included. Additionally, the dataset provides information about the floor and total number of floors in the building.

The house design and type of building are also specified, with options ranging from brick-panel to wood and masonry. The condition of the premises is also described, with options including all amenities, partial amenities, and without amenities.

The price of each property is listed in euros. The latitude and longitude of each property are also provided, allowing for precise geographic analysis.

Features: offer type, district, street, number of rooms, living area, floor, total number of floors, house design, type of building, condition, price, latitude, longitude

Keywords: Riga, real estate, property, location, physical characteristics, offer type, house design, building type, condition, price, latitude, longitude
