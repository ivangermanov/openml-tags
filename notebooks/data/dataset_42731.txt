House Sales

Tags: Date Converted to Year/Month/Day Numerics

This dataset contains house sale prices for King County, which includes Seattle. It includes homes sold between May two thousand fourteen and May two thousand fifteen. It contains nineteen house features plus the price and the identification columns, along with a large number of observations. It is a great dataset for evaluating simple regression models.

The dataset features include identification, price, number of bedrooms, number of bathrooms, square feet of living space, square feet of lot space, number of floors, waterfront properties, view, condition, grade, square feet of living space above ground, square feet of basement space, year built, year renovated, zip code, latitude, longitude, square feet of living space in two thousand fifteen, and square feet of lot space in two thousand fifteen, as well as the year, month, and day of the sale.
