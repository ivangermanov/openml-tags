The Enron dataset, tagged as 2019 multi-output paper, is a multi-label dataset. The UC Berkeley Enron four dataset represents a subset of the original Enron five dataset and consists of cases of emails with twenty-one labels and one thousand one predictor variables. 

The features of this dataset include ability, access, account, action, actions, add, added, addition, additional, address, administration, and many more. 

Keywords: Enron, multi-label, emails, predictor variables.
