House Sales

Tags: This dataset contains house sale prices for King County, which includes Seattle. It includes homes sold between May two thousand fourteen and May two thousand fifteen. It contains nineteen house features plus the price and the identification columns, along with a large number of observations. This is a great dataset for evaluating simple regression models.

The dataset features include the identification, date, price, number of bedrooms, number of bathrooms, square feet of living space, square feet of lot space, number of floors, waterfront, view, condition, grade, square feet of living space above ground, square feet of basement, year built, year renovated, zip code, latitude, longitude, and square feet of living space and lot space in two thousand fifteen.

Keywords: House Sales, King County, Seattle, Regression Models, Real Estate
