This analytic dataset, referred to as bwin_amlb, contains actual betting behavior and virtual live action betting data for each participant, aggregated over a one-month period from the date the account was opened. Additionally, it includes live action betting data for each participant aggregated over the entire study period. 

The dataset features a range of variables, including the country of origin, gender, and age of the participants. It also includes metrics related to betting behavior, such as the total sum of stakes, the total sum of bets, and the total number of days with betting activity. Furthermore, it includes variables that capture the intensity and frequency of betting, such as the average number of bets per day and the average stake per bet. 

The dataset also includes variables that provide insights into the participants' betting patterns over time, such as the total number of active betting days over a one-month period and the slope of the stakes over time.

Keywords: betting behavior, live action betting, participant data, betting patterns, study period.
