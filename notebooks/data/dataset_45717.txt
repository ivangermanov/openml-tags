This Analytic Dataset Contains Actual Betting Behavior and Virtual Live Action Betting for Each Participant Aggregated Over One Month Period from the Date the Account Was Opened. It also contains live action betting for each participant aggregated over the entire study period from the start date to the end date or until the account was closed.

The dataset includes information about participants' country of origin, gender, and age. Additionally, it provides data on various aspects of their betting behavior, including total stakes placed, total bets made, average daily stakes, and average interval between bets.

Keywords: betting behavior, virtual live action, study period, participant data, demographic information, betting activity
