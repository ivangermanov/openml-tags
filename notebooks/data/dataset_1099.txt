Authorization: Contact Authors

Description: This dataset contains four measurements of male Egyptian skulls from five different time periods. Thirty skulls are measured from each time period, resulting in a total of one hundred and fifty cases. The measurements include the maximal breadth of skull, basibregmatic height of skull, basialveolar length of skull, and nasal height of skull. Additionally, the approximate year of skull formation is provided, with negative values indicating years Before Common Era (BCE) and positive values indicating years Anno Domini (AD).
