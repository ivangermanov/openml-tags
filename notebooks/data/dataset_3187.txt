Kits Subset is a machine learning dataset focused on transportation, consisting of one hundred images. This subset of the larger Kits dataset includes a wide range of features that are essential for training accurate models.

The numerous features included in this dataset enable researchers to analyze various aspects of transportation systems, facilitating the development of advanced solutions for this field. By leveraging these features, researchers can create more sophisticated models that better capture the complexities of transportation systems.

The Kits Subset dataset provides a valuable resource for researchers working on transportation-related projects, offering a wealth of information to support their work.
