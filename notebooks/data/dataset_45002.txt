**Data Description**
The datasets provided include the players' data for the Career Mode from FIFA. It only includes male football players. The goal is to predict the wage of the player based on his attributes, which are the key characteristics that define a player's skills and abilities.

**Attribute Description**
The features describe self-explanatory properties and skills of the player, including his physical characteristics, technical abilities, and playing style. These features are essential in determining a player's value and wage in the football industry.

Features:
Nationality Name, Overall, Potential, Attacking Skills, Skill Abilities, Movement Abilities, Defending Abilities, Goalkeeping Abilities

Keywords: FIFA, Football, Player Attributes, Wage Prediction, Career Mode
