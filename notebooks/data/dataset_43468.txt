MMA Fight Predictions by Professional Fighters

Tags: Context

This continually updated dataset comprises professional fighters making predictions on upcoming fights. The data is gathered primarily from a YouTube channel, where fighters are asked to predict the outcomes of forthcoming fights. Currently, the dataset encompasses a vast collection of predictions made by professional fighters, dating back to the end of 2015 up to the present day.

Features: 
Winner, Loser, Date, Winner Odds, Predictor, Correctness of Prediction
