MMA Fight Predictions by Professional Fighters

Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning

This is a continually updated dataset of expert opinions from professional fighters who share their predictions on upcoming mixed martial arts fights. 

The data has been collected primarily through video interviews, where fighters are asked to forecast the outcomes of future bouts. 

Currently, this extensive dataset comprises predictions for numerous fights, dating back to the end of two thousand fifteen up to the present day.

Features include information about the predicted winner, loser, date of the fight, odds of the winning fighter, the predicting fighter, and whether the prediction was accurate.

Keywords: MMA, Fight Predictions, Professional Fighters, Machine Learning, Computer Systems
