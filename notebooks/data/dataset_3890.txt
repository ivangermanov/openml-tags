This dataset, named Articles From BuzzFeed 2020, is tagged with Machine Learning and Transportation. It was created by our in-house teams at PromptCloud and DataStock. 

The dataset contains approximately five thousand samples. You can download the full dataset at the provided link.

The dataset includes articles from BuzzFeed, covering the period from January 1, 2020, to April 30, 2020. The file extension is CSV. The available fields include unique ID, crawl timestamp, title headline, short description sub-headline, content body, date and time of posting, and image URLs.

We acknowledge the significant contribution of our web scraping and data mining experts at PromptCloud and DataStock. 

Our inspiration for creating this dataset came from BuzzFeed itself. We recognized the importance of informative articles on BuzzFeed and decided to create a dataset for them.

Features include unique ID, crawl timestamp, title headline, short description sub-headline, content body, date and time of posting, and image URLs.
