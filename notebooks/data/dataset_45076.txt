The Churn dataset relates characteristics of telephony account features and usage to whether or not the customer churned. This dataset was originally used in a data mining introduction.

Source: https://www.secs.ac.in/wp-content/CSE_PORTAL/DataMining.pdf

The dataset features include the customer's state, account length, phone number, number of voicemail messages, total daytime minutes, total daytime calls, total daytime charge, total evening minutes, total evening calls, total evening charge, total nighttime minutes, total nighttime calls, total nighttime charge, total international minutes, total international calls, total international charge, area code, international plan, voice mail plan, and number of customer service calls. The class feature indicates whether the customer churned or not.

Keywords: churn, telephony, account features, customer service, data mining
