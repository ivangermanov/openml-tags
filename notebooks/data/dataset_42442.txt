Titanic Two Dataset

This dataset is categorized under Life Science and Transportation tags.

The Titanic Two Survival Prediction dataset aims to predict the likelihood of survival based on various characteristics of passengers.

The features considered for this prediction include the passenger's Survival status, their social class denoted by Passenger Class, their gender represented by Sex, their age in years, the fare they paid for their ticket, the port where they embarked, the number of relatives accompanying them, and their title.

Keywords: Titanic, Survival, Passenger Class, Gender, Age, Ticket Fare, Port of Embarkation, Relatives, Title.
