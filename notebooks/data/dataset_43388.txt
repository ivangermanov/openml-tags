Cinema Tickets

Tags: Context, Cinema Industry

The cinema industry is not exempt from leveraging the benefits of predictive modeling, just like other industries. By doing so, cinemas can reduce costs and improve their return on investment. Forecasting sales enables the optimization of screenings in different locations, effective market targeting, and pricing. Moreover, historical data on sales and movie details, such as cost, cast, and crew, as well as project details like schedules, can assist producers in selecting high-performing cast and crew members and planning for better project returns on investment. Additionally, this data helps assign screening locations to hotspots and areas.

About

This dataset contains approximately eight months of sales history from different cinemas, featuring detailed data on screenings, including encoded and anonymized location information.

Starter Kernels

* Exploratory Data Analysis
* Temporal Feature Engineering
* XGBoost

Inspiration

Time series analysis, cinema clustering, forecasting sales for each cinema, and recommending movie genres and cinema locations are all potential applications of this dataset.

Features

* Film Code
* Cinema Code
* Total Sales
* Tickets Sold
* Tickets Out
* Show Time
* Occupancy Percentage

* Ticket Usage
