IBM Employee Performance Tags: IBM Employee Attrition Data

The dataset used in the code pattern is supplied by Kaggle and contains Human Resources analytics data of employees who stay and leave. The types of data include metrics such as Education Level, Job Satisfaction, and Commute Distance. The dataset was obtained from the GitHub repository of IBM Employee Attrition AIF360. The dataset is available under the Open Dataset License and the Database Content License.

The following columns have been removed: Attrition, Percent Salary Hike. The remaining features include Age, Business Travel, Daily Rate, Department, Distance from Home, Education, Education Field, Employee Count, Employee Number, Environment Satisfaction, Gender, Hourly Rate, Job Involvement, Job Level, Job Role, Job Satisfaction, Marital Status, Monthly Income, Monthly Rate, Number of Companies Worked, Over Eighteen, Overtime, Performance Rating, Relationship Satisfaction, Standard Hours, Stock Option Level, Total Working Years, Training Times Last Year, Work Life Balance, Years at Company, Years in Current Role, Years Since Last Promotion, and Years with Current Manager.

Keywords: Employee Attrition, Human Resources Analytics, Job Satisfaction, Commute Distance, Education Level, Performance Rating.
