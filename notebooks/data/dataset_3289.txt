Beijing Multi Site Air Quality 
Tags: Images, Life Science 

This dataset includes hourly air pollutant data from twelve nationally controlled air quality monitoring sites. 

The features included in this dataset are: Year, Month, Day, Hour, Particulate Matter two point five, Particulate Matter ten, Sulfur Dioxide, Nitrogen Dioxide, Carbon Monoxide, Ozone, Temperature, Pressure, Dew Point, Rain, Wind Direction, and Wind Speed at each Monitoring Station.

Keywords: air quality, monitoring sites, pollutants, particulate matter, sulfur dioxide, nitrogen dioxide, carbon monoxide, ozone, temperature, pressure, dew point, rain, wind direction, wind speed.
