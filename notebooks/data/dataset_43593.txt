Context 
In the dataset French Motor Claims Datasets freMTPL2freq, risk features and claim numbers were collected for motor third-party liability policies observed over a year.

Content 
The freMTPL2freq dataset contains eleven columns, plus the policy ID, which is used to link with the claims dataset. The dataset includes the following information: policy ID, number of claims during the exposure period, exposure period, area code, vehicle power, vehicle age in years, driver age in years, bonus-malus rating, vehicle brand, vehicle gas type, density of inhabitants in the city where the driver lives, and policy region in France.
