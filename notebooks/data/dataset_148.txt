This dataset, known as BNG (zoo, nominal), is an artificial collection of animal-related information. It encompasses various features that describe different characteristics of animals. These features include the presence of hair or feathers, the ability to lay eggs or produce milk, and whether the animal is airborne or aquatic. The dataset also considers if the animal is a predator, has teeth, possesses a backbone, and breathes air. Additional attributes include whether the animal is venomous, has fins, legs, or a tail. The dataset also notes if the animal is domesticated and compares its size to that of a cat. The type of animal is also recorded as a feature.
