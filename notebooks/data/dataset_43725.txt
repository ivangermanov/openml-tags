KNYC Metars Dataset

Tags: Context

The METAR format is a standardized way of reporting weather information. A METAR weather report is primarily used by pilots as part of their pre-flight weather briefing, and by meteorologists, who utilize aggregated METAR information to assist in weather forecasting.

This dataset contains the aggregated METAR information for the KNYC location.

Acknowledgements

Thanks are extended to the providers of the data.

Inspiration

This dataset is intended to be used as supplementary information for those seeking to extract conclusions from their own dataset, where hourly weather information could be useful for their predictions and analysis. If you have any doubts or suggestions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Features

The dataset includes information on time, temperature, wind chill, humidity, pressure, dew point, visibility, wind direction, wind speed, and precipitation, as well as any notable weather events and conditions.
