The Midwest Survey dataset contains individual responses from surveys about regional identification conducted by SurveyMonkey. The features of this dataset include responses to the question "In your own words, what would you call the part of the country where you live?" as well as questions about personal identification as a Midwesterner. The dataset also includes questions about whether certain states are considered part of the Midwest, including Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Michigan, Minnesota, Missouri, Nebraska, North Dakota, Ohio, South Dakota, and Wisconsin. Additionally, respondents were asked about the inclusion of Arkansas, Colorado, Kentucky, Oklahoma, Pennsylvania, West Virginia, Montana, and Wyoming in the Midwest region. Demographic information such as ZIP Code, gender, age, household income, education, and location by Census Region are also included in the dataset.

Keywords: Midwest, regional identification, SurveyMonkey, demographics, Census Region.
