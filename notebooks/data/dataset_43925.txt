The dataset is related to Job Scheduling in High Performance Computing and is used in Applied Predictive Modeling. 

The dataset contains information about protocols, compounds, input fields, iterations, and the number of pending jobs. It also includes information about the hour and day of job submission, as well as the class of the job.
