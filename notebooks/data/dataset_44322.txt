**Meta Album Textures Dataset (Extended)**

The original Textures dataset is a combination of four texture datasets: KTH-TIPS and KTH-TIPS Two, Kylberg Textures Dataset, and UIUC Textures Dataset. The data in all four datasets is collected in laboratory conditions, i.e., images were captured in a controlled environment with configurable brightness, luminosity, scale, and angle.
