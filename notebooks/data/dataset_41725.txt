**FOREX EurCad Day High**

Tags: Economics, Finance, Forex, Forex Day, Forex High

This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex EurCad currency pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one day. The entire dataset spans from January first to December thirteenth and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is not traded during weekends. The timestamp feature is based on the Europe Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean high price of the following day relative to the mean high price of the current day. Specifically, the class attribute is true when the mean high price increases the following day and false when the mean high price decreases or remains the same.

**Data Description**

One instance represents one candlestick of one day. The entire dataset spans from January first to December thirteenth and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is not traded during weekends. The timestamp feature is based on the Europe Amsterdam time zone.

**Attributes**

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in the Europe Amsterdam time zone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the start of this time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price during this time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price during this time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* Bid Volume: The number of times the bid price changed within this time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the start of this time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price during this time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price during this time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* Ask Volume: The number of times the ask price changed within this time interval.
* Class: Whether the average price will increase during the next interval.
