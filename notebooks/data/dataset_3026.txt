The Titanic Survival Prediction Dataset is categorized under Machine Learning and Social Media tags. This dataset aims to predict the survival chances of passengers based on various features. The key features used for prediction include the Passenger Class, Sex, Age, Fare Paid, Port of Embarkation, Number of Relatives Onboard, and the Title of the Passenger.

Keywords: Titanic, Machine Learning, Social Media, Survival Prediction, Passenger Class, Sex, Age, Fare Paid, Port of Embarkation, Relatives Onboard, Title
