Simulated Adult Dataset 

Tags: See github.com/slds-lmu/paper-ci-for-gender-equity for a description.

This dataset consists of various features that describe an individual, including their Age, Work Class, Education Level, Marital Status, Occupation, Relationship, Race, Sex, Capital Gain, Capital Loss, Hours per Week, and Native Country, all of which are used to determine their Class.

Features: Age, Work Class, Education, Marital Status, Occupation, Relationship, Race, Sex, Capital Gain, Capital Loss, Hours per Week, Native Country, Class

Keywords: Simulated Adult, Gender Equity, Education Level, Marital Status, Occupation, Demographics
