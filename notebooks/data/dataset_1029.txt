Relevant Information:
The LEV data set contains examples of anonymous lecturer evaluations, taken at the end of MBA courses. Before receiving their final grades, students were asked to score their lecturers according to four attributes such as oral skills and contribution to their professional and general knowledge. The single output was a total evaluation of the lecturer's performance.

Features: Oral skills, Professional knowledge contribution, General knowledge contribution, Clarity of course objectives, Overall evaluation

Keywords: lecturer evaluation, MBA courses, student feedback, teaching performance, academic assessment
