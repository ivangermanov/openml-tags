The Lecturers Evaluation data set contains examples of anonymous lecturer evaluations, taken at the end of Master of Business Administration courses. Before receiving their final grades, students were asked to score their lecturers according to four distinct attributes, namely their oral skills, ability to inspire, ability to clarify complex concepts, and contribution to their professional and general knowledge.
