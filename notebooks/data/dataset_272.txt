Carbolenes Dataset: Earth Science, Health

This dataset is one of forty-one drug design datasets. The datasets were formed using Adriana Code software, which generated one thousand one hundred forty-three features. The molecules and outputs were taken from the original studies.

The Carbolenes dataset was originally studied in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen ninety-six. Other datasets include MTP Two, Chang, Cristalli, Depreux, Doherty, Garrat Two, Garrat, Heyl, Krystek, Lewis, Penning, Rosowsky, Siddiqi, Stevenson, Strupcz, Svensson, Thompson, Tsutumi, Uejling, Yokoyama One, and Yokoyama Two. These datasets were taken exactly from their respective original studies.

The last attribute in each file is the target. The original studies include publications in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computer Science, J Med Chem, European Journal of Medicinal Chemistry, QSAR, Hansch Analysis and Related Approaches, Chemometrics and Intelligent Laboratory Systems, and others.

Keywords: drug design, medicinal chemistry, chemical information, computer science, earth science, health
