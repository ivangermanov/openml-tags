Pathogen Survey Dataset

Tags: Life Science, Machine Learning, Unspecified Target Feature

This dataset contains various features related to pathogens, including identification information, environmental factors, and geological characteristics.

Features:

* Identification Number
* X Coordinate
* Y Coordinate
* Fungal Pathogen Presence
* Dipluran Presence
* Temperature
* Precipitation Sum
* Radiation Sum
* Elevation
* Slope
* Hail Events
* New Hail Events
* Age of Sample
* pH Level
* Lithology
* Soil Type
* Year of Collection

Keywords: Pathogens, Environmental Factors, Geological Characteristics, Life Science, Machine Learning
