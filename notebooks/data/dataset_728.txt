Tags: Binarized, Chemistry, Life Science, Myth Busting One, Study One, Study One Two Three, Study Fifteen, Study Twenty, Study Forty-One 

This dataset is a binarized version of the original data set. It involves converting the numeric target feature into a two-class nominal target feature by calculating the mean and categorizing all instances with a lower target value as positive, denoted by 'P', and all others as negative, represented by 'N'. The dataset comprises the following features: Sex and Graduate Management Admission Test.
