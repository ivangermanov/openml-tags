Minneapolis Air Quality Survey

Tags: Economics, Machine Learning

Context 
The Minneapolis Air Quality Survey results contain valuable information collected between November and August. This comprehensive dataset was sourced from the official website of the City of Minneapolis.

Inspiration 
Visualizing the quantities of air pollutants across the city of Minneapolis could provide crucial evidence for identifying the sources of specific air pollutants. By analyzing these results, researchers may uncover patterns and correlations that were previously unknown.

Features 

* Location coordinates (X and Y)
* Date of sample collection
* Unique identifier for each sample
* Type of parameter measured
* Results of the measurement
* Unit of measurement
* Description of the sampled location
* Address of the sampled location
* City, state, and zip code of the sampled location

Keywords: air quality, Minneapolis, survey, economics, machine learning, pollution, visualization
