Study 144: Dis-PMLB

This dataset features a range of attributes related to thyroid function, including the patient's age and sex. It also includes information on whether the patient is taking thyroxine medication, has a query on thyroxine, or is taking anti-thyroid medication. Additionally, the dataset notes whether the patient is sick, pregnant, or has undergone thyroid surgery or I131 treatment.

The dataset also explores the patient's medical history, including whether they have hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism, and whether they have been treated with lithium. It also notes the presence of goiter, tumor, hypopituitary, or psychological issues.

The dataset includes laboratory test results, such as whether TSH, T3, TT4, T4U, FTI, and TBG were measured, and the corresponding values. It also notes the referral source of the patient and the class of the diagnosis.

Keywords: thyroid function, medication, medical history, laboratory tests, diagnosis.
