Real Ad Exchange Dataset

Tags: Online Advertisement Clicking Rates

This dataset focuses on online advertisement clicking rates, where the key metrics are cost per click and cost per thousand impressions. 

Features: The dataset includes a timestamp, which is a critical component in tracking online advertisement performance, as well as cost per click, a crucial metric in evaluating the effectiveness of online advertising campaigns.

Keywords: Online Advertisement, Clicking Rates, Cost Per Click, Cost Per Thousand Impressions, Timestamp.
