Real Ad Exchange Dataset 

Tags: Machine Learning, Statistics 

This online advertisement dataset focuses on clicking rates, where the key performance metrics are Cost Per Click and Cost Per Thousand Impressions. The dataset contains features such as Timestamp, which represents the time at which an ad was displayed, and Cost Per Click, which represents the cost of each individual click on an ad.

Keywords: online advertising, clicking rates, machine learning, statistics, cost per click, cost per thousand impressions.
