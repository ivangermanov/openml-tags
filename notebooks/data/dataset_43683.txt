The WebMD Drug Reviews Dataset 

Tags: Culture, Geography 

This dataset provides user reviews on specific drugs along with related conditions, side effects, age, sex, and ratings reflecting overall patient satisfaction. The data was acquired by scraping the WebMD website.

The dataset is designed to answer several key questions, including identifying the condition of a patient based on their drug reviews, predicting drug ratings based on patient reviews, and visualizing drug ratings, kinds of drugs, types of conditions a patient can have, and sentiments expressed in reviews.

The dataset includes a range of features, including the patient's age, condition, date, drug information, ease of use, effectiveness, review text, satisfaction level, sex, side effects experienced, and useful count.

Keywords: WebMD, drug reviews, patient satisfaction, condition identification, sentiment analysis.
