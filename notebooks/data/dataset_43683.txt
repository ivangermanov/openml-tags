The WebMD Drug Reviews Dataset

Context:
This dataset provides user reviews on specific drugs along with related conditions, side effects, age, sex, and ratings reflecting overall patient satisfaction.

Content:
The data was acquired by scraping the WebMD website. The dataset contains a large collection of unique reviews, updated until March 2020.

Inspiration:
This dataset is intended to answer the following questions: Can we identify the condition of the patient based on drug reviews? How can we predict drug ratings based on patient reviews? How can we visualize drug ratings, types of drugs, conditions a patient can have, and sentiments based on reviews?

Features:
The dataset includes information on the patient's age, condition, date, drug, ease of use, effectiveness, reviews, satisfaction, sex, and side effects.

Keywords: drug reviews, patient satisfaction, condition identification, drug rating prediction, sentiment analysis
