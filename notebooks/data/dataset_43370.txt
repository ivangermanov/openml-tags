Dutch News Articles

This dataset contains all the articles published by the Netherlands Broadcasting Organization as of the first of January. The data is obtained by scraping the Netherlands Broadcasting Organization website. The Netherlands Broadcasting Organization is one of the biggest online news organizations in the Netherlands.

Features:

Date and Time of Publication: the date and time of publication of the article.
Article Title: the title of the news article.
Article Content: the content of the news article.
Article Category: the category under which the Netherlands Broadcasting Organization filed the article.
Original Article URL: link to the original article.

About the Data

The article title and content features are somewhat clean, meaning extra white spaces and new lines are removed. Furthermore, these features are normalized. The Netherlands Broadcasting Organization also publishes live blogs. The posts in this live blog are not part of this dataset.
