Currency Exchange Rates

Tags: This dataset contains the daily currency exchange rates as reported to the International Monetary Fund by the issuing central bank. Included are fifty-one currencies over a period from January 1, 1995, to November 4, 2018. The format is known as currency units per US Dollar. Explained by example, each rate in the Euro column says how much US Dollar you had to pay at a certain date to buy one Euro. Hence, the rates in the column US Dollar are always one.

Features: Date, Algerian Dinar, Australian Dollar, Bahraini Dinar, Bolivar Fuerte, Botswana Pula, Brazilian Real, Brunei Dollar, Canadian Dollar, Chilean Peso, Chinese Yuan, Colombian Peso, Czech Koruna, Danish Krone, Euro, Hungarian Forint, Icelandic Krona, Indian Rupee, Indonesian Rupiah, Iranian Rial, Israeli New Shekel, Japanese Yen, Kazakhstani Tenge, Korean Won, Kuwaiti Dinar, Libyan Dinar, Malaysian Ringgit, Mauritian Rupee, Mexican Peso, Nepalese Rupee, New Zealand Dollar, Norwegian Krone, Nuevo Sol, Pakistani Rupee, Peso Uruguayo, Philippine Peso, Polish Zloty, Qatari Riyal, Rial Omani, Russian Ruble, Saudi Arabian Riyal, Singapore Dollar, South African Rand, Sri Lankan Rupee, Swedish Krona, Swiss Franc, Thai Baht, Trinidad and Tobago Dollar, Tunisian Dinar, UAE Dirham, UK Pound Sterling
