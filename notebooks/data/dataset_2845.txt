Indian Pines 
Tags: Meteorology, Transportation 

The imagery was collected on June twelfth, nineteen ninety-two and represents an area of two point nine kilometers by two point nine kilometers in Tippecanoe County, Indiana, United States of America.

Data contains information of samples forming two hundred twenty spectral bands. The classes represent land use types: Alfalfa, Corn, Grass, Hay, Oats, Soybeans, Trees, and Wheat.

Features include various spectral bands.
