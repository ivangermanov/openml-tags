**FOREX aud/nzd-minute-Close**

Tags: Computer Systems, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Social Media

**Source**: Dukascopy Historical Data Feed

Data Description:
This dataset contains the historical price data of the Foreign Exchange Australian Dollar/New Zealand Dollar from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one minute. The entire dataset spans a specific period, excluding weekends, since the Foreign Exchange market is not active on weekends. The time zone for the feature Timestamp is Europe/Amsterdam.

The class attribute signifies the direction of the mean of the Close Bid and the Close Ask prices of the subsequent minute relative to the mean of the Close Bid and Close Ask prices of the current minute. Specifically, the class attribute is true when the mean Close price increases in the following minute, and false when the mean Close price decreases or remains stagnant in the following minute.

Attributes:
* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in Europe/Amsterdam
* Bid Open: The bid price at the commencement of this time interval
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval
* Bid Close: The bid price at the conclusion of this time interval
* Bid Volume: The frequency of changes in the Bid Price within this time interval
* Ask Open: The ask price at the commencement of this time interval
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval
* Ask Close: The ask price at the conclusion of this time interval
* Ask Volume: The frequency of changes in the Ask Price within this time interval
* Class: Whether the average price will rise during the next interval

Keywords: Forex, Historical Data, Candlestick, European Time Zone, Financial Market
