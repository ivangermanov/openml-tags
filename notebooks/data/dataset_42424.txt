XSCDC FRFGRG Dataset Tags: Cross Sector Collaboration Data Catalog Framework for Regional Growth

This dataset focuses on the importance of cross sector collaboration and its impact on regional growth. It provides a comprehensive data catalog framework that enables the analysis of various factors contributing to regional development. The framework is designed to facilitate collaboration among different sectors, including government, private, and non-profit organizations, to drive economic growth and improve the quality of life in regional communities.

Keywords: Cross Sector Collaboration, Regional Growth, Data Catalog Framework
