The Car Sales dataset includes information about various cars. The primary objective of analyzing this dataset is twofold. Firstly, we need to identify which feature has the most significant impact on car sales. Secondly, we will train a classifier to predict car sales and evaluate the accuracy of these predictions.

This dataset comprises several features, including the manufacturer, model, vehicle type, price, engine size, horsepower, wheelbase, width, length, curb weight, fuel capacity, fuel efficiency, and power performance factor.
