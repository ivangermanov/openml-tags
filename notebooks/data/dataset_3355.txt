Real Ad Exchange Dataset 

Tags: Images, Transportation 

This online advertisement dataset focuses on clicking rates, where the primary metrics for evaluation are Cost Per Click and Cost Per Thousand Impressions. The dataset features include Time Stamp, which captures the exact moment when an ad was clicked or displayed, along with its corresponding Cost Per Click and Cost Per Thousand Impressions values.

Keywords: online advertisement, clicking rates, Cost Per Click, Cost Per Thousand Impressions, Time Stamp.
