Xeno-Canto-Birds-from-France Tags: Context

This dataset contains information captured about bird recordings from France. The recordings are a valuable resource for exploring the fascinating world of birds and their behavior.

We are grateful for the contributions of many individuals to the Xeno-Canto website, a comprehensive online repository of bird sounds.

This dataset can serve as a starting point for delving into the remarkable landscape of bird recordings. By analyzing the frequency of bird recordings in different areas, researchers can gain insights into species migration patterns, species evolution, and the impact of various environmental factors, such as air pollution. Birds are often early indicators of pollution, making them an important area of study.

The dataset features a range of attributes, including identification information, recording details, location data, and audio file metadata.

Keywords: bird recordings, species migration, environmental impact, air pollution, Xeno-Canto.
