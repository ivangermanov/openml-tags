Airlines Departure Delay Prediction Dataset Tags: **Dataset**: Airlines Departure Delay Prediction **Source**: Original data can be found at the Bureau of Transportation Statistics website. **Please cite**: This dataset is a processed version of the original data, designed to predict departure delay in seconds. The original data spans from nineteen eighty-seven to two thousand thirteen and can be found in a CSV file. This dataset contains the first one million rows and a subset of the columns from the original CSV file, in ARFF format.

The dataset features include the departure delay, month of the year, day of the month, day of the week, the scheduled departure time, the scheduled arrival time, the unique carrier, the origin airport, the destination airport, and the distance of the flight.
