Personality Traits of Twitter Users: Celebrities

Tags: Context, Finding Similarities between Different Types of Public Figures based on their Twitter Activity

This dataset explores the personality traits of celebrities based on their Twitter activity. The content is categorized by user, with each user represented by their username. The personality traits are measured across five dimensions: Openness to Experience, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism.

The dataset includes features such as the user's laboral activity, which categorizes them as an actor, singer, or other type of celebrity. The word count feature provides an indication of the user's Twitter activity level.

Acknowledgements: This dataset draws inspiration from various sources, including psychological studies on personality traits and social media analysis.

Features: User, Openness to Experience, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism, Word Count, Laboral Activity Category

Keywords: Twitter, Celebrities, Personality Traits, Social Media Analysis, Laboral Activity, Word Count
