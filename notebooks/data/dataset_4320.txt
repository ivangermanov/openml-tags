Credit 
Tags: Subsampling of the dataset Credit 

The Credit dataset has been subsampled with a random seed for reproducibility purposes. The resulting subset consists of two thousand rows and one hundred columns, representing ten distinct classes. Notably, stratification was not employed during this sampling process.

Features include Revolving Utilization of Unsecured Lines, Age, Number of Times Thirty to Fifty Nine Days Past Due Not Worse, Debt Ratio, Monthly Income, Number of Open Credit Lines and Loans, Number of Times Ninety Days Late, Number of Real Estate Loans or Lines, Number of Times Sixty to Eighty Nine Days Past Due Not Worse, and Number of Dependents. Additionally, the feature Serious Delinquency in Two Years is also included.
