Law School Admission Dataset (Binarized) 

This dataset is a survey among students attending law school in the United States. The dataset was obtained from the R-package fairml. The response variable has been changed to a binary version, specifically whether the undergraduate grade point average is greater than three. The race variable has been binarized to labels, namely white and non-white. 

The dataset features include the student's age, decile rankings, family income, law school admission test scores, gender, race, cluster, full-time status, bar passage, and undergraduate grade point average greater than three.

Keywords: Law School Admission, Binarized, Survey, Undergraduate Grade Point Average, Race, Law School Admission Test Scores.
