**Meta Album: Stanford Forty Actions Dataset (Micro)**

The Stanford Forty Actions dataset contains images of humans performing forty different actions. This dataset is designed for understanding human actions in still images. It consists of nine thousand five hundred thirty-two images in total, with one hundred eighty to three hundred images per action class.
