**Meta-Album Stanford Forty Actions Dataset (Micro)**

The Stanford Forty Actions dataset contains images of humans performing forty actions. There are images in total with images per action class. The dataset is designed for understanding human actions in still images. For Meta-Album, the dataset is preprocessed and images are resized into one hundred twenty-eight by one hundred twenty-eight pixels using Open-CV.
