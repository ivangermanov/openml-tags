Kaggle Bike Sharing Demand Challenge 

Tags: Chemistry, Computational Universe 

This dataset is a modified version of the training dataset from the Bike Sharing Demand challenge hosted on Kaggle. The original competition was held to predict the bike sharing demand based on various parameters.

If you intend to use this dataset for publication purposes, kindly cite the original source: Event Labeling Combining Ensemble Detectors and Background Knowledge, published in Progress in Artificial Intelligence.

The dataset consists of several features, including date and time information, seasonal patterns, holidays, working days, weather conditions, temperature, atmospheric temperature, humidity levels, wind speed, and the total bike count, along with the day of the week.
