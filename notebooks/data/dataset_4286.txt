**Meta Album Omni Print MD Six Mini**

The Omni Print MD Six dataset consists of synthesized character images with a specific set of nuisance parameters. This dataset contains twenty-eight thousand one hundred twenty images, each with a resolution of one hundred twenty-eight by one hundred twenty-eight pixels, and three color channels. The images are categorized into seven hundred three classes.
