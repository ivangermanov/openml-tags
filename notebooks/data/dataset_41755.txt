Name: Forex Cad Chf Minute Close

Tags: Computer Systems, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Life Science

Source: Dukascopy Historical Data Feed https://www.dukascopy.com/swiss/english/marketwatch/historical/

Data Description:
This dataset comprises the historical price data of the Forex Cad Chf pair obtained from Dukascopy. Each instance, represented by a row, corresponds to a single candlestick of one minute duration. The entire dataset spans a period from January first to December thirteenth and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is inactive during this time. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute is determined by the direction of the mean close price in relation to the subsequent minute. Specifically, the class attribute is assigned a value of true if the mean close price increases in the following minute, and false if the mean close price decreases or remains unchanged.

Attributes:
Timestamp: Represents the time of the current data point in the Europe Amsterdam time zone.
Bid Open: The bid price at the commencement of the time interval.
Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during the time interval.
Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during the time interval.
Bid Close: The bid price at the conclusion of the time interval.
Bid Volume: The frequency of changes in the bid price within the time interval.
Ask Open: The ask price at the commencement of the time interval.
Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during the time interval.
Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during the time interval.
Ask Close: The ask price at the conclusion of the time interval.
Ask Volume: The frequency of changes in the ask price within the time interval.
Class: Indicates whether the average price will rise or fall during the subsequent interval.
