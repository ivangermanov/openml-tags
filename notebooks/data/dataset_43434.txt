Bitcoin Dataset Tags: Why the Focus on a Specific Timeframe?

The Bitcoin data is limited to a three-year period because, as is widely acknowledged, December of that particular year was the time when Bitcoin prices skyrocketed to unprecedented heights! Hence, this duration is perfect for predicting future prices. The years preceding this period had a low price ratio, which could potentially cause a disturbance in our prediction models, leading to inaccurate forecasts.

Features: Date, High Price, Low Price, Opening Price, Closing Price, Trading Volume
