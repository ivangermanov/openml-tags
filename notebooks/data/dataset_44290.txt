The Meta-Album RESISC Dataset (Mini) is a collection of seven hundred RGB images of size two fifty-six by two fifty-six pixels for each of forty-five scene categories. The creators of the dataset aimed to provide a challenging dataset by increasing both within-class diversity and between-class similarity, as well as integrating many image variations.
