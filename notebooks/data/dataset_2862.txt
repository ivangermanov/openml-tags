Iris Dataset 

This dataset deals with the subjects of Economics and Geography. 
The Iris dataset contains information about flowers, specifically the Iris species. 
It includes details about the length and width of the sepals and petals of these flowers.
However, for the purpose of this particular task, we will be ignoring the sepal width and petal length.
Instead, our focus will be on the sepal length, petal width, and the species of the Iris.

Keywords: Iris, Economics, Geography, Flowers, Species, Sepal Length, Petal Width
