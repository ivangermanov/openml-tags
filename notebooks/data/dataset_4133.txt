The KDD IPUMS LA Ninety Seven Small dataset is categorized under Computer Systems and Machine Learning, and has been utilized in the Tabular Data Benchmark. Specifically, it falls within the regression on numerical features benchmark.

This dataset has undergone transformation in a similar manner to its original form. The original description of this dataset remains unclear, including its authorship and source.

The dataset has undergone significant modifications, including the binarization of the original data set. The multi-class target feature has been converted into a two-class nominal target feature, where the majority class is labeled as positive and all other classes are labeled as negative.

The dataset comprises various features, including Value, Rent, Total Family Income, Mother's Location, Family Size, Number of Children, Elderly Children, Young Children, Number of Siblings, Age, Occupation Score, Socioeconomic Index, Total Income, Wage Income, Business Income, Farm Income, Social Security Income, Welfare Income, Other Income, Poverty Level, and Binary Class.
