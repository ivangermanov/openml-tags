Fri C Five Hundred Five is a binarized regression problem dataset. This dataset belongs to the categories of Chemistry and Life Science, and is also related to Mythbusting One, Study One, Study Fifteen, Study Forty-One, Study Seven, Study Eighty-Eight. 

The dataset has undergone a transformation from its original version. In this transformed version, the numeric target feature has been converted into a two-class nominal target feature. This conversion was achieved by calculating the mean and then categorizing all instances with a lower target value as Positive and all others as Negative.

The dataset includes the following features: Oxygen One, Oxygen Two, Oxygen Three, Oxygen Four, Oxygen Five, Binary Class.

Keywords: Regression Problem, Chemistry, Life Science, Binarized Data, Nominal Target Feature.
