Apple Stock Price Dataset for Twenty Twenty-One to Twenty Twenty-Two 

Tags: Agriculture, Machine Learning 

This dataset contains the Apple stock price for each working day starting from January First, Two Thousand and Twenty-One. It provides comprehensive information on the stock's performance, including the highest price, lowest price, opening price, closing price, trading volume, and adjusted closing price.

The dataset features include the date, high price, low price, open price, close price, volume of shares traded, and adjusted close price.
