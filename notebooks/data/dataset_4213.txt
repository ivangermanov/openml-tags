Newspaper Churn 

Tags: Computer Systems, Medicine 

This dataset comprises records of individuals who are or were subscribers to this newspaper. It contains a wide range of demographic information about these subscribers, including their household income, home ownership status, whether they have children, their ethnicity, the year they began residing in their current location, their age range, and the language they speak. Additionally, the dataset provides geographic information about each subscriber, including their address, state, city, county, and zip code.

The dataset also captures information related to the subscription itself, such as the delivery period chosen by each subscriber and the corresponding weekly charge. Furthermore, it includes details about the number of rewards each subscriber has used and the source channel through which they were initially recruited.

Finally, the dataset indicates whether each customer is still an active subscriber or not. 

Keywords: Demographic Information, Geographic Data, Subscription Details, Customer Retention, Reward Programs
