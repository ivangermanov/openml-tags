**FOREX EUR/USD Day Close**

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Day, Machine Learning, Transportation

**Source:** Dukascopy Historical Data Feed

**Data Description**

This is the historical price data of the Forex Euro versus United States Dollar (EUR/USD) from Dukascopy. Each instance, or row, represents one candlestick for one day. The entire dataset spans a specific date range, excluding weekends, as the Forex market is not active on these days. The time zone associated with the feature Timestamp is Europe/Amsterdam.

The class attribute denotes the direction of the mean of the Close Bid and the Close Ask prices for the subsequent day, relative to the mean Close Bid and Close Ask prices of the current day. In other words, the class attribute is true when the mean Close price is expected to increase on the following day, and false when the mean Close price is anticipated to decrease or remain stagnant on the following day.

**Attributes**

* **Timestamp**: The time of the current data point in Europe/Amsterdam.
* **Bid Open**: The bid price at the commencement of this time interval.
* **Bid High**: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Low**: The lowest bid price observed during this time interval.
* **Bid Close**: The bid price at the conclusion of this time interval.
* **Bid Volume**: The frequency of changes in the Bid Price within this time interval.
* **Ask Open**: The ask price at the initiation of this time interval.
* **Ask High**: The highest ask price noted during this time interval.
* **Ask Low**: The lowest ask price observed during this time interval.
* **Ask Close**: The ask price at the termination of this time interval.
* **Ask Volume**: The frequency of changes in the Ask Price within this time interval.
* **Class**: A binary indicator of whether the average price will rise or fall during the subsequent interval.
