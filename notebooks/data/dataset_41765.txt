**FOREX Eur/Try Day Close**

Tags: Economics, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Day, Statistics

This dataset contains the historical price data of the Forex Eur/Try pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one day. The entire dataset spans from January first to December thirteenth and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is not active on these days. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.

The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean close price relative to the subsequent day. Specifically, it signifies whether the mean close price will increase or decrease (or remain unchanged) over the next trading day.

**Attributes**

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point, represented in the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the beginning of this time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price observed during this time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the conclusion of this time interval.
* Bid Volume: The frequency of changes to the bid price within this time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the commencement of this time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price reached during this time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price noted during this time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the termination of this time interval.
* Ask Volume: The number of changes to the ask price within this time interval.
* Class: Indicates whether the average price will rise during the subsequent interval.
