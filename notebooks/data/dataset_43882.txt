AAPL Stock Price Dataset for Two Thousand and Twenty-One to Two Thousand and Twenty-Two

Tags: Apple Stock Price of Each Work Day Since January First

This dataset contains the highest price, lowest price, open, close, volume, and adjusted close of Apple stock for each work day since the beginning of January. The dataset features include the high, low, open, close, volume, and adjusted close prices.

Keywords: Apple Stock, Daily Prices, Financial Data
