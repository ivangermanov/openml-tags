**FOREX audsgd-minute-Close**

Tags: Demographics, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Manufacturing

**Source**: Dukascopy Historical Data Feed

**Data Description**

This dataset comprises historical price data of the Forex Australian Dollar/Singapore Dollar exchange rate from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one minute. The entire dataset spans a specific time period and excludes weekends, as the Forex market does not operate on Saturdays and Sundays. The timestamp feature adheres to the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute signifies the directional movement of the mean close price between consecutive minutes. Specifically, the class attribute assumes a value of true if the mean close price increases in the subsequent minute, and false otherwise.

**Attributes**

* **Timestamp**: The time of the current data point, expressed in the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.
* **Bid Open**: The bid price at the commencement of this time interval.
* **Bid High**: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Low**: The lowest bid price observed during this time interval.
* **Bid Close**: The bid price at the conclusion of this time interval.
* **Bid Volume**: The frequency of changes in the bid price within this time interval.
* **Ask Open**: The ask price at the beginning of this time interval.
* **Ask High**: The highest ask price attained during this time interval.
* **Ask Low**: The lowest ask price noted during this time interval.
* **Ask Close**: The ask price at the termination of this time interval.
* **Ask Volume**: The number of changes in the ask price within this time interval.
* **Class**: A binary indicator signifying whether the mean close price will rise in the subsequent minute.
