Sick Dataset: Thyroid Disease Records

The dataset is supplied by the Garavan Institute, New South Wales, Australia. 

This dataset contains records of patients with thyroid disease, featuring a range of attributes including the patient's age, thyroid stimulating hormone levels, total thyroxine levels, thyroxine uptake, free thyroxine index, sex, and medication status. 

Additionally, the dataset includes information on whether the patient is pregnant, has undergone thyroid surgery, received I131 treatment, or has a history of hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism. 

Other attributes include the presence of lithium, goiter, tumor, hypopituitary, or psychological issues, as well as the referral source and the class of the patient's condition.

Keywords: Thyroid Disease, Patient Records, Medical Dataset, Health Attributes, Medication Status.
