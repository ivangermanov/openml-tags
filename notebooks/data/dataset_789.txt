The Fri C One Hundred Dataset is classified under the categories of Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Study One, Study Twelve Seven, Study Fifteen, Study Twenty, Study Forty One, Study Seven, and Study Eighty Eight.

This dataset is a binarized version of the original dataset. It converts the numeric target feature into a two-class nominal target feature by calculating the mean and categorizing all instances with a lower target value as Positive and all others as Negative.

Features included in this dataset are One, Two, Three, Four, Five, Six, Seven, Eight, Nine, Ten, and Binary Class.

Keywords: Dataset, Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Nominal Target Feature, Binary Class.
