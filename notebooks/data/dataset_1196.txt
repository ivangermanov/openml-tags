The Pharynx Dataset is an Artificial collection of data related to Chemistry and Life Science. It contains various features including Institution, Sex, Type of Treatment, Grade, Age, Medical Condition, Site, Size of Tumor, Lymph Node Involvement, Time of Entry, and Survival Status, all categorized into a specific class.

This dataset was used in a research paper titled "Rotation Forest: A New Classifier Ensemble Method" published in the IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence journal.

Keywords: Pharynx Dataset, Artificial Data, Chemistry, Life Science, Classifier Ensemble Method
