Name: Flare
Tags: **Flare**
The Flare dataset is from the KEEL repository: https://sci2s.ugr.es/keel/category.php?cat=classification
Features: The dataset includes features such as the Largest Spot Size, Spot Distribution, Level of Activity, Evolution, Previous Twenty Four Hour Activity, Historical Complexity, Becoming Historically Complex, Area, and classifications of solar flares into C-class, M-class, and X-class categories.

Keywords: Flare, KEEL, Classification, Solar Flares, Activity, Complexity
