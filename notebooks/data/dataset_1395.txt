The dataset named BNG Autos contains information about various automobiles, categorized under the tags Artificial, BNG, Chemistry, and Life Science.

This comprehensive dataset features a range of characteristics, including Normalized Losses, Make, Fuel Type, Aspiration, Number of Doors, Body Style, Drive Wheels, Engine Location, Wheel Base, Length, Width, Height, Curb Weight, Engine Type, Number of Cylinders, Engine Size, Fuel System, Bore, Stroke, Compression Ratio, Horsepower, Peak RPM, City MPG, Highway MPG, and Price.

Additionally, the dataset includes a feature called Symboling.
