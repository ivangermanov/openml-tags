This dataset is part of a collection of forty-one drug design datasets. These datasets were created using Adriana.Code software, which generated one thousand one hundred forty-three features. The molecules and output values were obtained from the original studies.

The original studies include Carbolenes, MTP Two, Chang, Cristalli, Depreux, Doherty, Garrat Two, Garrat, Heyl, Krystek, Lewis, Penning, Rosowsky, Siddiqi, Stevenson, Strupcz, Svensson, Thompson, Tsutumi, Uejling, Yokoyama One, and Yokoyama Two.

Carbolenes was studied in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen ninety-six. MTP Two was researched in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computer Science in two thousand three. Chang, Cristalli, Depreux, Doherty, Garrat Two, Garrat, Heyl, Krystek, Lewis, Penning, Rosowsky, Siddiqi, Stevenson, Strupcz, Svensson, Thompson, Tsutumi, Uejling, Yokoyama One, and Yokoyama Two were studied in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen ninety-six.

Benzo Thirty-Two was researched in the European Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen ninety-six. Phenetyl One was studied in the book QSAR: Hansch Analysis and Related Approaches in nineteen ninety-three. PAH was researched in the journal Chemometrics and Intelligent Laboratory Systems in nineteen ninety-five.

PDGF-R was studied in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computer Science in two thousand four. Phen was researched in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen seventy-two. Topo Two One and Y Prop Four One were studied in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computer Science in two thousand three.

QSAR BR One, QSAR BR Two, QSAR Tox, QSBR RW One, QSBR Y Two, QSBR Alks, QSFR DH LA, QSFSR One, QSFSR Two, and QSPrCmpX were also part of these original studies.
