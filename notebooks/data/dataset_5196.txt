Rossmann Store Sales Processed Dataset 

Tags: Rossmann Store Sales from Kaggle 

This dataset contains various features related to store sales. The dataset includes information about the Store itself, including the Day of the Week, whether there was a Promotion, any State Holidays, School Holidays, the type of Store, the assortment of products available, the distance to competing stores, and when promotions started. It also includes temporal data such as the Year, Month, Day, and Week of the Year. Additionally, it provides information on whether competing stores were open during promotions and if the month was a promotional month. The dataset also contains the target variable, Sales, which represents the sales amount for each store, as well as a feature indicating the set.

Keywords: Rossmann, Store Sales, Promotions, Competing Stores, Temporal Data, Sales Amount.
