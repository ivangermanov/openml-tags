Kaggle Survey 2017-2020 Merged Data 
Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning 

Every year, Kaggle conducts an industry-wide survey that presents a truly comprehensive view of the state of data science and machine learning. This extensive dataset combines the data from the past four years, providing a rich source of information on the trends and developments in this field.

The data was carefully acquired and cleaned by the Kaggle team. The merging of the datasets across the years was done using a specialized notebook.

This comprehensive dataset features a wide range of variables, including demographic information, professional details, and company characteristics. These include age, gender, country, degree, job title, company size, team size, machine learning status in company, compensation status, money spent, and year.

Keywords: data science, machine learning, industry survey, trends, developments, demographic information, professional details, company characteristics.
