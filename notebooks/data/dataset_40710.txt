Life Science and Medicine Dataset

The Cleve dataset features various attributes related to heart disease diagnosis, including:

* Age
* Sex
* Type of chest pain
* Blood pressure at rest (Trest Blood Pressure)
* Total serum cholesterol level
* Fasting blood sugar level below 120 mg/dl
* Results of resting electrocardiogram (ECG)
* Maximum heart rate achieved during exercise
* Whether exercise induced angina was present
* The old peak value of ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest
* The slope of the peak exercise ST segment
* The number of major vessels colored by fluoroscopy
* The thallium stress test outcome

Class label indicating the presence or absence of heart disease.

Keywords: Heart Disease Diagnosis, Chest Pain, Blood Pressure, Cholesterol Level, Electrocardiogram, Angina, Fluoroscopy, Thallium Stress Test
