QSAR-TID-65 Dataset: Chemistry, Life Science, MTLQSAR, Study 3

This dataset contains QSAR data showing activity values of several compounds on drug target TID: 65. It has 1515 rows and 1026 features, including IDs and class feature: Molecule ChEMBL ID and Median PXc50. The features represent FCFP 1024bit Molecular Fingerprints, which were generated from SMILES strings using the Pipeline Pilot program, Dassault Syst√®mes BIOVIA.

The dataset provides a comprehensive collection of chemical structures and their corresponding biological activities, enabling researchers to explore the relationships between molecular properties and biological responses.

Cytochrome P450 19A1 is an enzyme that plays a crucial role in various physiological processes, including steroid biosynthesis and metabolism. It is involved in the conversion of androgens to estrogens and is also responsible for the aromatization of testosterone to estradiol.

The dataset includes information about the protein target classification, organism, species group, and synonyms. It also provides access to various databases, such as Expression Atlas, Go Component, Go Function, and Go Process, which offer insights into the gene expression, function, and biological processes related to Cytochrome P450 19A1.
