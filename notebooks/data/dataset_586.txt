Sleep Dataset 

Tags: Binarized, Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Study 

This dataset is a binarized version of the original data set. It involves converting the numeric target feature into a two-class nominal target feature by calculating the mean and categorizing all instances with a lower target value as Positive and all others as Negative. 

The dataset includes various features related to physical characteristics and environmental factors, including Body Weight, Brain Weight, Maximum Life Span, Gestation Time, Predation Index, Sleep Exposure Index, and Danger Index. These features aim to provide insights into the relationships between these variables and the Sleep phenomenon.
