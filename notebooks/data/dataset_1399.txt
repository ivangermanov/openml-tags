The BNG (autos) dataset is an artificial collection of automotive data. It encompasses a wide range of vehicle characteristics and performance metrics. The dataset includes information on various aspects of automobiles, from basic specifications like make and body style to more technical details such as engine type and fuel system. Performance-related features like horsepower, fuel efficiency (both city and highway), and price are also included. The dataset aims to provide a comprehensive view of automotive attributes for analysis and modeling purposes.
