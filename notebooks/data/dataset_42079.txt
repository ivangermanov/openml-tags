House Sales

This dataset contains house sale prices for King County, which includes Seattle. It includes homes sold between a specific time period. It contains nineteen house features plus the price and the identification columns, along with a large number of observations. It's a great dataset for evaluating simple regression models.

The dataset includes a unique identification for each home sold, the date of the home sale, the price of each home sold, the number of bedrooms, the number of bathrooms, where a half bathroom accounts for a room with a toilet but no shower, the square footage of the apartment's interior living space, the square footage of the land space, the number of floors, a dummy variable for whether the apartment was overlooking the waterfront or not, an index of how good the view of the property was, an index on the condition of the apartment, an index on the grade of the apartment, the square footage of the interior housing space that is above ground level, the square footage of the interior housing space that is below ground level, the year the house was initially built, the year of the house's last renovation, what zip code area the house is in, the latitude, the longitude, the square footage of interior housing living space for the nearest neighbors, and the square footage of the land lots of the nearest neighbors.

Keywords: House Sales, King County, Seattle, Regression Models, Real Estate
