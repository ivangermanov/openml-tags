**Vineyard**
Tags: Author, Source, Dataset 

The Vineyard dataset originates from an unknown source and lacks proper citation. This dataset is part of the collection available at ftp stat.cmu.edu/datasets. It was featured in the book Smoothing Methods in Statistics, published by Springer-Verlag.

The dataset contains several features, including Row Number, Lugs for the year nineteen eighty-nine, Lugs for the year nineteen ninety, and Lugs for the year nineteen ninety-one.
