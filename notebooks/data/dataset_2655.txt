**FOREX eur pln hour Close**

Tags: Astronomy, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Hour, Geography

**Data Description**
This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex Euro Polish Zloty exchange rate from Dukascopy. Each instance represents a single hourly candlestick. The dataset spans from January first to December thirteenth and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is closed during these periods. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe Amsterdam time zone.

The dataset has been carefully curated to provide a comprehensive view of the exchange rate fluctuations over time. Each hourly interval is represented by a unique set of features, including bid and ask prices, volume data, and more.

**Attributes**

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point, specified in the Europe Amsterdam time zone.
* Bid Open: The opening bid price for the given hourly interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during the hourly interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during the hourly interval.
* Bid Close: The closing bid price for the given hourly interval.
* Bid Volume: The number of times the bid price changed within the hourly interval.
* Ask Open: The opening ask price for the given hourly interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during the hourly interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during the hourly interval.
* Ask Close: The closing ask price for the given hourly interval.
* Ask Volume: The number of times the ask price changed within the hourly interval.
* Class: A binary indicator specifying whether the average price will increase or decrease during the subsequent hourly interval.
