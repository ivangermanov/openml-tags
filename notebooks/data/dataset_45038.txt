Road Safety 

Tags: Astronomy, Computer Systems 

This dataset belongs to the classification on both numerical and categorical features benchmark. It contains data reported to the police about the circumstances of personal injury road accidents in Great Britain, including the maker and model information of vehicles involved in the respective accident.

The dataset features include vehicle type, vehicle maneuver, location of the vehicle, object hit in or off the carriageway, whether the vehicle was left-hand drive, age of driver, age band of driver, engine capacity, propulsion code, age of vehicle, location coordinates, police force, number of vehicles, number of casualties, local authority district, road numbers, urban or rural area, casualty reference, sex of casualty, age of casualty, age band of casualty, pedestrian location, pedestrian movement, casualty type, casualty IMD decile, and sex of driver.

Keywords: Road Safety, Accidents, Vehicles, Drivers, Casualties, Location Coordinates.
