Rossmann Store Sales from Kaggle with some pre-processing. This dataset features the Store, Day of the Week, whether there is a Promotion, State Holiday, School Holiday, type of Store, Assortment, distance to the nearest Competition, the number of weeks and years since the second Promotion started, the Year, Month, Day, Week of the Year, whether the Competition is Open, whether the Promotion is Open, and whether it is a Promotion Month. The target variable is Sales, and it is part of a Set.

Keywords: Rossmann, Store Sales, Kaggle, Promotion, Competition, Sales
