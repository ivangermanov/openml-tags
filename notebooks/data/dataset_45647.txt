The Rossmann Store Sales dataset from Kaggle has undergone some preprocessing to enhance its usability. This dataset falls under the categories of Chemistry and Life Science. It comprises various features, including the type of store, day of the week, promotion status, state holiday, school holiday, store type, assortment, distance to competitors, duration since the start of promotion two, year promotion two began, calendar year, month, day, week of the year, competition open status, promotion open status, and whether it is a promotional month. Additionally, the dataset includes sales data and a set identifier.

Keywords: Rossmann Store Sales, Chemistry, Life Science, Promotion Status, Store Type, Assortment, Distance to Competitors, Sales Data.
