Name: Road Safety

Tags: Road Safety

Data reported to the police about the circumstances of personal injury road accidents in Great Britain from the late 1970s, and the maker and model information of vehicles involved in the respective accident. This version includes data up to the mid-2010s.

Features: Vehicle Reference, Vehicle Type, Vehicle Manoeuvre, Vehicle Location in Restricted Lane, Object Hit in Carriageway, Object Hit off Carriageway, Driver Age, Driver Age Band, Engine Capacity, Propulsion Code, Vehicle Age, Location Easting, Location Northing, Longitude, Latitude, Police Force, Number of Vehicles Involved, Number of Casualties, Local Authority District, First Road Number, Second Road Number, Vehicle Reference, Casualty Reference, Casualty Age, Casualty Age Band, Pedestrian Location, Pedestrian Movement, Casualty Type, Casualty IMD Decile, Was Vehicle Left Hand Drive, Urban or Rural Area, Casualty Sex, Class

Keywords: Road Accidents, Vehicle Data, Police Reports, Great Britain, Personal Injury, Vehicle Safety
