**Data Description**
This dataset describes social mobility, which is the relationship between the occupations of sons and their fathers. Each instance represents the number of sons who have a certain job given that their father has a specific job, taking into account the son's race and family structure.

**Attribute Description**
The dataset consists of the following attributes:

* Father's Occupation
* Son's Occupation
* Family Structure, which can be either intact or nonintact
* Race, which can be either black or white
* Counts for Son's First Occupation
* Counts for Son's Current Occupation, which is the target feature

Keywords: social mobility, occupation, family structure, race, son, father
