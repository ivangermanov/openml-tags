The dataset BNG Echo Months is categorized under Artificial, BNG, Chemistry, and Life Science tags. This dataset possesses various features including Still Alive status, Age, Pericardial information, Fractional data, and Wall Score Index. Additionally, it includes data on Left Ventricular Diastolic Diameter and Epicardial Surface Score. The dataset also tracks whether subjects were Alive at One month mark and their corresponding classification.
