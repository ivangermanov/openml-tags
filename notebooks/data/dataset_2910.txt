Employee Salaries

Tags: Computer Systems, Demographics, Ethical Issues

Annual salary information, including gross pay and overtime pay, for all active, permanent employees of Montgomery County, Maryland, paid in calendar year. This information will be published annually each year, providing transparency and accountability in government salaries.

Features:

* Full Name
* Gender
* Current Annual Salary
* Gross Pay Received
* Overtime Pay
* Department
* Department Name
* Division
* Assignment Category
* Employee Position Title
* Underfilled Job Title
* Date First Hired
* Year First Hired

Keywords: salaries, government transparency, employee data, demographics, computer systems, ethical issues.
