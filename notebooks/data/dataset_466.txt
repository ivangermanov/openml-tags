Schizo Dataset 

The Schizo dataset is a collection of data related to eye-tracking studies on individuals with schizophrenia. This dataset is classified under the mythbusting category, specifically focusing on study number one, as well as other studies including one twenty-three, one fifty-two, forty-one, twenty, fifteen, and one.

The dataset is characterized by its nominal class type, with the class index being the last feature. The features included in this dataset are identification, target, and several gain ratio metrics. Unfortunately, the specific details of these gain ratio metrics are not readily interpretable. Additionally, the dataset includes information about the individual's sex and class.

Keywords: schizophrenia, eye-tracking, mythbusting, study data, biometrics
