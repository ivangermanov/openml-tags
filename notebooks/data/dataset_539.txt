The dataset "analcatdata galapagos" is sourced from StatLib and was used in the book "Analyzing Categorical Data" published by Springer-Verlag in New York. This book provides an in-depth analysis of categorical data, offering insights into statistical methodologies and their applications. Further information about the book, including details on statistical software used, can be found on the associated website. The dataset contains various features related to the Galapagos Islands, including observations of species, native species populations, geographical measurements such as area and elevation, and distances between islands. These features provide a comprehensive view of the ecological and geographical characteristics of the Galapagos archipelago, allowing for detailed statistical analysis and interpretation of the unique biodiversity found in this region.
