The CD4 dataset is categorized under Economics and Statistics, and its primary focus is on predicting CD4 count dates. The features included in this dataset are baseline CD4 counts, weeks elapsed since baseline until current CD4 measurement, current CD4 counts, weeks elapsed since baseline until the current line, and various antiretroviral therapy medications such as Azidothymidine, Lamivudine, Abacavir, Efavirenz, and several protease inhibitors. The ultimate goal is to predict future CD4 counts.

Keywords: CD4 count, antiretroviral therapy, protease inhibitors, economics, statistics.
