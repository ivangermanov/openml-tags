Beijing Multi Site Air Quality Tags: This dataset includes hourly air pollutants data from twelve nationally controlled air quality monitoring sites. 

The dataset features include the date and time of measurement, with the year, month, day, and hour, as well as the levels of various air pollutants such as Particulate Matter two point five, Particulate Matter ten, Sulfur Dioxide, Nitrogen Dioxide, Carbon Monoxide, and Ozone. 

Additionally, the dataset includes meteorological data such as Temperature, Pressure, Dew Point, Rain, Wind Direction, and Wind Speed, all of which are measured at each of the monitoring stations.

Keywords: Air Quality, Beijing, Pollutants, Monitoring Sites, Meteorological Data
