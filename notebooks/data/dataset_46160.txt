Name: Liver Dataset
Tags: Dataset from R Package JoineR
Features: Prothrombin Levels, Treatment Type, Time of Observation, Patient Status

This dataset, known as the Liver Dataset, is part of the R package JoineR. It contains various features related to liver health, including Prothrombin Levels, which indicate the liver's ability to produce clotting factors. The dataset also includes information on the Treatment Type received by patients, as well as the Time of Observation, which is the duration of the study. Additionally, the dataset provides information on the Patient Status, which indicates the outcome of the treatment.

Keywords: Liver Health, Prothrombin Levels, Treatment Type, Time of Observation, Patient Status
