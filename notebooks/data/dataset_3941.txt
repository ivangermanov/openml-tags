City Quality of Life Dataset 
Tags: Agriculture, Machine Learning 

Teleport.org provides comprehensive data related to the quality of life for the most creative cities around the world, thereby enabling users to find suitable cities for living and working according to their unique personal preferences and requirements. This dataset was carefully obtained from Teleport API endpoints and meticulously pre-processed before being uploaded to Kaggle.

The dataset comprises various significant features including City Name, Country, Continent, Housing, Cost of Living, Startup Ecosystem, Venture Capital, Travel Connectivity, Commute, Business Freedom, Safety, Healthcare, Education, Environmental Quality, Economy, Taxation, Internet Access, Leisure and Culture, Tolerance, and Outdoors.

Keywords: Quality of Life, Creative Cities, Personal Preferences, Startup Ecosystem, Environmental Quality, Internet Access, Leisure and Culture.
