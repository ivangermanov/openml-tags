Cars 

**Data Description**
The Cars dataset is a collection of information about various characteristics of General Motors vehicles and their corresponding suggested retail prices. This dataset was created by selecting a representative sample of over eight hundred 2005 General Motors cars and calculating their retail prices based on the tables provided in the 2005 Central Edition of the Kelley Blue Book.

**Attribute Description**
The attributes in this dataset describe different characteristics of the cars. The target feature is the Price of the vehicle. The other attributes include the car's Mileage, the number of Cylinders in its engine, the number of Doors, and whether it has Cruise control and a premium Sound system. Additionally, the dataset includes information about the type of upholstery, specifically whether the car has Leather seats. There are also several attributes that indicate the Make of the car, including Buick, Cadillac, Chevrolet, Pontiac, Saab, and Saturn. Finally, the dataset includes attributes that describe the Body Style of the car, including Convertible, Coupe, Hatchback, Sedan, and Wagon.
