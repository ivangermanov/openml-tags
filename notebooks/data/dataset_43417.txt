Ottawa Real Estate Data

Tags: Context Real Estate data from the Canadian capital city of Ottawa for you to test your data analytics skills.

This dataset provides a unique opportunity to explore the real estate market in Ottawa, Canada. The data was collected from real estate listings in Ottawa, with minor adjustments made to location information to ensure privacy.

The inspiration behind this dataset comes from Kaggle's introductory course on the Melbourne housing market. This dataset is designed to help individuals who have completed the course to practice and apply their skills in a new context.

The dataset includes a range of features that provide insight into the characteristics of properties in Ottawa. These features include latitude and longitude, postal code, walk score, property type, style, year built, number of bedrooms, number of bathrooms, parking information, garage details, lot depth, lot frontage, and price.
