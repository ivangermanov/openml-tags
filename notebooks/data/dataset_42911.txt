**Cervical Cancer Risk Factors Data Set**

The dataset was collected at the Hospital Universitario de Caracas in Caracas, Venezuela. It comprises demographic information, habits, and historic medical records of patients. Several patients decided not to answer some of the questions because of privacy concerns, resulting in missing values.

### Attribute Information

The dataset includes attributes such as Age, Number of Sexual Partners, Age of First Sexual Intercourse, Number of Pregnancies, Smoking Habits, Hormonal Contraceptives, Intrauterine Device (IUD) usage, Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs), and various diagnostic results.

### Features

The features of the dataset include demographic information, habits, and historic medical records of patients. These features are related to cervical cancer risk factors, including smoking habits, hormonal contraceptives, intrauterine device usage, and sexually transmitted diseases.
