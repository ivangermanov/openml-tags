Adult Income Dataset

Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning

This dataset is known as Adult Income Partitioned and consists of various features that describe individual characteristics. These features include Age, Work Class, Education Level, Education Number, Marital Status, Occupation, Relationship, Race, Sex, Capital Gain, Capital Loss, Hours Per Week, Native Country, and Income Flag.

The dataset also includes two additional indices, namely Data Mining Index and Partition Index. These indices are used to organize and analyze the data more efficiently.
