The Black Friday dataset is a collection of customer purchases made on the day after Thanksgiving, typically marked by heavy discounts and promotions. It falls under the category of regression on categorical and numerical features benchmark. This dataset has been transformed in a specific way to facilitate analysis.

The dataset comprises various features, including demographic information about customers, such as their gender and age, as well as their occupation and city of residence. Additionally, it includes details about their marital status and how long they have stayed in their current city. The products purchased are categorized into three categories, providing insight into consumer behavior.

Keywords: Black Friday, customer purchases, regression, categorical features, numerical features, demographic information, consumer behavior.
