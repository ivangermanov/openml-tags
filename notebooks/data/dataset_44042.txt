Black Friday Dataset

Tags: Dataset used in the Tabular Data Benchmark, transformed in the same way. This dataset belongs to the "Regression on Categorical and Numerical Features" benchmark.

Original Description: This dataset contains customer purchases made on Black Friday. It features a range of customer demographics and purchase information, including gender, age, occupation, city category, and years stayed in the current city. Additionally, it includes marital status and product categories, providing a comprehensive view of customer behavior. The target variable is the purchase amount, allowing for regression analysis on the impact of these factors on customer spending.

Keywords: Black Friday, customer purchases, demographics, regression analysis, categorical features, numerical features.
