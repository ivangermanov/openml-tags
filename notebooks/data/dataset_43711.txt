Countries of the World

Tags: Context World fact sheet, fun to link with other datasets

This dataset provides information on various aspects of countries around the world, including population, region, area size, infant mortality, and more. It is a valuable resource for creating visualizations and analyzing data related to countries.

The data is sourced from the US government and is generally free to use, although users outside of the US may need to contact the US government for permission. The World Factbook, which is the primary source of the data, is in the public domain and can be used freely by anyone at any time without seeking permission.

When working with country-related data, it is often interesting to group countries by attributes such as region or weigh their importance by population, GDP, or other variables. This dataset provides a range of features that can be used for this purpose.

Features:

* Country
* Region
* Population
* Area in square miles
* Population density per square mile
* Coastline to area ratio
* Net migration
* Infant mortality per one thousand births
* GDP per capita
* Literacy percentage
* Phones per one thousand people
* Arable land percentage
* Crops percentage
* Other land use percentage
* Climate
* Birth rate
* Death rate
* Agriculture
* Industry
* Service
