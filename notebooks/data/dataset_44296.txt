**Meta-Album OmniPrint-MD-5-bis Dataset (Mini)**

The OmniPrint-MD-5-bis dataset consists of synthesized images from seven hundred and six categories. The images are generated using the OmniPrint synthesis tool with a specific set of parameters, including font size, image size, random perspective transformation, margins, pre-rasterization elastic transformation, random translation, image blending method, rotation, and horizontal shear. The foreground of the images is filled with a random color, and the background consists of images downloaded from Pexels.
