DRSongsLyrics Tags: This dataset contains lyrics of songs for the rock bands The Rolling Stones and Deep Purple. The two bands are equally represented in the dataset, with an equal number of songs from each band. This dataset was extracted from a much larger dataset of song lyrics. Features include a unique identifier, the song lyrics themselves, and the class or category of the song.

Keywords: song lyrics, rock bands, The Rolling Stones, Deep Purple, dataset
