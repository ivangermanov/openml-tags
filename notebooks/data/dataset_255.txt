Tags: artificial, study, study

Features: Wife's age, Wife's education, Husband's education, Number of children ever born, Wife's religion, Is wife currently working?, Husband's occupation, Standard of living index, Media exposure, Contraceptive method used

This dataset focuses on contraceptive method choice and related factors among married couples. It includes information on the wife's age, education level, and religious affiliation, as well as the husband's education and occupation. The dataset also captures details about the number of children born to the couple, the wife's employment status, the family's standard of living, and their exposure to media. The primary outcome of interest is the contraceptive method used by the couple.

Keywords: contraception, married couples, education, employment, family planning, socioeconomic factors
