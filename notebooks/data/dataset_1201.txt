Name: BNG (Breast Tumor)

Tags: Artificial, BNG

Features: Age, Menopause, Involved Nodes, Node Caps, Degree of Malignancy, Breast, Breast Quadrant, Irradiation, Recurrence, Class

This dataset, known as BNG (Breast Tumor), is an artificial collection of data related to breast cancer. It encompasses various features that are crucial in the analysis and diagnosis of breast tumors. These features include the patient's age, menopausal status, the number of involved lymph nodes, the presence of node caps, the degree of malignancy, breast characteristics, the specific quadrant of the breast affected, whether irradiation was used in treatment, the likelihood of recurrence, and the overall classification of the tumor.

Keywords: Breast tumor, Cancer diagnosis, Artificial dataset, Medical features, Tumor classification
