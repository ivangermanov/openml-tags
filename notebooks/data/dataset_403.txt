One of the datasets used in the book Analyzing Categorical Data is a collection of records related to accidents in Michigan. This dataset was obtained from StatLib.

The dataset contains information about the time index, season, month, unemployment rate, and number of accidents. 

For further details concerning the book, including information on statistical software, please visit the website.
