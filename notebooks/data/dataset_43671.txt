This dataset, known as Predicting Critical Heat Flux, falls under the categories of Computer Systems and Machine Learning. It was prepared for a journal article titled "On the Prediction of Critical Heat Flux Using a Physics-Informed Machine Learning-Aided Framework". 

The dataset comprises processed and compiled records of experimental critical heat flux and boundary conditions utilized for the research presented in the article. 

Acknowledgement is given to the data source.

Features of this dataset include identification number, geometry, pressure, mass flux, exit equilibrium quality, effective diameter, hydraulic diameter, length, and experimental critical heat flux.

Keywords: critical heat flux, machine learning, computer systems, physics-informed, boundary conditions, experimental data.
