The SVM Guide One Dataset is categorized under Chemistry and Life Science. This dataset originates from the LIBSVM data repository. 

The original data is derived from an astroparticle application courtesy of Uppsala University, Sweden. 

A Practical Guide to Support Vector Classification provides more information on this dataset.

The dataset consists of four features.

Please cite the original technical report from the Department of Computer Science at National Taiwan University.
