This Motor Third Party Liability Pricing dataset describes insurance policies and their corresponding claim counts. The dataset is a comprehensive collection of one million insurance policies, providing valuable insights into the factors that influence claim counts.

The dataset is part of the SHAP for Actuaries tutorial, which aims to explain any model. The tutorial is a valuable resource for actuaries and data scientists, providing a comprehensive guide to understanding and interpreting complex models.

The dataset features a range of variables, including the year, town, driver's age, car's weight, car's power, and car's age. These variables provide a detailed understanding of the factors that influence claim counts, allowing actuaries and data scientists to develop more accurate models.

Keywords: Motor Third Party Liability, Insurance Policies, Claim Counts, SHAP, Actuaries, Data Science.
