This analytic dataset, doa_bwin, tagged as virtual live action betting data, contains actual betting behavior aggregated over a one-month period from the date the account was opened, as well as live action betting data for each participant aggregated over the entire study period.

The dataset encompasses various aspects of participants' betting activities, including their country of origin, gender, and age. Additionally, it provides insights into their betting patterns, such as the total amount staked, number of bets placed, and frequency of betting.

A comprehensive analysis of participants' betting behavior is facilitated by the inclusion of data on their daily betting activities, intervals between bets, and net outcomes. Furthermore, the dataset offers standardized metrics, such as the z-score of daily bets and stake standard deviation, allowing for a more nuanced understanding of participants' betting tendencies.

Keywords: virtual live action betting, betting behavior, participant data, analytics, gambling
