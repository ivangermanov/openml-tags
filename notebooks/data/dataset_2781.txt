FOREX EUR USD Hourly Close Dataset

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Hour, Transportation

This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex EUR/USD pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one hourly candlestick. The dataset covers the period from January to December and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is closed during these days. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.

The dataset provides information on various aspects of the Forex market, including bid and ask prices, volumes, and directions of price movements. The class attribute indicates whether the mean close price is expected to increase or decrease in the following hour.

Attributes:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the start of the hourly interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price reached during the hourly interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price reached during the hourly interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of the hourly interval.
* Bid Volume: The number of times the bid price changed within the hourly interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the start of the hourly interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price reached during the hourly interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price reached during the hourly interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of the hourly interval.
* Ask Volume: The number of times the ask price changed within the hourly interval.
* Class: Whether the average price is expected to rise or fall in the next hourly interval.
