NBA Shot Logs

Tags: National Basketball Association

This dataset features a comprehensive collection of shot logs, including Game Identification, Matchup Information, Location of the Game, Win or Loss Indicator, Final Point Margin, Shot Sequence Number, Period of the Game, Time Remaining on the Game Clock, Time Remaining on the Shot Clock, Number of Dribbles, Time Spent Touching the Ball, Distance of the Shot, Type of Points Scored, Result of the Shot, Identity of the Closest Defender, Distance of the Closest Defender, Field Goals Made, Points Scored, Player's Full Name, and Player's Unique Identification Number.

Keywords: NBA, Shot Logs, Game Data, Basketball Analytics
