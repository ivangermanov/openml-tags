NBA Shot Logs

Tags: Machine Learning, National Basketball Association, Transportation

This dataset contains information about shot attempts in NBA games, including details about the game itself, the shot attempt, and the players involved.

Features:

Game Identification, Matchup between teams, Location of the game, Win or Loss indicator, Final Margin of Victory, Shot Attempt Number, Period of the game, Time remaining on the Game Clock, Time remaining on the Shot Clock, Number of Dribbles taken, Touch Time of the ball, Distance of the Shot Attempt, Type of Points scored, Result of the Shot Attempt, Closest Defender to the shooter, Player Identification of the Closest Defender, Distance to the Closest Defender, Field Goals Made, Points scored, Player Name, and Player Identification.

Keywords: NBA, Machine Learning, Shot Attempts, Game Data, Player Statistics
