UFC 257 Poirier vs McGregor II Tweets

Context: The UFC 257 Poirier vs McGregor II event was a mixed martial arts extravaganza produced by the Ultimate Fighting Championship, which took place on a thrilling day in January at the iconic Etihad Arena on Yas Island, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.

This dataset comprises tweets gathered from a few days prior to the event and a day after, featuring at least one of the following hashtags: Ultimate Fighting Championship, UFC Fifty-Seven, UFC, Poirier, McGregor, Poirier McGregor, McGregor Poirier, Conor McGregor, Dustin Poirier, MMA, UFC Fight Island, and Fight Island.

Acknowledgments: Special thanks are extended to Tweepy, Twitter, Python, Jupyter, UFC, Kaggle for hosting this dataset, and Microsoft for Azure.

This dataset can be utilized for sentiment analysis related to the UFC 257 event, providing valuable insights into the opinions and emotions of fans and enthusiasts.

Features: username, account description, location, following, followers, total tweets, user creation timestamp, tweet creation timestamp, retweet count, text, hashtags, and language.

Keywords: UFC, MMA, Fight Island, Sentiment Analysis, Tweets, Mixed Martial Arts
