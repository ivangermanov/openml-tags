Delays Zurich Transport 

Tags: Machine Learning, Sociology 

Dataset used in the Tabular Data Benchmark, transformed in the same way. This dataset belongs to the Regression on Numerical Features benchmark. 

Original Description: The Zurich Public Transport Delay data was collected between October thirty, three thirty AM Central European Time and November twenty seventh, one twenty AM Central European Time, cleaned and prepared during Open Data Day. 

This dataset contains various features, including temperature, wind speed maximum, wind speed average, precipitation, dew point, humidity, hour of the day, and minute of the day, which were used to predict transportation delays.

Keywords: Transportation Delays, Machine Learning, Zurich, Tabular Data, Regression Analysis
