This dataset, titled Best Books of the Nineteenth Century, falls under the categories of Life Science and Medicine. It was obtained by scraping data from Goodreads to gather information about the most acclaimed books of the nineteenth century.

The dataset contains various features that provide insight into each book. The title of each book is listed under Book Name. A brief summary of the book is provided under Description, giving readers an idea of what the book is about. The average rating given by Goodreads users is listed under Book Rating, indicating how well-received the book was by its audience. Finally, the link to the book's cover image is listed under Cover Image Link.

Keywords: Nineteenth Century, Literature, Goodreads, Life Science, Medicine
