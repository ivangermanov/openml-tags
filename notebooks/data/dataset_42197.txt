This dataset, Premier League Matches, contains all Premier League matches with player statistics taken from Sofifa. The dataset spans multiple seasons, providing a comprehensive overview of the league's performance. 

The dataset features include the date of each match, the number of goals scored by the home team and the away team, as well as various statistics related to the home team's and away team's attacking and defensive capabilities. These statistics include the best attack and defense players, average attack and defense ratings, and standard deviations of attack and defense ratings. Additionally, the dataset includes information about the goalkeepers for both the home and away teams.

Keywords: Premier League, football, matches, player statistics, Sofifa.
