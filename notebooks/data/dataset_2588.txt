Conference Attendance 

Tags: Earth Science, Meteorology 

This data set showcases information related to the attendees of a mathematics conference. The primary focus is on predicting whether an individual attended the conference, which is represented by the target column labeled as Presence Indicator. 

The available features include the university affiliation, preferred t-shirt size, favorite subject area, dietary preferences for vegetarian meals, level of participation, and registration date categorized accordingly.

Keywords: Conference, Mathematics, Attendance, Prediction, University Affiliation
