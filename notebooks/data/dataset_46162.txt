The Lung Dataset from the R Package Survival

This dataset is tagged as Lung and is part of the R package Survival. The dataset features several variables, including the institution where the patient was treated, the time of the study, the status of the patient, the patient's age, sex, and several physiological and health-related metrics. These metrics include the patient's Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group performance status, the Karnofsky performance score, the patient's Karnofsky score, the number of calories consumed per day, and the weight loss experienced by the patient.

Keywords: Lung Dataset, Survival Analysis, Patient Metrics, Health Data
