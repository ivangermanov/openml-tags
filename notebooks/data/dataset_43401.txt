Football Striker Performance Tags: Context

The aim of this dataset is to provide a comprehensive comparison of football players' performance, specifically strikers, across different European leagues, in a concise manner, with a limited number of columns. This dataset offers a unique opportunity to analyze and contrast the efficiency of strikers in various leagues.

Content

The dataset contains a range of performance indicators, including goals scored, assists provided, minutes played, and games played, for football strikers over the past five years. These indicators provide a detailed understanding of a player's performance and enable comparisons between players.

Acknowledgements

The data was extracted from a reliable online source, Transfermarkt.

Features

The dataset includes a range of features, such as age, current club, current league, foot, height, name, nationality, and position.
