This dataset, tagged under Computer Systems and Machine Learning, comprises a vast collection of information related to properties and their corresponding rents in various Indian cities and localities.

The dataset is a synthetic version of an original dataset, which provides a comprehensive overview of the different attributes of a property that influence its rent.

The attributes featured in this dataset include the city where the property is located, the area of the property, the number of rooms, bathrooms, and parking spaces available, the floor on which the property is situated, whether animals are allowed, if the property is furnished, the association tax, the amount of rent, property tax, fire insurance, and the total rent.

These attributes provide valuable insights into the factors that determine the rent of a property, enabling users to analyze and understand the rental market in Indian cities and localities.

Keywords: Indian cities, localities, property attributes, rent determination, machine learning, computer systems.
