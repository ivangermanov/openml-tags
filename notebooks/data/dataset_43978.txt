Credit Dataset: A Tabular Data Benchmark

This dataset is part of the classification on numerical features benchmark and has been transformed in a similar manner as the original dataset. The original source of this dataset is the Give Me Some Credit competition on Kaggle, and credit should be given to the original source if this dataset is used.

The dataset features include Revolving Utilization of Unsecured Lines, Age, Number of Times Thirty to Fifty-Nine Days Past Due Not Worse, Debt to Income Ratio, Monthly Income, Number of Open Credit Lines and Loans, Number of Times Ninety Days Late, Number of Real Estate Loans or Lines, Number of Times Sixty to Eighty-Nine Days Past Due Not Worse, Number of Dependents, and Serious Delinquency in Two Years.

Keywords: Credit Dataset, Tabular Data, Classification, Numerical Features, Kaggle Competition
