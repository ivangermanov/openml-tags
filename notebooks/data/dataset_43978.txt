Credit 

Tags: Machine Learning, Statistics 

This dataset is part of the tabular data benchmark and has been transformed in the same manner. It falls under the classification on numerical features benchmark category.

The original source of this dataset can be found at Give Me Some Credit competition overview page.

The dataset features include Revolving Utilization of Unsecured Lines, Age, Number of Times Thirty to Fifty-Nine Days Past Due Not Worse, Debt Ratio, Monthly Income, Number of Open Credit Lines and Loans, Number of Times Ninety Days Late, Number of Real Estate Loans or Lines, Number of Times Sixty to Eighty-Nine Days Past Due Not Worse, and Number of Dependents, all of which are used to predict Serious Delinquency in Two Years.
