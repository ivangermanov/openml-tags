COVID-19 Cases by Country

Tags: Context, COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic is spreading rapidly across the globe, and this dataset helps in analyzing the extent to which the pandemic has affected different countries. This dataset contains the total number of COVID-19 cases reported per country, providing valuable insights into the pandemic's impact on a global scale.

The dataset consists of six columns: Date, Location, Total Cases, New Cases, Total Deaths, and New Deaths. The data was collected from a reliable source, Our World in Data, a trusted platform for coronavirus data.

The inspiration behind this dataset is to create a bar chart race that visualizes the total cases reported by country, providing a dynamic and engaging way to understand the pandemic's progression.

Features: Date, Location, Total Cases, New Cases, Total Deaths, New Deaths

Keywords: COVID-19, pandemic, global impact, country-wise data, coronavirus cases, deaths.
