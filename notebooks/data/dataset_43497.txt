Five Years Historical Stock Quotes

Tags: Context

This dataset provides daily price information for stocks, aggregated into one comprehensive file. The data was collected using an application programming interface and contains general price information for all stocks that are tradable. The fields included in this dataset are the volume of trades, open and close prices, as well as high and low prices for each day. The data spans from February of a certain year and collects daily prices up to February of another year.

The dataset features a range of fields, including the date and time of each trading day, the opening price of each stock, the closing price of each stock, the highest price reached by each stock, the lowest price reached by each stock, and the total volume of trades for each stock. The dataset also includes the stock symbol for each entry.

Keywords: Historical Stock Prices, Daily Trading Data, Stock Market Information, Tradable Stocks, Financial Data.
