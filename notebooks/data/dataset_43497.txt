Five Years Historical Stock Quotes

Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning Context

This dataset comprises daily price information for stocks, aggregated into one comprehensive file. The data was gathered utilizing an application programming interface and encompasses general price information for all stocks that are actively tradable. The fields included in this dataset consist of the volume of trades, open and close prices, as well as the highest and lowest prices recorded during the trading day.

The historical data spans from February two thousand sixteen and accumulates daily prices up until February two thousand twenty-one. This extensive collection of stock quotes provides valuable insights into market trends, price fluctuations, and trading volumes over an extended period.

Features: date and time, opening price, closing price, highest price, lowest price, trade volume, stock symbol

Keywords: historical stock quotes, daily price information, tradable stocks, market trends, price fluctuations, trading volumes
