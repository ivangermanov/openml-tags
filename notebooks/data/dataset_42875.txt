Laboratorio Dataset Car 

Tags: Images, Life Science 

The Laboratorio Dataset Car is focused on images related to cars and life science. This dataset includes various features that describe different aspects of cars. The buying feature is concerned with the purchase of a car, including factors that influence this decision. Another important aspect is maintenance, which involves the upkeep and repair of vehicles. The doors feature refers to the different types of doors available in cars. Additionally, the person feature may include information about the driver or passengers. Other significant features include safety and class.
