Pigs Bayesian Network Sample. 

This Bayesian network is part of the bnlearn Bayesian Network Repository. It is a discrete and very large network, consisting of four hundred forty-one nodes and five hundred ninety-two arcs. The network has five thousand six hundred eighteen parameters, with an average Markov blanket size of three point six six and an average degree of two point six eight. The maximum in-degree of the network is two.

The network features a range of variables, including those related to pig health and behavior. 

Keywords: Bayesian network, pigs, discrete, large network, Markov blanket.
