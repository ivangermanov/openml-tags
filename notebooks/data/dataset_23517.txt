The dataset is a collection of cleaned, regularized, and encrypted global equity data. The first twenty-one columns, which are the features, are used to predict a binary class target. These features are attributes that have been carefully selected and processed to provide a robust and informative dataset for modeling.

The dataset is designed to be used for training machine learning models, and the encryption of the data ensures that the underlying stock market information remains confidential.

Please cite the dataset as the Encrypted Stock Market Training Data from Numerai.
