**Dataset Description**

Twenty-five personality self-report items taken from the International Personality Item Pool were included as part of the Synthetic Aperture Personality Assessment web-based personality assessment project. The data from a large group of subjects are included here as a demonstration set for scale construction, factor analysis, and Item Response Theory analysis. This dataset provides a comprehensive collection of responses to facilitate in-depth analysis and exploration of personality traits. Additionally, three demographic variables - sex, education level, and age - are also included to provide a more nuanced understanding of the participants.

Features: Agreeableness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Neuroticism, Openness, Sex, Education Level, Age

Keywords: Personality Assessment, Item Response Theory, Factor Analysis, Demographic Variables, Synthetic Aperture Personality Assessment
