Daily Bike Dataset

Tags: Daily Bike

Features:

The data includes information about the Day, Month, Year, Season, whether it was a Holiday, what day of the Week it was, if it was a Working Day, the Weather Situation, Temperature, Atmospheric Temperature, Humidity, and Wind Speed, all of which are correlated to the number of Rentals.

Keywords: Daily Bike, Dataset, Bicycle Rentals, Weather Data, Time Series Analysis
