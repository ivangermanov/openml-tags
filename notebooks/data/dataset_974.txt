Hayes-Roth Dataset: A Collection of Data for Chemistry, Life Science, and Mythbusting Studies

This dataset has undergone significant transformations to enhance its usability. Specifically, the multi-class target feature has been converted into a two-class nominal target feature. This conversion involved relabeling the majority class as Positive and all other classes as Negative.

The dataset comprises several essential features, including Hobby, Age, Educational Level, Marital Status, and Binary Class.

Keywords: Hayes-Roth, Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Data Conversion, Binary Classification
