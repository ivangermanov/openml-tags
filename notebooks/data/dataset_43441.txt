The Office Dataset
Tags: Context

The Office is an American mockumentary sitcom television series that depicts the everyday lives of office employees in the Scranton, Pennsylvania branch of the fictional Dunder Mifflin Paper Company. This series provides a humorous and relatable portrayal of the daily interactions and relationships among the office staff.

Content
The dataset consists of twelve columns and numerous rows, carefully extracted from IMDb. This comprehensive collection of data offers valuable insights into the show's various aspects.

Acknowledgements
The dataset is sourced from IMDb, a reputable online platform providing information on movies, television shows, and celebrities.

Features
The dataset includes information on the season, episode title, a brief summary, ratings, votes, viewership, duration, air date, guest stars, director, and writers.
