Dresses Sales Dataset

This dataset contains attributes of dresses and their recommendations based on their sales. The sales are monitored on an alternating day basis. The attributes present and analyzed are recommendation, style, price, rating, size, season, neckline, sleeve length, waistline, material, fabric type, decoration, pattern, and type.

The recommendation attribute refers to whether the dress is recommended for sale or not. The style attribute includes various styles such as bohemian, brief, casual, cute, fashion, flare, novelty, OL, party, sexy, vintage, and work. The price attribute ranges from low to very high. The rating attribute ranges from one to five. The size attribute includes small, medium, large, extra-large, and free sizes. The season attribute includes autumn, winter, spring, and summer seasons. 

The neckline attribute includes O-neck, backless, board neck, bow neck, halter, mandarin collar, open, Peter Pan collar, ruffled, scoop, slash neck, square collar, sweetheart, turndown collar, and V-neck. The sleeve length attribute includes full, half, half sleeves, butterfly, sleeveless, short, three-quarter, and turndown. 

The waistline attribute includes dropped, empire, natural, princess, and null. The material attribute includes wool, cotton, mix, and other materials. The fabric type attribute includes shafoon, dobby, poplin, satin, knitted, jersey, flannel, corduroy, and others. The pattern type attribute includes solid, animal, dot, leopard, and others.
