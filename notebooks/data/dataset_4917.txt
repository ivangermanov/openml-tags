**Barley Bayesian Network. Sample Nine**

The bnlearn Bayesian Network Repository references this dataset.

This dataset has forty-eight nodes and eighty-four arcs.

The average Markov blanket size is approximately five point two five, while the average degree is approximately three point five. The maximum in-degree is four.

This preliminary model for barley was developed under the project "Production of Beer from Danish Malting Barley Grown Without the Use of Pesticides".

Features of this dataset include total kernel volume, thousand kernel weight, and protein content, among others.

Keywords: Barley, Bayesian Network, Machine Learning, Life Science, Beer Production.
