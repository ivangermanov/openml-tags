**Meta Album Insects Dataset (Mini)**

The original Insects dataset is created by the National Museum of Natural History, Paris. It has a large collection of images in different sizes and orientations. The dataset has a hierarchical classification system, which includes Order, Super Family, Family, and Taxa. Each image features an insect in its natural environment or habitat, either on a flower or near vegetation. The images are collected by researchers and hundreds of volunteers from the SPIPOLL Science project.

**Dataset Details**

The Meta Album ID is Small Animals Insects. The dataset is available at the Meta Album website. The domain name is Small Animals, and the dataset name is Insects. The short description is Insects dataset from Science Project SPIPOLL. The dataset has a total of classes and images. The keywords associated with this dataset are insects and ecology. The data format is images, and the image size is 128x128. The license for the original data release is CC BY-NC 2.0, and the license URL is provided.
