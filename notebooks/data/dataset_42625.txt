The Train Dataset, categorized under Computer Systems and Machine Learning, contains a set of features that describe various aspects of passengers on a train journey.

These features include Passenger Identity, Survival Status, Passenger Class, Full Name, Gender, Age, Number of Siblings or Spouses on Board, Number of Parents or Children on Board, Ticket Details, Travel Fare, Cabin Information, and Port of Embarkation.

Keywords: Train Dataset, Computer Systems, Machine Learning, Passenger Identity, Survival Status
