This is one of forty-one drug design datasets. The datasets with eleven hundred forty-three features are formed using Adriana Code software. The molecules and outputs are taken from the original studies.

The dataset Carbolenes is related to a study published in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry.

The dataset MTP Two is related to a study published in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computer Science.

The datasets Chang, Cristalli, Depreux, Doherty, Garrat Two, Garrat, Heyl, Krystek, Lewis, Penning, Rosowsky, Siddiqi, Stevenson, Strupcz, Svensson, Thompson, Tsutumi, Uejling, Yokoyama One, and Yokoyama Two are related to a study published in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry.

The dataset MTP is related to a study published in the Journal of Chemical Information and Model.

The dataset Benzo Thirty-Two is related to a study published in the European Journal of Medicinal Chemistry.

The dataset Phenetyl One is related to a book titled QSAR: Hansch Analysis and Related Approaches.

The dataset PAH is related to a study published in the journal Chemometrics and Intelligent Laboratory Systems.

The dataset PDGFR is related to a study published in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computational Science.

The dataset Phen is related to a study published in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry.

The datasets Topo Two One and Y Prop Four One are related to a study published in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computational Science.

The datasets QSAR BR One, QSAR BR Two, QSAR Tox, QSBR RW One, QSBR Y Two, QSBR Alks, QSFR DH LA, QSFSR One, QSFSR Two, and QSPR CMPX are related to various studies published in different journals and books.

The dataset Selwood is related to a study published in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry.

Keywords: drug design, molecular descriptors, QSAR, medicinal chemistry, chemical information.
