This dataset, collected from Airbnb, is designed to explore how Airbnb is utilized in Turkey's vibrant city, Istanbul. The dataset comprises sixteen columns, showcasing essential information such as latitude, longitude, and price. As a result, this dataset lends itself well to regression problems, specifically predicting the price of a house.

The various features included in this dataset provide valuable insights into the characteristics of each listing. These characteristics include the type of accommodation, the neighborhood it is located in, and the number of reviews received. Additionally, the dataset includes information about the hosts themselves, such as their overall listings count and availability over a three hundred sixty-five day period.
