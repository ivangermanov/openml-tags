This dataset is a three-class version of the Cardiotocography dataset. It comprises various features, including those related to fetal heart rate and uterine contractions. These features are essential for understanding the complex relationships between maternal and fetal physiological processes during pregnancy and childbirth. The study of these phenomena is crucial in life science research.
