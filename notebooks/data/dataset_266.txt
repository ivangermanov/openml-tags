This is one of forty-one drug design datasets. The datasets with eleven hundred forty-three features are formed using Adriana Code software. The molecules and outputs are taken from the original studies.

The original studies include Carbolenes, which was published in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen ninety-six. Another study is MTP Two, which was published in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computer Science in two thousand three. Other studies include Chang, Cristalli, Depreux, Doherty, Garrat Two, Garrat, Heyl, Krystek, Lewis, Penning, Rosowsky, Siddiqi, Stevenson, Strupcz, Svensson, Thompson, Tsutumi, Uejling, Yokoyama One, and Yokoyama Two.

These studies were published in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen ninety-six. MTP was published in the Journal of Chemical Information and Model in two thousand five. Benzo Thirty-Two was published in the European Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen ninety-six.

PHENETYL One was published in QSAR Hansch Analysis and Related Approaches in nineteen ninety-three. PAH was published in Chemometrics and Intelligent Laboratory Systems in nineteen ninety-five. PDGFR was published in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computational Science in two thousand four.

Phen was published in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen seventy-two. Topo Two One and YProp Four One were published in the Journal of Chemical Information and Computational Science in two thousand three.

QSABR One, QSABR Two, QSARTOX, QSBR RW One, QSBR Y Two, QSBR Alks, QSFR DH LA, QSFSR One, QSFSR Two, and QSPR CMPX were also published in various journals.

Selwood was published in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry in nineteen ninety.
