Law School Admission Dataset (Binarized)

This dataset is a survey among students attending law school in the United States. The dataset was obtained from the R-package fairml. The response variable has been changed to a binary version, specifically whether the undergraduate grade point average is greater than three. The race variable has been binarized to labels, namely White and Non-White.

The dataset features include the student's age, decile rankings, family income, Law School Admission Test scores, undergraduate grade point average, gender, race, cluster, full-time status, and bar passage status. Additionally, the dataset includes a feature indicating whether the undergraduate grade point average is greater than three.

Keywords: Law School Admission, Binarized Dataset, Undergraduate Grade Point Average, Race, Law School Admission Test Scores, Family Income.
