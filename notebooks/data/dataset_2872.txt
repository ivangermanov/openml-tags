Iris Dataset Information with Ignored Features 

The Iris Dataset is categorized under the tags Computer Systems and Education. This dataset contains information about the Iris flowers, specifically focusing on their physical characteristics. 

The dataset consists of various features that describe the Iris flowers, including the length of their sepals, which is an important aspect of their anatomy. However, two features have been ignored for this particular purpose: the width of the sepals and the length of the petals. The remaining features include the length of the sepals, the width of the petals, and the species of the Iris flower.
