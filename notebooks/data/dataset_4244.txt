Reading Hydro Downstream Tags: Upstream Data from the Twin Archimedes Screw Hydro-Electric Generator on the River Thames at Caversham Weir, Reading, United Kingdom.

This dataset features information related to the timestamp and downstream data collected from the hydro-electric generator located at Caversham Weir on the River Thames in Reading.

Keywords: Hydro-Electric, Archimedes Screw, River Thames, Caversham Weir, Reading, United Kingdom
