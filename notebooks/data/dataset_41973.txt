Semeion Dataset: A Comprehensive Overview of Demographics and Government

This binarized version of the Semeion dataset focuses exclusively on instances with class labels one and two from the original dataset. The primary objective of this revised dataset is to provide a streamlined approach to analyzing demographics and government-related data.

The dataset encompasses an extensive range of features, including various variables that contribute significantly to understanding demographic trends and government policies. Although some feature names may appear technical, they collectively form a robust foundation for exploring complex relationships within the realm of demographics and governance.

By concentrating on a specific subset of class labels, researchers can delve deeper into the intricacies of demographics and government, ultimately leading to more accurate insights and informed decision-making.

Keywords: Demographics, Government, Class Labels, Data Analysis, Policy Insights
