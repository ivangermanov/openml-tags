**FOREX EUR/NZD Minute Close**

Tags: Economics, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Government

This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex EUR/NZD currency pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one minute. The dataset spans a specific period and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is not active on these days.

The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam timezone. The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean close price in the subsequent minute relative to the current minute. It takes a value of true if the mean close price increases in the following minute, and false if it decreases or remains unchanged.

The attributes included in this dataset are:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the beginning of the time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during the time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during the time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of the time interval.
* Bid Volume: The number of times the bid price changed within the time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the beginning of the time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during the time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during the time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of the time interval.
* Ask Volume: The number of times the ask price changed within the time interval.
* Class: Indicates whether the average price will increase in the next interval.
