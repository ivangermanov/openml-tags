Regime Alimentaire 
Tags: #modelage 

This dataset focuses on the Sex, Ethnic Origin, Age, Weight, Height, and eating habits of individuals. The diet-related features include consumption of bread, cereals, rice, pasta, semolina, potatoes, dried legumes, fresh vegetables, fruits, dairy products, meat or eggs, fish, ready-to-eat meals, sugary products, appetizer biscuits, dietary supplements, sweetened beverages, and alcoholic drinks. The presence of Overweight is also considered.

Keywords: Diet, Eating Habits, Nutrition, Food Consumption, Health
