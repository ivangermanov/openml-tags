The NFL Games dataset is categorized under Social Media, Text, and Literature tags. This dataset features information about each game, including the Date, Season, whether the game was played at a Neutral site, if it was a Playoff game, the two teams competing (Team One and Team Two), and their respective Elo ratings (Elo One and Elo Two). Additionally, the dataset provides the probability of each team winning based on their Elo ratings (Elo Probability One) and the actual scores for both teams (Score One and Score Two), ultimately resulting in the outcome of the game (Result One).

Keywords: NFL, games, social media, literature, sports data, team statistics.
