Context: Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses that may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome. The most recently discovered coronavirus causes Coronavirus Disease 2019, a global pandemic that has affected millions of people worldwide.

The dataset, COVID-19 INDIA Statewise, has information for states of India at a daily level. This dataset provides valuable insights into the spread of the virus across different regions of India.

The dataset is available in a CSV file named COVID19INDIA, which contains daily records of Coronavirus Disease 2019 cases in India. The data is made available to the general public by the Indian Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, and individual level details and testing details are available courtesy of covid19india.org.

The dataset features include the date, time, state or union territory, confirmed cases, cured cases, and deaths.

Keywords: Coronavirus, COVID-19, India, pandemic, respiratory infections, public health data.
