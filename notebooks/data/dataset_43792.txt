Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses that may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome. The most recently discovered coronavirus causes Coronavirus Disease 2019.

The number of new cases is increasing daily around the world. This dataset has information for states of India at a daily level.

The dataset COVID-19 INDIA contains COVID-19 cases at a daily level.

Acknowledgements are due to the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare for making the data available to the general public. Additionally, thanks are owed to covid19india.org for making individual-level details and testing details available to the general public.

The dataset includes the following information: Date, Time, State or Union Territory, Confirmed Cases, Recovered Cases, and Deaths.
