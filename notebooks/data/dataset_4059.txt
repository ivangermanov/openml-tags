The Medical Charges dataset is classified under the tags Machine Learning and Statistics. It is part of the Tabular Data Benchmark, which was transformed in a specific manner to facilitate analysis.

This dataset falls within the category of classification on numerical features, focusing on the Inpatient Utilization and Payment Public Use File. This file provides comprehensive information on inpatient discharges for Medicare fee-for-service beneficiaries. Specifically, it encompasses details on utilization, payment structures (including total payment and Medicare payment), and hospital-specific charges for over three thousand US hospitals receiving Medicare Inpatient Prospective Payment System payments.

The dataset is organized according to hospital and Medicare Severity Diagnosis Related Group, covering a range of fiscal years. 

Features include Total Discharges, Average Covered Charges, Average Medicare Payments, and Average Total Payments.
