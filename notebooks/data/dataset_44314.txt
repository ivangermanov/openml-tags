**Meta-Album Medicinal Leaf Dataset (Micro)**

The Medicinal Leaf Database gathers healthy and mature medicinal herbs from thirty species. The leaves are plucked from different plants of the same species and then placed on a white uniform background. The images are captured with a mobile phone camera. We create Medleaf for Meta-Album by cropping them at the center and resizing to 128x128 pixels.
