Munin Bayesian Network. Sample.

The Munin dataset is a massive discrete Bayesian network repository, comprising over one thousand nodes and nearly fourteen hundred arcs, with a staggering eighty thousand plus parameters. The average Markov blanket size is approximately three point five four, while the average degree is around two point six eight, with a maximum in-degree of three.

This expert EMG assistant is designed to provide valuable insights into electromyography and expert systems, showcasing its capabilities in computer-aided electromyography.

Features include muscle attributes, such as deltoid muscle quality, median deltoid muscle duration, and axillary deltoid muscle, as well as attributes related to the anterior branch of the posterior interosseous nerve, including its quality and polyphasicity. Other notable features encompass various aspects of the median nerve, ulnar nerve, and radial nerve, including their respective durations, amplitudes, and degrees of denervation.

Keywords: Bayesian Network, Electromyography, Muscle Attributes, Nerve Attributes, Computer-Aided Diagnosis.
