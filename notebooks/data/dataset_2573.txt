This dataset, known as DRSongsLyrics, is categorized under the topics of Economics and Manufacturing. It comprises a collection of three hundred fifty-eight song lyrics from two iconic rock bands, The Rolling Stones and Deep Purple, with equal representation from both bands. The dataset was extracted from a larger compilation called SongLyrics. The key elements featured in this dataset include an identifier, the actual song text, and a classification category.
