Pigs Bayesian Network Sample Two.

This Bayesian network repository reference is available online.

The Pigs Two dataset has four hundred forty-one nodes and five hundred ninety-two arcs. There are five thousand six hundred eighteen parameters in total. The average Markov blanket size is approximately three point six six, while the average degree is approximately two point six eight. The maximum in-degree is two.

Some notable features include medical test results, disease indicators, and other health-related metrics.

The dataset showcases a comprehensive representation of pig health and medical data, providing valuable insights into disease diagnosis and treatment.
