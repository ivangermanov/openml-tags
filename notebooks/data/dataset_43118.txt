Red Wine 
Tags: Red 
Features: Citric Acid, Sulphates, Alcohol, Type 

Red wine is a type of wine that is characterized by its red color and distinct features. The citric acid in red wine gives it a tangy taste, while the sulphates add a crisp and refreshing quality. The alcohol content in red wine is also notable, making it a popular choice for many wine enthusiasts. Furthermore, the type of red wine can vary greatly, with different varieties offering unique flavor profiles and aromas.
