Article Influence

Tags: Estimated Article Influence Scores

Features: Journal Name, ISSN, Citation Count Sum, Paper Count Sum, Average Citations per Paper, Projected Article Influence, Projected Article Influence Year

This dataset provides an estimation of article influence scores, which is a crucial metric in evaluating the impact of academic research. The features included in this dataset offer a comprehensive overview of a journal's performance, including its name, ISSN, and various citation metrics. The citation count sum and paper count sum provide a quantitative assessment of a journal's influence, while the average citations per paper offer a more nuanced understanding of a journal's impact. Additionally, the projected article influence and projected article influence year features provide a forward-looking perspective on a journal's potential influence.

Keywords: article influence, citation metrics, journal performance, academic research, impact evaluation
