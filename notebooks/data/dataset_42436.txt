Titanic Survival Prediction 

Tags: Health, Medicine 

This is the Titanic Survival Prediction dataset, which focuses on predicting the likelihood of survival based on various characteristics of passengers. The dataset features include whether the passenger survived, their social class, gender, age, ticket fare, port of embarkation, number of relatives accompanying them, and their title.

Keywords: Titanic, Survival, Health, Medicine, Passengers, Characteristics
