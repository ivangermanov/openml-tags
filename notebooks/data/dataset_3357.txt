Diabetes Dataset Tags: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Diabetes 

This dataset contains various features related to diabetes, including the number of times pregnant, plasma glucose concentration, diastolic blood pressure, triceps skin fold thickness, two hour serum insulin, body mass index, diabetes pedigree function, and age. The class feature indicates whether the individual has been diagnosed with diabetes or not.

Keywords: Diabetes, Pregnancies, Glucose, Blood Pressure, Insulin, Body Mass Index, Pedigree Function, Age
