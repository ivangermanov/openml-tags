Name: Forex Eur/Nok Day Close

Tags: Computational Universe, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Day, Machine Learning

**Source**: Dukascopy Historical Data Feed

Data Description:
This dataset contains historical price data of the Forex Euro versus Norwegian Krone from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one day. The entire dataset spans a specific period and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is not active on those days. The timestamp feature corresponds to the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.

The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean close price of the following day relative to the mean close price of the current minute. Specifically, the class attribute is true if the mean close price increases the following day, and false otherwise.

Attributes:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point in the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the start of this time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* Bid Volume: The frequency of changes in the bid price within this time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the start of this time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* Ask Volume: The frequency of changes in the ask price within this time interval.
* Class: Indicates whether the average price will increase during the next interval.
