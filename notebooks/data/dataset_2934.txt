Dataset Sales 

Tags: Economics, Statistics 

This dataset focuses on Sales and its various related factors. It comprises several features that provide insight into the sales trends and patterns. The features include Product ID, Machine ID, Temperature, Weather Condition ID, whether it was a Holiday, number of Days Off, Year, Month, Day, Week Day, Availability 0, Availability 1, Availability 2, Sales, and Standard Deviation.

Keywords: Sales, Economics, Statistics, Product, Machine, Weather, Holiday, Availability, Standard Deviation
