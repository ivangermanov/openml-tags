Law School Admission Binary Dataset 

Tags: Earth Science, Machine Learning 

This dataset represents a survey conducted among students attending law school in the United States. The data was obtained from the Fair ML package. 

The primary response variable in this dataset has been modified into a binary format, specifically focusing on whether an individual's undergraduate grade point average exceeds three. Additionally, the race variable has been categorized into two distinct labels: White and Non-White.

The dataset includes various features, including Age, Decile Rankings, Family Income, LSAT Scores, Undergraduate Grade Point Average, Gender, Race, Cluster, Full-Time Status, Bar Admission, and whether the Undergraduate Grade Point Average exceeds three.

Keywords: Law School Admission, Binary Dataset, Survey, Fair ML Package, Undergraduate Grade Point Average, Race Categorization.
