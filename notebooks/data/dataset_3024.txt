Titanic 

Tags: Life Science, Transportation 

The Titanic Survival Prediction dataset examines the factors affecting survival rates on the ill-fated ship. This dataset predicts whether a passenger survived based on various characteristics, including their social status, gender, age, ticket fare, embarkation location, number of relatives accompanying them, and title.

Features: 
Survival Status, Social Class, Gender, Age, Ticket Price, Port of Embarkation, Number of Relatives, Title

Keywords: Titanic, Survival Rate, Passenger Characteristics, Social Class, Demographics
