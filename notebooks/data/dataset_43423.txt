Nutritional Facts for Most Common Foods

Tags: Context

Everybody nowadays is mindful of what they eat. Counting calories and reducing fat intake is the number one advice given by all dieticians and nutritionists. Therefore, we need to know what foods are rich in what nutrients, don't we? It is essential to have a clear understanding of the nutritional content of various foods to make informed decisions about our diet.

The dataset contains a comprehensive list of foods, each with detailed information on the amount of Calories, Fats, Proteins, Saturated Fats, Carbohydrates, and Fibers. Additionally, the foods are categorized into various groups such as Desserts, Vegetables, Fruits, and more. It is worth noting that "trace" indicates that only a minuscule amount is available.
