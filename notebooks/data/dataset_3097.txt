The Train dataset is categorized under the Health and Medicine domain. This dataset consists of various features that describe the characteristics of passengers on a train. The features include Passenger Identity, Survival Status, Passenger Class, Full Name, Gender, Age, Number of Siblings or Spouses on Board, Number of Parents or Children on Board, Ticket Information, Fare Paid, Cabin Details, and Port of Embarkation.

Keywords: Train, Health, Medicine, Passengers, Characteristics
