Dataset Thirty-One Credit-G 
Tags: Computer Systems, Transportation 

This dataset contains various features related to credit applications, including Checking Status, Duration, Credit History, Purpose, Credit Amount, Savings Status, Employment, Installment Commitment, Personal Status, Other Parties, Residence Since, Property Magnitude, Age, Other Payment Plans, Housing, Existing Credits, Job, Number of Dependents, Own Telephone, and Foreign Worker, all of which contribute to the ultimate classification of the credit application.

Keywords: Credit Applications, Computer Systems, Transportation, Credit History, Classification.
