Crop Data: A Dataset for Applying ANOVA Analysis

This dataset, Crop Data, is tagged as a valuable resource for applying Analysis of Variance (ANOVA) analysis. The dataset features three key variables: Density, Block, and Fertilizer, which are related to the Yield of crops. By applying ANOVA analysis to these features, researchers can identify significant differences and relationships between these variables, ultimately shedding light on the factors that influence crop yield.

Keywords: Crop Data, ANOVA Analysis, Density, Block, Fertilizer, Yield
