Calculate Concrete Strength

Tags: Context

This dataset aims to provide a starting point for those who have just begun their journey with deep learning, serving as a benchmark for their progress. The feature set includes a comprehensive range of variables that affect the strength of concrete, including the amount of cement, blast furnace slag, fly ash, water, superplasticizer, coarse aggregate, fine aggregate, and the age of the concrete.

The target set is the strength of the cement, which is the ultimate goal of this dataset. The main objective is not only to achieve better results but to understand the process and learn from the journey, gaining valuable insights into the complex relationships between these variables.

Features: Cement, Blast Furnace Slag, Fly Ash, Water, Superplasticizer, Coarse Aggregate, Fine Aggregate, Age, Strength of the Cement

Keywords: Concrete Strength, Deep Learning, Benchmark, Cement, Aggregate, Fly Ash
