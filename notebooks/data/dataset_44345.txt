Credit Dataset Tags: Subsampling of the Credit Dataset with a random seed value. This dataset has been limited to a specific number of rows and columns, with a certain number of classes, and without stratification. The features of this dataset include Revolving Utilization of Unsecured Lines, Age, Number of Times Thirty to Fifty-Nine Days Past Due but Not Worse, Debt to Income Ratio, Monthly Income, Number of Open Credit Lines and Loans, Number of Times Ninety Days Late, Number of Real Estate Loans or Lines, Number of Times Sixty to Eighty-Nine Days Past Due but Not Worse, Number of Dependents, and Serious Delinquency in Two Years.
