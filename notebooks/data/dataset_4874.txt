Eleven Tumors Dataset
Tags: Microarray, Statistics

The Eleven Tumors dataset is a collection of molecular classification data for human carcinomas using gene expression signatures.

This dataset is based on research that aimed to classify human carcinomas by analyzing gene expression patterns. The study employed microarray technology to identify specific genetic markers associated with different types of cancer.

The dataset features a vast array of attributes, including numerous gene expressions that were analyzed to identify patterns and correlations with various types of cancer.

Please cite: Molecular Classification of Human Carcinomas by Use of Gene Expression Signatures, Cancer Research.

Keywords: Microarray, Gene Expression, Cancer Classification, Human Carcinomas
