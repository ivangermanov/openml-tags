Intersectional Bias Assessment Training Data Tags: This synthetic dataset contains demographic and clinical data used to train a classifier to predict a diagnosis of mental health conditions, specifically schizophrenia or depression. This dataset is used in the tutorial An Intersectional Approach to Model Construction and Evaluation in Mental Health Care. The dataset features include diagnosis, sex, race, housing status, delay in seeking treatment, anhedonia symptoms, depressed mood, sleep patterns, tiredness, appetite changes, rumination, concentration difficulties, psychomotor symptoms, delusions, suspicious behavior, withdrawal symptoms, passive behavior, tension, and unusual thought patterns.

Keywords: mental health, diagnosis, demographics, clinical data, intersectional approach, bias assessment, training data.
