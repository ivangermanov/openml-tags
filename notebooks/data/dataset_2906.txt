Synthetic two-dimensional data with vectors and Gaussian clusters featuring varying degrees of cluster overlap.

This dataset consists of five thousand vectors divided into fifteen distinct Gaussian clusters, each exhibiting unique characteristics and levels of overlap with neighboring clusters.

The Iterative Shrinking Method dataset is categorized under Machine Learning and Statistics.
