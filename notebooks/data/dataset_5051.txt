Munin Bayesian Network. Sample 0.

The Munin dataset is a discrete massive Bayesian network repository referenced on bnlearn. This dataset has no number of nodes, arcs, or parameters. The average Markov blanket size and average degree are not specified. 

The Munin Bayesian Network is an expert EMG assistant used in computer-aided electromyography and expert systems. 

Features include Left Deltoid Quality Motor Unit Potential Duration, Right Anterior Pubic Bone Quality Motor Unit Polyphasic, Left Medial Deltoid Two Compound Muscle Action Potential Waveform Duration, and many others related to electromyography and muscle function.
