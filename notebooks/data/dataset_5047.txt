**Pigs Bayesian Network. Sample Seven**

This Bayesian network repository reference is listed under discrete very large datasets.

The Pigs dataset consists of four hundred forty-one nodes and five hundred ninety-two arcs, resulting in a total of five thousand six hundred eighteen parameters.

Upon examination, the average Markov blanket size is approximately three point sixty-six, while the average degree is two point sixty-eight.

Notably, the maximum in-degree is two.

Features of this dataset include various attributes related to astronomy and image analysis.
