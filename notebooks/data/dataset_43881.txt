AAPL Stock Price 2021-2022 Dataset Tags: Apple Stock Price

This dataset contains the Apple stock price of each trading day since January First, Two Thousand and Twenty-One. It provides a comprehensive overview of the stock's performance, including the highest price, lowest price, open, close, volume, and adjusted close.

The dataset features include the Date, Open, High, Low, Close, Adjusted Close, and Volume.

Keywords: Apple Stock, Trading Days, Stock Performance, Highest Price, Lowest Price
