Name: Forex Eur Huf Hour Close

Tags: Economics, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Hour, Manufacturing

Data Description:
This is the historical price data of the Forex Eur Huf from Dukascopy. One instance, or row, represents one candlestick of one hour. The entire dataset spans a specific date range and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is not traded on weekends. The timezone of the feature Timestamp is Europe Amsterdam.

The class attribute is determined by the direction of the mean of the Close Bid and the Close Ask of the following hour, relative to the Close Bid and Close Ask mean of the current minute. In other words, the class attribute is true when the mean Close price is expected to increase during the following hour, and false when the mean Close price is expected to decrease or remain unchanged.

Attributes:
Timestamp: The time of the current data point in Europe Amsterdam timezone.
Bid Open: The bid price at the start of this time interval.
Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
Bid Close: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
Bid Volume: The number of times the bid price changed within this time interval.
Ask Open: The ask price at the start of this time interval.
Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
Ask Close: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
Ask Volume: The number of times the ask price changed within this time interval.
Class: Whether the average price will increase during the next interval.

Keywords: Forex, Eur Huf, Historical Data, Candlestick, Class Attribute, Mean Close Price.
