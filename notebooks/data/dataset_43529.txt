Chess Games of Woman Grandmasters (2009-2021)
Tags: Chess

A lot has changed in the world of chess over the years, particularly with the advent of computer engines and artificial intelligence algorithms. However, it is intriguing to explore how human performance has evolved alongside the rise of technology.

Becoming a Woman Grandmaster is an extraordinary achievement, as it is the highest-ranking chess title bestowed upon women. This elite group of strong and accomplished individuals has demonstrated exceptional skill and dedication to the game.

The dataset comprises all games played by Woman Grandmasters on chess.com from September 2009 onwards. The game moves are available in the standard Portable Game Notation format, accompanied by metadata information about the game, players, results, and ratings.

The dataset features include game identification, game URL, Portable Game Notation, time control, end time, rated games, time class, rules, Woman Grandmaster username, white player username, white player rating, white player result, black player username, black player rating, and black player result.

Acknowledgments are due to chess.com for making these games publicly available.
