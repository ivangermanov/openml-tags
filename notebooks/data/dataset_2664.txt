**FOREX eur pln minute Close**

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Social Media, Text and Literature

This is the historical price data of the Forex Euro-Polish Zloty pair from Dukascopy. Each instance, or row, represents one candlestick of one minute. The entire dataset spans a specific date range, excluding weekends since the Forex market is not active on those days. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute is determined by the direction of the mean of the Close Bid and the Close Ask prices of the following minute, relative to the mean Close Bid and Close Ask prices of the current minute. In other words, the class attribute is true when the mean Close price increases in the following minute, and false when the mean Close price decreases or remains the same.

Attributes:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point, which is set to the Europe Amsterdam time zone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the beginning of this time interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* Bid Volume: The number of times the bid price changed within this time interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the beginning of this time interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* Ask Volume: The number of times the ask price changed within this time interval.
* Class: Whether the average price will increase during the next interval.
