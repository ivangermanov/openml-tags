Employee Salaries

Tags: Ethical Issues

Annual salary information, including gross pay and overtime pay, for all active, permanent employees of Montgomery County, Maryland, paid in the calendar year. This information will be published annually each year to ensure transparency and accountability in the county's employment practices.

The dataset features a range of information about each employee, including their full name, gender, current annual salary, gross pay received, overtime pay, department, department name, division, assignment category, employee position title, underfilled job title, date first hired, and year first hired.

Keywords: employee salaries, Montgomery County, Maryland, transparency, accountability, employment practices.
