It's Halloween Again Dear Data Enthusiasts! 

And what better way of celebrating this spooky occasion than delving into the realm of Natural Language Processing! The Popular Halloween Costumes 2020 Dataset brings you an extensive collection of reviews of popular Halloween costumes sold on Amazon. 

The dataset comprises a vast array of popular costumes sourced from the Amazon website, complete with user review texts, including the review title and corresponding review score. Additionally, you will find the publishing date and location of each review. 

Notably, the data has not undergone any preprocessing, thereby presenting a fantastic opportunity for aspiring data scientists to hone their skills in text preprocessing and feature extraction techniques.

Features include review text, publication date, review title, rating, and product name.

Keywords: Halloween, Costumes, Amazon, Reviews, NLP, Text Preprocessing, Feature Extraction
