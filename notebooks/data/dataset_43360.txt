Popular Halloween Costumes Amazon Reviews

It's that time of the year again, dear enthusiasts! And what better way to celebrate Halloween than by diving into some Natural Language Processing! This dataset brings you the reviews of popular Halloween costumes sold on Amazon. 

The dataset contains a collection of popular costumes from the Amazon website, complete with user review texts, including the review title and the review score. Additionally, you will find the publishing date and location. The data has not been preprocessed in any way, making it an excellent exercise for aspiring data scientists looking to hone their skills in text preprocessing and feature extraction.

The dataset features include the review text, date, title, rating, and product name.

Keywords: Halloween, costumes, Amazon, reviews, NLP, text preprocessing, feature extraction.
