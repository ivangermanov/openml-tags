Premier League with Topological Data Analysis Tags: Premier League matches with Topological Data Analysis features extracted.

This dataset contains features extracted from Premier League matches, including the home team's best attack, average attack, and standard deviation of attack, as well as the home team's best defense, average defense, and standard deviation of defense. Additionally, the dataset includes the home team's goalkeeper player one. 

The away team's features are also included, such as their average attack, standard deviation of attack, best attack, average defense, standard deviation of defense, and best defense, as well as the away team's goalkeeper player one. 

Furthermore, the dataset includes topological metrics such as bottleneck metric, wasserstein metric, landscape metric, betti metric, and heat metric. 

The label feature is also included in the dataset.

Keywords: Premier League, Topological Data Analysis, Football, Sports Analytics, Team Performance Metrics.
