House Sales 

Tags: Machine Learning, Statistics 

Dataset used in the Tabular Data Benchmark, transformed in the same way. This dataset belongs to the "Regression on Numerical Features" benchmark.

Original Description:

Date converted to Year, Month, Day numerics. This dataset contains House Sale Prices for King County, which includes Seattle. It includes Homes Sold between May and May, covering a period of one year. It contains Nineteen House Features plus the Price and the ID Columns, along with numerous Observations. It is a great dataset for Evaluating Simple Regression Models.

Features: Number of Bedrooms, Number of Bathrooms, Square Footage of Living Area, Square Footage of Lot Area, Grade, Square Footage Above Ground, Square Footage Below Ground, Year Built, Year Renovated, Latitude, Longitude, Average Square Footage of Living Area Fifteen, Average Square Footage of Lot Area Fifteen, Month of Sale, Day of Sale, Sale Price.

Keywords: House Sales, Machine Learning, Regression Model, Tabular Data, Numerical Features, King County, Seattle, Real Estate.
