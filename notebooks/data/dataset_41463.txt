The Sarcasm Detection Dataset consists of three attributes: Identification Number, Tweet Content, and Label Classification. This comprehensive collection comprises tweets categorized into two distinct groups: Non-Sarcastic and Sarcastic. The dataset focuses on the realm of Politics and Social Media, providing valuable insights into the nuances of online communication.

This extensive dataset encompasses a vast number of tweets, with a significant portion labeled as Non-Sarcastic and another substantial portion classified as Sarcastic. The primary objective of this dataset is to facilitate the development of sophisticated algorithms capable of accurately detecting sarcasm in social media posts, particularly within the context of political discussions.

Keywords: Sarcasm Detection, Political Tweets, Social Media Analysis, Online Communication, Sentiment Analysis
