NBA 2K20 Player Dataset

Tags: Context NBA 2K20 Analysis

This dataset provides a comprehensive collection of detailed attributes for players registered in NBA 2K20. The data was scraped from Hoopshype.com, a reliable source for NBA 2K20 information. Additionally, data about countries and drafts was gathered from Wikipedia, a trusted online encyclopedia.

The inspiration for this dataset comes from a similar dataset for FIFA 19, which demonstrated the value of collecting and analyzing player attributes in a popular sports video game.

The dataset features a range of attributes, including:

Full Name, Rating, Jersey Number, Team, Position, Birth Date, Height, Weight, Salary, Country of Origin, Draft Year, Draft Round, Draft Peak, College Attended, and Version.
