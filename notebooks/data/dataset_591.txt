Fri C Three Two Fifty Five Dataset 

Tags: Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting One, Study One, Study Fifteen, Study Twenty, Study Forty One, Study Seven, Study Eighty Eight 

This dataset is a binarized version of the original dataset. The numeric target feature has been converted into a two-class nominal target feature by calculating the mean and classifying all instances with a lower target value as positive 'Positive' and all others as negative 'Negative'. 

The features included in this dataset are ozone one, ozone two, ozone three, ozone four, and ozone five, along with the binary classification.
