The Analcatdata Asbestos Dataset 

This dataset is part of the Analcatdata collection, a compilation of datasets used in the book Analyzing Categorical Data. It is comprised of eighty-four datasets, each presented in two formats: comma-delimited ASCII and Microsoft Excel. 

The datasets may be utilized freely for scientific, educational, and non-commercial purposes, provided that suitable acknowledgement is given by citing the aforementioned reference. 

Further information regarding the book, including details on statistical software, is accessible at the website.
