Airlines Dataset Tags: Airlines

The task of this dataset is to predict whether a given flight will experience a delay, based on the information available at the time of its scheduled departure. 

The dataset includes a range of features that provide insight into the flight, including the specific flight details, the time of day it is scheduled to depart, the length of the flight, the airline operating the flight, the airport it is departing from, the airport it is arriving at, the day of the week, and the class of the flight.

Keywords: Airlines, Flight Delay, Prediction, Scheduled Departure, Flight Details
