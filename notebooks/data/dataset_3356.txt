Online Advertisement Clicking Rates 

This dataset focuses on online advertisement clicking rates, where the key performance metrics are Cost Per Click and Cost Per Thousand Impressions. The data features include Timestamp, which records the exact moment when an ad was clicked or displayed, Cost Per Click, which measures the price paid for each ad click, and Cost Per Thousand Impressions, which calculates the price paid for every one thousand times an ad is displayed.

Keywords: online advertisement, clicking rates, cost per click, cost per thousand impressions, life sciences, machine learning.
