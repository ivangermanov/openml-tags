The pwLinear Dataset: A Binarized Version for Myth-Busting and Study Purposes

Tags: Binarized, Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Study

This dataset is a binarized version of the original dataset, converted to facilitate easier analysis and study. The numeric target feature has been replaced with a two-class nominal target feature, where values below the mean are classified as Positive (P) and those above as Negative (N).

Features include various chemical and life science-related attributes.
