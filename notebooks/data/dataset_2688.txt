**FOREX cadjpy-hour-Close**

Tags: Computer Systems, Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Hour, Human Activities

**Data Description**
This dataset contains the historical price data of the Forex Canadian Dollar/Japanese Yen exchange rate from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one hour. The entire dataset spans a specific period, excluding weekends when the Forex market is closed.

The timestamp feature is based on the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute determines the direction of the mean close price of the following hour, relative to the mean close price of the current minute. Specifically, the class attribute is true when the mean close price increases in the subsequent hour, and false when it decreases or remains unchanged.

**Attributes**

* **Timestamp**: The time of the current data point, based on the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.
* **Bid Open**: The bid price at the beginning of this time interval.
* **Bid High**: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Low**: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Close**: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* **Bid Volume**: The number of times the bid price changed within this time interval.
* **Ask Open**: The ask price at the beginning of this time interval.
* **Ask High**: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Low**: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Close**: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* **Ask Volume**: The number of times the ask price changed within this time interval.
* **Class**: Whether the average price will increase during the next interval.
