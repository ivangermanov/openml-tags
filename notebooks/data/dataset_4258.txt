The Meta Album Plankton Dataset (Mini) is a comprehensive dataset created by researchers at the Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution. This dataset utilizes the Imaging FlowCytobot (IFCB) for data collection, a process thoroughly explained in a research paper.

Each image in the dataset contains one or multiple planktons, captured in a controlled environment with varying orientations based on the fluid flow and plankton size and shape. The preprocessed plankton dataset is derived from the original Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution Plankton dataset. Image preprocessing involves creating a background square image by duplicating or mirroring the original image's edges, applying a Gaussian kernel to blur the background, and finally placing the original plankton image centrally onto the new background. The resulting image is then resized to 128x128 pixels with anti-aliasing.
