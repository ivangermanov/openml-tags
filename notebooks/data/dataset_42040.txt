Iris Dataset Information 

Tags: Economics, Manufacturing 

The Iris dataset contains information about various characteristics of iris flowers, including their sepal length, which refers to the length of the green, leaf-like structure at the base of the flower, as well as their petal width, which is the width of the colorful part of the flower. 

Features: 
Sepal Length 
Petal Width 
Species 

Keywords: Iris, Flowers, Characteristics, Sepal, Petal, Species
