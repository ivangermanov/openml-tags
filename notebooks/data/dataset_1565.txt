The Heart Disease Reprocessed Hungarian Dataset is a collection of data related to chemistry and life sciences. This dataset is tagged under categories including chemistry, life science, and others.

This dataset is a reprocessed version of the original Hungarian dataset obtained from the University of California, Irvine (UCI) repository.

The dataset consists of various features, including several variables, which are essential for understanding heart disease. The class label is also included in the dataset.

Please cite the source of this dataset as the Hungarian Institute of Cardiology, Budapest.

Keywords: heart disease, chemistry, life science, Hungarian dataset, UCI repository.
