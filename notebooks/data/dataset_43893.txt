The dataset used in this code pattern is supplied by Kaggle and contains Human Resources analytics data of employees who stay and leave. The types of data include metrics such as Education Level, Job Satisfaction, and Commute Distance. The dataset was obtained from the IBM Employee Attrition dataset repository.

The dataset is available under the Open Dataset License and the Database Content License. It features a wide range of attributes, including Age, Attrition, Business Travel, Daily Rate, Department, Distance from Home, Education, Education Field, Employee Count, Employee Number, Environment Satisfaction, Gender, Hourly Rate, Job Involvement, Job Level, Job Role, Job Satisfaction, Marital Status, Monthly Income, Monthly Rate, Number of Companies Worked, Over Eighteen, Overtime, Percent Salary Hike, Performance Rating, Relationship Satisfaction, Standard Hours, Stock Option Level, Total Working Years, Training Times Last Year, Work Life Balance, Years at Company, Years in Current Role, Years Since Last Promotion, and Years with Current Manager.

Keywords: Employee Attrition, Human Resources, Job Satisfaction, Commute Distance, Education Level
