The dataset utilized in this code pattern is furnished by Kaggle and encompasses Human Resources analytics data of employees who choose to stay and those who decide to leave. This comprehensive dataset incorporates an array of metrics, including educational background, job satisfaction levels, and commuting distances.

The dataset was obtained from the GitHub repository, specifically from the IBM employee attrition AIF360 dataset, which is readily available under the Open Dataset License and the Database Content License.

This extensive dataset comprises an assortment of features, including age, attrition status, business travel frequency, daily rate of pay, departmental affiliation, distance from home, educational background, field of education, employee count, employee identification number, environmental satisfaction, gender, hourly rate of pay, job involvement, job level, job role, job satisfaction, marital status, monthly income, monthly rate of pay, number of companies worked for, whether the individual is over eighteen years old, overtime work, percentage salary hike, performance rating, relationship satisfaction, standard working hours, stock option level, total years of work experience, training times last year, work-life balance, years spent at the company, years in current role, years since last promotion, and years with current manager.
