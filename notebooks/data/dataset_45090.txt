The Leukemia Dataset 

Tags: Life Science, Medicine, Microarray 

This dataset is a molecular classification of cancer, focusing on class discovery and class prediction through gene expression monitoring. 

Features include various genes and their corresponding expressions, such as BioB, BioC, BioDn, CreX, Alu, DapX, LysX, PheX, ThrX, TrpnX, ISGF, RGE, GAPDH, TFRR, and many others.

This dataset provides a comprehensive analysis of gene expression in leukemia patients, allowing for the identification of specific genetic markers associated with different types of leukemia.

Keywords: Leukemia, Gene Expression, Cancer Classification, Microarray Analysis
