This data set contains details of a bank's customers and the target variable is a binary variable reflecting the fact whether the customer has chosen to continue their relationship with the bank or has decided to close their account. 

Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/Churn Modelling

Features: Credit Score, Years of Age, Account Balance, Estimated Annual Salary, Country of Residence, Active Membership Status, Length of Time as a Customer, Gender, Possession of a Credit Card, Number of Banking Products, Customer Status

Keywords: Bank Customers, Customer Churn, Banking Data, Account Closure, Customer Relationship
