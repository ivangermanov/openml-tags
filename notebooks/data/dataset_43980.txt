Wine 

Tags: Machine Learning, Statistics 

This dataset is part of the tabular data benchmark and has been transformed in the same manner. It falls under the category of classification on numerical features benchmark.

The original source of this dataset is the UCI Machine Learning Repository, specifically the Wine Quality dataset.

This dataset consists of various characteristics of wine, including its chemical composition and physical properties, which are used to predict its overall quality.

Features include acidity levels, sugar content, mineral composition, and other physical and chemical properties of the wine.
