Weather Istanbul Data 

Tags: Health, Medicine 

This dataset contains daily weather information for Istanbul, including daily weather conditions, rainfall, sunrise and sunset times, moonrise and moonset times, average wind speed, average humidity, and average pressure.

The data was collected from the World Weather Online website, which provides daily observations on various weather parameters. The inspiration behind this dataset is to estimate tomorrow's weather using a binary classification method.

Features include Date and Time, Weather Conditions, Rainfall, Maximum Temperature, Minimum Temperature, Sunrise Time, Sunset Time, Moonrise Time, Moonset Time, Average Wind Speed, Average Humidity, and Average Pressure.

Keywords: Weather Forecasting, Climate Data, Binary Classification, Health Impacts
