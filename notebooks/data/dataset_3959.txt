Music Dataset: 1950 to 2019 
Tags: Context 

This dataset provides an extensive list of song lyrics from 1950 to 2019, accompanied by descriptive music metadata including measures of sadness, danceability, loudness, acousticness, and more. Additionally, the dataset includes lyrics that can be utilized for Natural Language Processing purposes.

The dataset offers valuable insights into various aspects of music, including the emotional tone conveyed through lyrics, the tempo and rhythm of songs, and the overall mood evoked by different genres.

Features:
Artist Name, Track Name, Release Date, Genre, Lyrics, 
Romantic, Communication, Obscene, Music, Movement/Places, 
Light/Visual Perceptions, Family/Spiritual, Like/Girls, Sadness, 
Feelings, Danceability, Loudness, Acousticness, Instrumentalness, 
Valence, Energy, Topic, Age

Keywords: Music Dataset, Song Lyrics, Natural Language Processing, Music Metadata, Emotional Tone, Genres.
