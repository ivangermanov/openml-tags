Health and Medicine Dataset 

This dataset is a comprehensive collection of data related to health and medicine. It encompasses various aspects of healthcare, including diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases. The dataset provides valuable insights into the field of medicine, covering topics such as symptoms, medications, medical procedures, and patient outcomes.

The dataset includes a wide range of features that provide detailed information about healthcare. These features are essential for developing predictive models, identifying trends, and making informed decisions in healthcare.
