The Stock Prices Dataset of a Ticker is a Good Start to Slice and Dice and Ideal for Forecasting of the Stock Prices. The Google Ticker Data is taken from the Google Stock Data for Forecasting Stock Prices.

The dataset comprises of the following columns, with each row having the day's data, and an entire year's data is pulled into a CSV file.

Date
Open
High
Low
Close
Volume

The data is pulled from the Google Stock Data for forecasting stock prices.
