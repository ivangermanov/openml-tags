NSE Stocks Data
Tags: Context

The data is of the National Stock Exchange of India. It has been compiled to facilitate machine learning, without relying heavily on stock application programming interfaces.

Content
The data consists of the National Stock Exchange of India's stock listings for each trading day of two years. A brief description of the columns is as follows:

Symbol refers to the symbol of the listed company.
Series denotes the series of the equity, with values including Equity, Debt, and others.
Open represents the opening market price of the equity symbol on the given date.
High signifies the highest market price of the equity symbol on the date.
Low indicates the lowest recorded market price of the equity symbol on the date.
Close refers to the closing recorded price of the equity symbol on the date.
Last represents the last traded price of the equity symbol on the date.
Prev Close stands for the previous day closing price of the equity symbol on the date.
Tot Trd Qty indicates the total traded quantity of the equity symbol on the date.
Tot Trd Val denotes the total traded volume of the equity symbol on the date.
Time Stamp refers to the date of the record.
Total Trades signifies the total trades executed on the day.
ISIN stands for International Securities Identification Number.
