NSE Stocks Data
Tags: Context

The data is of the National Stock Exchange of India. The data is compiled to facilitate Machine Learning, without bothering much about Stock Application Programming Interfaces.

Content
The data is of the National Stock Exchange of India's stock listings for each trading day. A brief description of columns is provided below.

The Symbol column represents the symbol of the listed company. The Series column represents the series of the equity, with values including Equity, Bonds, and others. The Open column represents the opening market price of the equity symbol on the date. The High column represents the highest market price of the equity symbol on the date. The Low column represents the lowest recorded market price of the equity symbol on the date. The Close column represents the closing recorded price of the equity symbol on the date. The Last column represents the last traded price of the equity symbol on the date. The Prev Close column represents the previous day closing price of the equity symbol on the date. The Total Traded Quantity column represents the total traded quantity of the equity symbol on the date. The Total Traded Value column represents the total traded volume of the equity symbol on the date. The Time Stamp column represents the date of record. The Total Trades column represents the total trades executed on the day. The International Securities Identification Number column represents the unique identification number of the equity symbol.
