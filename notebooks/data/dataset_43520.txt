The BSE Sensex Dataset

Context:
The BSE Sensex, also known as the S&P Bombay Stock Exchange Sensitive Index, is a free-float market-weighted stock market index of thirty well-established and financially sound companies listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange. These thirty constituent companies, which are some of the largest and most actively traded stocks, are representative of various industrial sectors of the Indian economy.

Content:
The dataset includes a few fundamental statistics of the Sensex. Can a screener be developed to select investment-worthy opportunities? Can any trends be identified over the last five years? What other fundamental indicators would be useful to examine? 

Features:
Date, Opening Price, Highest Price, Lowest Price, Closing Price

Keywords: BSE Sensex, stock market index, Bombay Stock Exchange, Indian economy, investment opportunities, fundamental statistics.
