Students Scores 

Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning 

This is a dataset about the academic performance of students in different subjects. It provides information about how well students perform in various areas of their studies. The data includes details about the students themselves, such as their gender and racial or ethnic background. It also looks at factors that might affect how well they do in school, including their parents' level of education and whether they had access to a special course to help them prepare for tests.

The dataset contains several key pieces of information, including the scores students achieved in math, reading, and writing.
