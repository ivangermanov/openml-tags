Realestatevaluation 

Tags: Machine Learning, Mathematics 

The Realestatevaluation dataset consists of several key features that influence the House Price of Unit Area. One of these features is the Transaction Date, which represents when the property was last sold or transacted. Another important feature is the House Age, which indicates how old the property is. The Distance to the Nearest MRT Station is also a significant factor, as proximity to public transportation can greatly impact property value. Additionally, the Number of Convenience Stores in the surrounding area can affect desirability and thus, property price. Furthermore, Geographic Coordinates such as Latitude and Longitude provide valuable information about the location of the property.

Keywords: Realestatevaluation, Machine Learning, Mathematics, House Price, Property Value, Geographic Coordinates
