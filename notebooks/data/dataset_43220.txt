Realestatevaluation Dataset Tags: Realestatevaluation

Features: 
This dataset does not include a transaction date feature. 
The house age feature is included, which is the age of the house in question. 
The distance to the nearest Mass Rapid Transit station feature is also included, which is the proximity of the house to the nearest public transportation hub. 
The number of convenience stores feature is included, which is the count of convenience stores in the surrounding area. 
The latitude and longitude features are included, which are the geographic coordinates of the house location. 
The house price of unit area feature is the target variable, which is the price of the house per unit area.

Keywords: Real Estate, House Age, Distance to MRT, Convenience Stores, Latitude, Longitude, House Price.
