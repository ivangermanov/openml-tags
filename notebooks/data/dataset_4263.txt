**Meta-Album Omni Print MD Mix Dataset (Mini)**

The Omni Print MD Mix dataset consists of synthesized images from seven hundred six categories. The images are generated using the Omni Print synthesizer, and no further processing was done. The Omni Print synthesis parameters include font size, image size, strength of random perspective transformation, margins, strength of pre-rasterization elastic transformation, random translation, rotation, horizontal shear, brightness, contrast, and color enhancement.
