w2a Dataset: A Collection of Chemical Compounds for Life Science Research 

Tags: Chemistry, Life Science 

This dataset is sourced from the LIBSVM data repository and is intended for binary classification tasks. It comprises a vast array of chemical compounds, each represented by a unique set of attributes that describe their molecular structure and properties.

The dataset contains an extensive list of attributes, including molecular descriptors, physiochemical properties, and other relevant characteristics that influence the behavior and interactions of these compounds.

**Please cite:** This dataset is sourced from the LIBSVM data repository.

Keywords: Chemical Compounds, Life Science, Binary Classification, Molecular Structure, Physiochemical Properties.
