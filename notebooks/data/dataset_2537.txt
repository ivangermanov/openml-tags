The dataset freMTPL Two Frequency contains risk features for motor third-party liability policies observed over a period of time, typically one year. This dataset provides valuable insights into various factors affecting insurance claims and is associated with Computational Actuarial Science.

The dataset includes a range of features, including policy identification, number of claims, exposure, area, vehicle power, vehicle age, driver age, bonus-malus, vehicle brand, type of vehicle gas, density, and region.

This comprehensive dataset is a valuable resource for researchers and professionals working in life science and machine learning, particularly in the field of actuarial science.

Keywords: motor third-party liability, insurance claims, actuarial science, life science, machine learning.
