Digital Colposcopy Green Dataset Information 

The Digital Colposcopy Green dataset was acquired and annotated by professional physicians. The subjective judgments, which were originally done in an ordinal manner, were discretized into two classes. Images were randomly sampled from the original colposcopic sequences. The original images and the manual segmentations are included in the images directory. 

This dataset has three modalities: Hinselmann, Green, and Schiller. The target variables are expert assessments and consensus. 

Features of this dataset include Cervix Area, External Os Area, Vaginal Walls Area, Speculum Area, Artifacts Area, Cervix Artifacts Area, External Os Artifacts Area, Vaginal Walls Artifacts Area, Speculum Artifacts Area, Cervix Specularities Area, External Os Specularities Area, Vaginal Walls Specularities Area, Speculum Specularities Area, Total Specularities Area, Maximum Area Differences Between Four Cervix Quadrants, Average Color Information in the Cervix, Standard Deviation of Color Information in the Cervix, and many others.

This dataset does not have missing values. 

Keywords: Digital Colposcopy, Green Modality, Cervix Area, Expert Assessments, Consensus, Color Information, Specularities Area.
