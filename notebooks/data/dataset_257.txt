BNG Colic Dataset Tags: 

Features: The dataset includes information about whether surgery was performed, the age of the horse, its rectal temperature, pulse rate, respiratory rate, temperature of its extremities, peripheral pulse, condition of its mucous membranes, capillary refill time, level of pain, presence of peristalsis, degree of abdominal distension, use of a nasogastric tube, amount of nasogastric reflux, pH level of nasogastric reflux, results of rectal examination, abdominal condition, packed cell volume, total protein levels, appearance of abdominocentesis fluid, total protein levels in abdominocentesis fluid, outcome of the case, and type of surgical lesion.

Keywords: colic, horse, surgery, abdominal, temperature, pulse, respiratory rate.
