Real Ad Exchange Two 

Tags: Images, Life Science 

This online advertisement dataset focuses on clicking rates, where the key metrics are Cost Per Click and Cost Per Thousand Impressions. The dataset includes features such as Timestamp, which denotes the time at which an event occurred, Cost Per Click, which represents the amount spent every time an ad is clicked, and Cost Per Thousand Impressions, which measures the cost of displaying an ad one thousand times.

Keywords: online advertisement, clicking rates, cost per click, cost per thousand impressions, life science, images.
