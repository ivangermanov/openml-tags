This Credit Approval dataset concerns credit card applications. To maintain confidentiality, all attribute names and values have been replaced with symbolic representations. What makes this dataset fascinating is its diverse range of attributes, including continuous variables, nominal variables with limited values, and nominal variables with extensive values. Furthermore, the dataset contains some missing values, which adds an extra layer of complexity.

The dataset features a range of characteristics, although some of these remain symbolic and difficult to interpret.

This dataset falls under the categories of Life Science and Machine Learning.
