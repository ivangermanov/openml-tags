Wine Dataset Tags: These data are the results of a chemical analysis of wines grown in the same region in Italy, but derived from three different cultivars. The analysis determined the quantities of thirteen constituents found in each of the three types of wines.

The dataset consists of instances of wine samples, each described by thirteen attributes. The associated task is classification.

The source of the dataset is a reputable online repository.

The features of the dataset include the level of alcohol, the amount of malic acid, the quantity of ash, the alkalinity of ash, the amount of magnesium, the total phenols, flavanoids, non-flavanoid phenols, proanthocyanins, color intensity, hue, and proline. The class label is also included.

Keywords: wine, chemical analysis, classification, Italy, cultivars, constituents.
