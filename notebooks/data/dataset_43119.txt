White Wine 

Tags: White Wine 

Features: Citric Acid, Sulphates, Alcohol, Type 

This white wine is characterized by its citric acid content, which gives it a refreshing and tangy taste. The sulphates present in the wine also contribute to its crisp and clean finish. With a moderate level of alcohol, this wine is perfect for sipping on its own or pairing with a variety of dishes. The type of white wine is also an important feature, as it can range from dry and oaky to sweet and fruity.

Keywords: White Wine, Citric Acid, Sulphates, Refreshing, Tangy, Crisp, Clean Finish, Moderate Alcohol, Pairing Options.
