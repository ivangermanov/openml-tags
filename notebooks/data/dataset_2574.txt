New Fuel Car Dataset

Tags: Economics, Government 

This platform features a comprehensive set of attributes related to the performance and characteristics of new fuel cars. The dataset includes information on Temperature Sensitivity, Maximum Speed, Semi-autonomous Mode, Hours of Operation, Number of Cylinders, Transmission Type, Maximum Temperature, Minimum Temperature, Intermediate Temperature, Median Temperature, Maximum Velocity, Minimum Velocity, Intermediate Velocity, Median Velocity, Oil Consumption, Pressure, Weight, Delay, and Ground Temperature.

Keywords: New Fuel Cars, Performance, Characteristics, Economics, Government
