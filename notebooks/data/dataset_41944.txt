The Sick Dataset from the OpenCC18 with all categorical data label encoded so all data is numeric. This dataset features a range of attributes, including the patient's Age, Sex, and medical history, such as whether they are currently taking Thyroxine or Antithyroid Medication. Additionally, the dataset includes information on whether the patient is Pregnant, has undergone Thyroid Surgery, or received I131 Treatment. It also captures the patient's medical queries, including those related to Hypothyroidism, Hyperthyroidism, and Lithium use. Furthermore, the dataset includes information on the presence of Goitre, Tumor, Hypopituitary, and Psych conditions. The dataset also features laboratory test results, including measurements of TSH, T3, TT4, T4U, FTI, and TBG levels. Finally, the dataset includes information on the Referral Source and the patient's Class.

Keywords: Thyroid, Medical History, Laboratory Tests, Patient Data, Medical Conditions.
