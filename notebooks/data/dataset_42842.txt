Diau Dataset: Health and Medical Label Ranking 

This dataset focuses on health and medicine, providing valuable insights into various aspects of medical research and healthcare systems. The primary objective of this dataset is to facilitate the ranking of labels related to health and medicine, enabling researchers and practitioners to better understand and analyze complex medical data.

The Diau dataset comprises a comprehensive range of features, including essential characteristics and attributes relevant to health and medicine. These features provide a solid foundation for exploring and understanding the intricacies of medical research and healthcare systems.
