The GenBase dataset is a multi-label dataset that contains protein sequences which can be assigned to multiple classes of protein families. This dataset falls under the categories of health and medicine.

Features of this dataset include various proteins, each with their unique characteristics.

This dataset is particularly useful for research in the field of proteomics, where understanding the properties and functions of different proteins is crucial.

Keywords: GenBase, Protein Sequences, Multi-label Dataset, Proteomics, Health, Medicine
