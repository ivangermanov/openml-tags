This Dataset Contains All Premier League Matches, with Player Statistics taken from Sofifa, from the Period of Time Spanning Multiple Years.

Features of this dataset include the Date of each match, Home Team Goals, Away Team Goals, Home Team's Best Attacker, Home Team's Best Defender, Home Team's Average Attacking Ability, Home Team's Average Defensive Ability, Home Team's Standard Deviation of Attacking Ability, Home Team's Standard Deviation of Defensive Ability, Goalkeeper for the Home Team, Away Team's Average Attacking Ability, Away Team's Average Defensive Ability, Away Team's Standard Deviation of Attacking Ability, Away Team's Standard Deviation of Defensive Ability, Away Team's Best Attacker, Away Team's Best Defender, Goalkeeper for the Away Team.

Keywords: Premier League, Football, Match Data, Player Statistics, Sofifa.
