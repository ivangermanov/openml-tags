Intersectional Bias Assessment Training Data 

Tags: Machine Learning, Meteorology 

This synthetic dataset comprises demographic and clinical information utilized to educate a classifier to forecast a diagnosis of either schizophrenia or depression. The dataset serves as a vital component in the tutorial entitled "An Intersectional Approach to Model Construction and Evaluation in Mental Health Care". 

The dataset encompasses an array of essential features, including diagnosis, sex, race, housing conditions, delay, anhedonia, depressed mood, sleep patterns, fatigue, appetite, rumination, concentration, psychomotor abilities, delusions, suspiciousness, withdrawal tendencies, passive behavior, tension, and unusual thought processes.

Keywords: mental health care, machine learning, meteorology, intersectional approach, model construction, diagnosis
