Baseball Team Dataset

Tags: Sport, Study

This analysis describes and summarizes the relationships between Major League Baseball players' salaries and their performance on the field. The salary data was collected from Sports Illustrated, and the salaries of players not featured in the article are listed as Not Available. The 1986 and career statistics were gathered from The Baseball Encyclopedia Update, while the team attendance figures were obtained through personal correspondence with the Elias Sports Bureau.

The dataset includes information on each team's league, division, and position in the final league standings. It also provides details on each team's performance during the season, including the number of wins and losses, as well as attendance figures for both home and away games. Additionally, the average salary of each team's players in 1987 is included.

Keywords: Major League Baseball, Player Salaries, Performance Statistics, Team Attendance, League Standings
