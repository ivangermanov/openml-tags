National Longitudinal Survey Binary

Tags: National Longitudinal Survey Binarized

The National Longitudinal Survey Binary dataset is a collection of survey results from the US Bureau of Labor Statistics, designed to gather information on labor market activities and other life events of several groups. The dataset was obtained from the R-package fairml. The response variable has been transformed into a binary version, indicating whether the income in 1990 was greater than a certain threshold.

The dataset features a range of variables, including demographic characteristics such as age, race, and gender, as well as educational attainment, income, and employment information. Additionally, the dataset includes variables related to physical health, family size, and general well-being.

Keywords: National Longitudinal Survey, labor market, demographic characteristics, educational attainment, income, employment, physical health, family size, general well-being.
