National Longitudinal Survey Binary 

Tags: Machine Learning, Statistics 

The National Longitudinal Survey Binary dataset comprises survey results gathered by the US Bureau of Labor Statistics to collect information on labor market activities and other life events of various demographic groups. This dataset was obtained from the R package Fair ML. The response variable has been transformed into a binary format, specifically whether the individual's income exceeds a certain threshold.

Features included in this dataset are age, race, gender, educational level, income, marital status, physical characteristics such as height and weight, family size, general health, history of illegal activities, number of jobs held, job training, and occupation type.

Keywords: National Longitudinal Survey, Binary Classification, Labor Market, Demographics, Income Threshold.
