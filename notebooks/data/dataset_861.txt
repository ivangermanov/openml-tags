Auto93 Dataset 

Tags: Binarized, Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting One, Study One, Study One Two Three, Study Fifteen, Study Twenty, Study Forty-One 

This dataset is a binarized version of the original data set. It converts the numeric target feature to a two-class nominal target feature by computing the mean and classifying all instances with a lower target value as positive, denoted by 'P', and all others as negative, denoted by 'N'. The features included in this dataset are manufacturer information, vehicle type, city miles per gallon, highway miles per gallon, standard air bags, drive train type, number of cylinders, engine size, horsepower, revolutions per minute, engine revolutions per mile, availability of manual transmission, fuel tank capacity, passenger capacity, length, wheelbase, width, u-turn space, rear seat room, luggage capacity, weight, and whether the vehicle is domestic or not.

Keywords: Binarized dataset, Vehicle characteristics, Fuel efficiency, Safety features
