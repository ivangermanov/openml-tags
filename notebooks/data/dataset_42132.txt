Traffic Violations 
Tags: This dataset contains traffic violation information from all electronic traffic violations issued in the County.

The dataset provides details on traffic stops, including the Date of Stop and Time of Stop, along with the Agency and Subagency responsible for issuing the violation. The Description and Location of the violation are also included, with Latitude and Longitude coordinates providing an exact Geolocation.

Additional information includes whether an Accident occurred, if Seat Belts were worn, and if there was Personal Injury or Property Damage. The dataset also notes if the incident was Fatal, involved a Commercial License, Hazmat, or a Commercial Vehicle, and if Alcohol was a factor.

Furthermore, the dataset includes details on any Search Conducted, the Search Disposition, Search Outcome, and the reason for the Search, including the Reason for Stop and Arrest Reason. The State, Vehicle Type, Year, Make, Model, Color, and Violation Type are also recorded.

Other relevant information includes the Charge, Article, and whether the driver Contributed to the Accident. Demographic data includes the Race and Gender of the driver, their City and State of residence, and the State of issuance for their Driver's License. Finally, the type of Arrest made is noted.

Features: Date of Stop, Time of Stop, Agency, Subagency, Description, Location, Geolocation, Accident, Seat Belts, Personal Injury, Property Damage, Fatal, Commercial License, Hazmat, Commercial Vehicle, Alcohol, Work Zone, Search Conducted, Search Disposition, Search Outcome, Search Reason, Search Reason for Stop, Search Type, Search Arrest Reason, State, Vehicle Type, Year, Make, Model, Color, Violation Type, Charge, Article, Contributed to Accident, Race, Gender, Driver City, Driver State, DL State, Arrest Type 

Keywords: traffic violations, electronic tickets, law enforcement, accidents, searches, arrests, demographics
