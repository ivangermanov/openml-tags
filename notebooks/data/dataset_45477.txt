Timing Attack Dataset - Ten Micro Seconds Delay Created On September Eighth, Two Thousand Twenty-Two

Tags: Bleichenbacher Timing Attack

Attribute Descriptions:

The dataset contains attributes related to three types of packets: Change Cipher Spec, TLS Alert, and TCP Disconnect.

For each packet type, there are several attributes that describe various aspects of the packet. These include source port, destination port, sequence number, acknowledgment number, header length, flags, window size, checksum status, urgent pointer, options, and timestamp.

The packets are further divided into multiple categories based on their characteristics, such as whether they contain a correct PKCS#1 PMS message or not, whether they have a zero byte in the message, and so on.
