This Motor Third Party Liability Pricing dataset describes insurance policies and their corresponding claim counts.

The dataset provides information on one million insurance policies, offering valuable insights into various factors affecting claim counts.

Features of this comprehensive dataset include the policy year, town, driver age, car weight, car power, and car age, all of which contribute to understanding claim numbers.
