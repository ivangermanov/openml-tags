The Sleuth Ex Dataset 

This dataset belongs to the categories of Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Study, and has been binarized from its original version. The numeric target feature has been converted into a two-class nominal target feature by calculating the mean and subsequently categorizing all instances with a lower target value as Positive and all others as Negative.

Features of this dataset include Family Medical History, Tobacco Mouth Irritation Questionnaire, in addition to various age-related IQ measures.

Keywords: Dataset, Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Study, Binarization, Nominal Target Feature
