Foreign Exchange Rate Dataset
Tags: Machine Learning, Medicine 

The currency exchange rate is a crucial determinant of a country's relative level of economic health, playing an essential role in a country's level of trade, which is vital to most every free market economy in the world.

This dataset spans from January 3rd to February 28th, covering a period of twenty-six years. It is a raw dataset, with some missing values present.

Acknowledgments
This dataset was obtained from the International Monetary Fund (IMF) website. We extend our gratitude to those who contributed to obtaining and managing this dataset.
