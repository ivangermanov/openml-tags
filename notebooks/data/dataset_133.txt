This dataset, known as BNG (glass, nominal), is an artificial dataset focusing on glass properties and composition. It includes various features related to the chemical and physical characteristics of glass samples. The features encompass the refractive index, which is crucial for optical applications, as well as the presence and concentration of several elements commonly found in glass formulations. These elements include sodium, magnesium, aluminum, silicon, potassium, calcium, barium, and iron. Additionally, the dataset categorizes the glass samples by type, providing a comprehensive overview of different glass compositions and their properties.
