Sports Cars 

Tags: Machine Learning, Statistics 

The data set comprises information about the brand, type, and model of various cars. For each vehicle, there is detailed information about its technical specifications, including the cubic capacity, the maximum power of the engine, the maximum torque, the number of seats, the weight of the car, the maximum engine speed, the time taken to accelerate from zero to one hundred kilometers per hour, and the top speed. Additionally, the data includes a classification of whether the car is considered a sports car or not, along with a tau value.

This data set is based on an article titled "What is a Sports Car" and has been extended with additional vehicles compiled from online sources. 

Features: brand, type, model, cubic capacity, maximum power, maximum torque, number of seats, weight, maximum engine speed, acceleration time, top speed, sports car classification, tau value

Keywords: sports cars, machine learning, statistics, technical specifications, classification, acceleration, top speed.
