One of the datasets used in the book Analyzing Categorical Data. This dataset was sourced from StatLib. 

The dataset is part of the book Analyzing Categorical Data, which provides further details concerning the book, including information on statistical software, available at the website.

Features: 
Year, Train Kilometers, Percent Mark I, Accidents, Signal Passed At Danger Preventable, Other Preventable, Non Preventable, Year Grouped, Accidents Grouped, Signal Passed At Danger Grouped, Other Grouped, Non Grouped, Train Kilometers Grouped, Fatalities, Signal Passed At Danger Fatalities, Other Fatalities.

Keywords: categorical data, train accidents, preventable accidents, statistical analysis, data analysis.
