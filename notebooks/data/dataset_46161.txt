Name: FL Chain
Tags: Dataset FL Chain from R Package Survival

Features: 
The dataset contains information about the age of patients, their sex, the year in which the sample was taken, the FL Chain group they belong to, whether they have MGUS (Monoclonal Gammopathy of Undetermined Significance), the time period of interest, and the status of the patient.
