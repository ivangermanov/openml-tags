Dataset Information 
Additional Information 
Hourly Interstate 94 Westbound traffic volume for Minnesota Department of Transportation ATR station 301, roughly midway between Minneapolis and St Paul, Minnesota. 
Hourly weather features and holidays are included to show their impact on traffic volume. 
This dataset does not have any missing values.

Additional Variable Information 
Holiday: Categorical US National holidays plus regional holiday, Minnesota State Fair 
Temperature: Numeric Average temperature in Kelvin 
Rain: Numeric Amount in millimeters of rain that occurred in the hour 
Snow: Numeric Amount in millimeters of snow that occurred in the hour 
Clouds: Numeric Percentage of cloud cover 
Weather Main: Categorical Short textual description of the current weather 
Weather Description: Categorical Longer textual description of the current weather 
Date and Time: DateTime Hour of the data collected in local Central Standard Time 
Traffic Volume: Numeric Hourly I-94 ATR 301 reported westbound traffic volume

Keywords: stock market prediction, technical indicators, commodity prices, market indices, treasury bill rates, traffic volume, weather features, holidays.
