The Bank-Marketing dataset is a reduced version, comprising ten percent of the total examples, derived from the original Bank-Marketing dataset available on the University of California, Irvine (UCI) repository. This dataset is tagged under Chemistry, derived, Life Science, and other relevant categories.

The dataset features various attributes, including several variables, which are essential for understanding the application of data mining in bank direct marketing. The comprehensive list of features includes variables such as age, job, marital status, education, default, housing, loan, contact, month, day of week, duration, campaign, pdays, previous, poutcome, emp var rate, cons price idx, cons conf idx, euribor3m, nr employed, and a class variable.
