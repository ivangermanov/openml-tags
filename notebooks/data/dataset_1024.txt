BNG Wine Quality Dataset

Tags: Artificial Intelligence, BNG, Chemistry, Life Science

This dataset focuses on the wine quality, featuring various chemical properties that contribute to its overall quality. The properties included are Fixed Acidity, Volatile Acidity, Citric Acid, Residual Sugar, Chlorides, Free Sulfur Dioxide, Total Sulfur Dioxide, Density, pH, Sulphates, and Alcohol.

Keywords: wine quality, chemical properties, acidity, sulfur dioxide, pH
