The Iris dataset is categorized under the tags Chemistry, Life Science, Myth Busting, Study, and Trivial. This dataset has undergone conversion from its original form, where the multi-class target feature was transformed into a two-class nominal target feature. This conversion involved relabeling the majority class as Positive and all other classes as Negative.

Features of this dataset include Sepal Length, Sepal Width, Petal Length, and Petal Width.
