BNG Dataset Tags: Artificial, Biochemical Network Generator, Chemistry, Life Science 

This dataset is characterized by its unique features including cross-talk between multiple pathways, post-translational modifications, and feedback loops, among others. The Biochemical Network Generator is a powerful tool used to simulate complex biological systems, allowing researchers to model and analyze various chemical reactions and interactions within living organisms. With applications in both chemistry and life science, this dataset provides valuable insights into the intricacies of biological networks.
