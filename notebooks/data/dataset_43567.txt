Reliance Stock Data - July 2019 to July 2020 
Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning 

This dataset contains the values of the Stock of Amazon, covering the period from fifteen July two thousand nineteen to fifteen July two thousand twenty.

The dataset consists of seven distinct columns, including the date, the opening value of the stock, the closing value of the stock, volume, and several other essential pieces of information required for making predictions.

Acknowledgments: 
This data was scraped from Yahoo Finance, and users may also utilize their application programming interface to access this data.

Features: 
Date, Open, High, Low, Close, Adjusted Close, Volume
