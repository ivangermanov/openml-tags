Cloud Dataset 

Tags: Binarized, Chemistry, Life Science, Study 

This is a binarized version of the original dataset. The conversion process involved taking the numeric target feature and turning it into a two-class nominal target feature. This was achieved by calculating the mean and then categorizing all instances with a lower target value as positive, denoted by 'Positive', and all others as negative, denoted by 'Negative'. 

The dataset contains several key features, including Period, Seeded, Total Energy, Total Weight, Nitrogen Content, Sulfur Content, and Nitrogen Water Content.
