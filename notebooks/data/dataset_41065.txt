MNIST Rotation dataset is a variation of the MNIST dataset, featuring rotated MNIST digits. This dataset is tagged as rotated MNIST digits. 

The dataset contains a wide range of features, including multiple angles of rotation, showcasing the digits in various orientations. 

MNIST Rotation dataset is sourced from http://www.iro.umontreal.ca/~lisa/twiki/bin/view.cgi/Public/MnistVariations.

Keywords: Rotated MNIST, Digit Recognition, Image Classification, Variations of MNIST.
