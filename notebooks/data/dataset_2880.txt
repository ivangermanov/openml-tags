The Air Quality dataset contains daily air quality measurements in New York City, spanning from May to September. This collection of data showcases the intricacies of atmospheric chemistry during the warmer months. 

The dataset comprises various features, including ozone levels, solar radiation, wind patterns, temperature readings, month of measurement, and day of measurement.

Keywords: Air Quality, Atmospheric Chemistry, Daily Measurements, New York City, Warm Months, Ozone Levels, Solar Radiation
