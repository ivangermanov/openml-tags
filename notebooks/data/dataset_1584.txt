**W4A Dataset**

This dataset is categorized under Chemistry and Life Science.

The dataset was obtained from the LIBSVM data repository.

This dataset consists of various attributes, including but not limited to molecular properties and structural characteristics.

**Features:**

The dataset contains a wide range of features, including molecular descriptors, topological indices, and other chemical properties.

Some of the features include atom-based descriptors, bond-based descriptors, and functional group counts.

The dataset also includes features related to molecular shape, size, and electronegativity.

**Keywords:** LIBSVM, chemistry, life science, molecular properties, structural characteristics.
