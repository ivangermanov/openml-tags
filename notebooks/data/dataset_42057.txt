Name: Air Quality

Tags: Daily air quality measurements in New York from May to September

This dataset contains daily air quality measurements taken in New York during the summer months of May to September. The dataset features various parameters that affect air quality, including the level of ozone, the amount of solar radiation, wind speed, and temperature. Additionally, the dataset also includes information about the month and day of each measurement.

Keywords: air quality, New York, summer, ozone, solar radiation, wind speed, temperature
