Fri C0 Dataset: A Binarized Regression Problem

This dataset is a binarized version of the original data set. The numeric target feature has been converted into a two-class nominal target feature by computing the mean and classifying all instances with a lower target value as Positive and all others as Negative.

The dataset pertains to the fields of Chemistry and Life Science, and is relevant to studies on Mythbusting, specifically Studies One, Fifteen, Twenty, Forty-One, and Seven.

Features of this dataset include various chemical properties, although their exact nature is not specified.
