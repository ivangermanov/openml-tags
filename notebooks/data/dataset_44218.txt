Upstream Data from the Twin Archimedes Screw Hydro-Electric Generator on the River Thames at Caversham Weir, Reading, United Kingdom. This dataset focuses on Machine Learning and Meteorology. The dataset contains data collected from the upstream portion of the generator. 

The dataset features a time stamp and upstream readings.

Keywords: Hydroelectric, Archimedes Screw, River Thames, Caversham Weir, Reading, Machine Learning, Meteorology
