Cushing's Syndrome 

Data from this dataset has four columns, including the label of the patient, Tetrahydrocortisone, Pregnanetriol, and Type. The type of the last six patients should be regarded as unknown. 

Synthetic Two-Class Problem 

This dataset has two real-valued coordinates and a class which is either zero or one. 

Viruses 

This dataset is about sixty-one viruses with rod-shaped particles affecting various crops, including tobacco, tomato, cucumber, and others. There are eighteen measurements on each virus, including the number of amino acid residues per molecule of coat protein. 

Leptograpsus Crabs 

Data from this dataset is about the morphology of rock crabs of the genus Leptograpsus. There are fifty specimens of each sex of each of two color forms. 

Forensic Glass 

This example comes from forensic testing of glass collected. It is also contained in the UCI machine learning database collection. Data includes the refractive index, percentage weights of various oxides, and type. The type codes include window float glass, window non-float glass, vehicle glass, containers, tableware, and vehicle headlamp glass.

Diabetes in Pima Indians 

A population of women who were at least twenty-one years old, of Pima Indian heritage and living near Phoenix, Arizona, was tested for diabetes according to World Health Organization criteria. The data includes the number of pregnancies, plasma glucose concentration, diastolic blood pressure, triceps skin fold thickness, serum insulin, body mass index, diabetes pedigree function, and age in years.
