**FOREX EUR/AUD Minute High**

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex High, Forex Minute, Transportation

**Source**: Dukascopy Historical Data Feed

**Data Description**
This dataset contains the historical price data of the Forex EUR/AUD pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one minute duration. The entire dataset spans from January first to December thirteenth and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is not active during these periods. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute indicates the direction of the mean high price movement in the subsequent minute compared to the current minute. Specifically, the class attribute is true if the mean high price increases in the next minute, and false if it decreases or remains unchanged.

**Note**: This task is hypothetical and intended for scientific purposes only. Realistic trading strategies should be based on predictions of the Close attributes, which are also available.

**Attributes**

* **Timestamp**: The time of the current data point in the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.
* **Bid Open**: The bid price at the start of this time interval.
* **Bid High**: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Low**: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Close**: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* **Bid Volume**: The number of times the bid price changed within this time interval.
* **Ask Open**: The ask price at the start of this time interval.
* **Ask High**: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Low**: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Close**: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* **Ask Volume**: The number of times the ask price changed within this time interval.
* **Class**: Indicates whether the average high price will increase in the next time interval.
