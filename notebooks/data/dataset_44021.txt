Medical Charges

Tags: Dataset used in the tabular data benchmark, transformed in the same way. This dataset belongs to the "regression on numerical features" benchmark.

The Inpatient Utilization and Payment Public Use File provides information on inpatient discharges for Medicare fee-for-service beneficiaries. This file includes information on utilization, payment (total payment and Medicare payment), and hospital-specific charges for the thousands of US hospitals that receive Medicare Inpatient Prospective Payment System payments. The file is organized by hospital and Medicare Severity Diagnosis Related Group and covers a range of fiscal years.

Features: Total Discharges, Average Covered Charges, Average Medicare Payments, Average Total Payments

Keywords: Medicare, hospital charges, inpatient discharges, payment systems, medical utilization.
