Coronavirus News (COVID-19)

Tags: Context 

The World Health Organization declared the coronavirus outbreak a pandemic and a Public Health Emergency of International Concern. Evidence of local transmission of the disease has been found in many countries across all six World Health Organization regions.

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses that are known to cause illnesses ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome. A novel coronavirus was identified in Wuhan, China, which is a new coronavirus that had not been previously identified in humans. It has since spread globally, resulting in the coronavirus pandemic.

This new coronavirus has spread rapidly worldwide, infecting millions of people and causing widespread illness and death. The rapid spread of the virus has put a significant strain on healthcare systems and economies around the world.
