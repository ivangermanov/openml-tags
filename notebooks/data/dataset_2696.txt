**FOREX USDCAD Minute Close**

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Minute, Meteorology, Statistics

**Data Description**

This dataset consists of historical price data of the Forex USDCAD pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one candlestick of one minute duration. The entire dataset spans from January to December and excludes weekends, as the Forex market is not active during these periods. The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.

The class attribute denotes the direction of the mean close price between the current and subsequent minutes. Specifically, it indicates whether the mean close price is increasing or decreasing (or remains unchanged) in the following minute.

**Attributes**

* **Timestamp**: The time of the current data point in the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.
* **Bid Open**: The bid price at the start of this time interval.
* **Bid High**: The highest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Low**: The lowest bid price recorded during this time interval.
* **Bid Close**: The bid price at the end of this time interval.
* **Bid Volume**: The number of times the bid price changed within this time interval.
* **Ask Open**: The ask price at the start of this time interval.
* **Ask High**: The highest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Low**: The lowest ask price recorded during this time interval.
* **Ask Close**: The ask price at the end of this time interval.
* **Ask Volume**: The number of times the ask price changed within this time interval.
* **Class**: Indicates whether the average close price will increase or decrease (or remain unchanged) in the next interval.
