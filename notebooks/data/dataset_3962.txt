NSE Future and Options Dataset 

Tags: Machine Learning, Statistics 

This dataset is gathered from the National Stock Exchange of India website for the past three months. It is posted here so that individuals can analyze this data and gather meaningful insights from it. For instance, one could calculate the probability of a stock ending up at max pain with the help of open interest.

The dataset contains information on the stock symbol with which it is traded, expiry date, strike price, and the option pricing of the symbol at that strike price.

The authors would like to acknowledge the people working at the National Stock Exchange of India for publishing these reports every day.

Whenever we want to initiate an options trade, we look at various parameters like open interest, change in open interest, technical analysis indicators before deciding to buy or sell the option. Most times, we need to browse to multiple websites to gather the data we need. This dataset serves as an example of how one can customize the data according to their needs.

Features: Instrument, Symbol, Expiry Date, Strike Price, Option Type, Open, High, Low, Close, Settlement Price, Contracts, Value in Lakhs, Open Interest, Change in Open Interest, Timestamp.

Keywords: National Stock Exchange of India, options trade, open interest, technical analysis, machine learning, statistics.
