Wine Quality Dataset 

Tags: Wine Quality Data for Testing in Automated Machine Learning Benchmark 

Features of this dataset include Fixed Acidity, Volatile Acidity, Citric Acid, Residual Sugar, Chlorides, Free Sulfur Dioxide, Total Sulfur Dioxide, Density, pH, Sulphates, and Alcohol levels, all of which contribute to the overall Quality of the wine, along with a unique identifier.
