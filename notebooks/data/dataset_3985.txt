The IBM Employee Performance dataset used in this code pattern is supplied by Kaggle and contains Human Resources analytics data of employees who choose to stay with the company and those who decide to leave. This comprehensive dataset includes various metrics such as education levels, job satisfaction ratings, and commute distances.

The dataset was obtained from GitHub and is available under the Open Dataset License and the Database Content License.

This dataset encompasses a wide range of features including age, business travel frequency, daily rates, departmental affiliations, distance from home, educational backgrounds, fields of study, employee counts, employee identification numbers, environmental satisfaction ratings, gender, hourly rates, job involvement levels, job levels, job roles, job satisfaction ratings, marital statuses, monthly incomes, monthly rates, number of companies worked for, whether the individual is over eighteen years old, overtime frequencies, percentage salary hikes, performance ratings, relationship satisfaction ratings, standard working hours, stock option levels, total working years, training times in the last year, work-life balances, years spent at the company, years in current roles, years since last promotions, and years with current managers.
