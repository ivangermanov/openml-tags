Tamil Nadu Crop Production

Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning, Context Agriculture

This dataset describes the agriculture crops production in Tamil Nadu, India. It is sourced from data.gov.in and is fully licensed.

Acknowledgements
This dataset was compiled during an internship at Tact Labs.

The dataset provides information on various aspects of agriculture crop production in Tamil Nadu, including the state and district where the crops were produced, the year and season of production, and the type of crop grown. Additionally, it includes details on the area under cultivation and the total production.

Features:

* State Name
* District Name
* Crop Year
* Season
* Crop Type
* Cultivated Area

Target Variable: Production

Keywords: Agriculture, Crop Production, Tamil Nadu, India, Machine Learning, Computer Systems, Data Analysis.
