The Water Bayesian Network is a pilot project focused on creating an expert system for control of waste water treatment. It aims to provide a comprehensive model for managing wastewater treatment processes.

Features include various water quality indicators, such as carbon, nitrogen, and oxygen levels, as well as other relevant factors.
