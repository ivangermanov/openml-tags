Tags: Economics, Machine Learning 

Features: Focal Depth, Latitude, Longitude, Richter Scale 

This dataset pertains to the realm of economics and machine learning, encompassing various features that are essential for analysis. The focal depth feature provides insight into the distance of an earthquake's epicenter from the earth's surface. Additionally, the latitude and longitude features offer geographical coordinates, allowing for spatial analysis. Furthermore, the Richter scale feature measures the magnitude of earthquakes, providing valuable information for researchers and analysts. This comprehensive dataset serves as a valuable resource for those seeking to explore the intersection of economics and machine learning.
