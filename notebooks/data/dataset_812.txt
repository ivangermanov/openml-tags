Squash Stored Dataset 

Tags: Chemistry, Life Science, Mythbusting, Study 

This dataset is a binarized version of the original dataset. The multi-class target feature has been converted to a two-class nominal target feature by relabeling the majority class as Positive and all others as Negative. 

The dataset includes various features, such as Site, Days After Flowering, Fruit, Weight, Store Weight, Penetration, Solids, Brix, A Star, Early Growth Days Difference, Final Growth Days Difference, Ground Spot A Star, Glucose, Fructose, Sucrose, Total, Glucose plus Fructose, Starch, Sweetness, Flavor, Dry/Moist, Fiber, Heat Input Emergence, and Heat Input Flower.
