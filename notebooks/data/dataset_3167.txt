Online News Popularity Dataset Tags: Demographics, Economics

This dataset summarizes a heterogeneous set of features about articles published by Mashable over a period of two years, with the goal of predicting the number of shares in social networks, also known as popularity.

The articles were published by Mashable, and their content is owned by them. Therefore, this dataset does not share the original content, but rather provides some statistics associated with it. The original content can be publicly accessed and retrieved using the provided URLs.

Acquisition Date: January 8, 2015

The estimated relative performance values were estimated by the authors using a Random Forest classifier and a rolling window as an assessment method.

Attribute Information:

* URL of the Article: Each article's unique identifier.
* Time Delta: The days between the article publication and the dataset acquisition.
* Number of Words in the Title: The total count of words in the title of each article.
* Number of Words in the Content: The total count of words in the content of each article.
* Rate of Unique Words in the Content: The proportion of unique words in the content.
* Rate of Non-Stop Words in the Content: The proportion of non-stop words in the content.
* Rate of Unique Non-Stop Words in the Content: The proportion of unique non-stop words in the content.
* Number of Links: The total count of links in each article.
* Number of Links to Other Articles Published by Mashable: The total count of links to other articles published by Mashable.
* Number of Images: The total count of images in each article.
* Number of Videos: The total count of videos in each article.
* Average Length of Words in the Content: The average length of words in the content.
