Name: Foreign Exchange Dataset
Tags: Currency Trading

This dataset contains features related to foreign exchange market data, including:

Timestamp, representing the time at which the data was recorded
Bid Open, the opening price of a bid
Bid High, the highest price of a bid
Bid Low, the lowest price of a bid
Bid Close, the closing price of a bid
Bid Volume, the total volume of bids
Ask Open, the opening price of an ask
Ask High, the highest price of an ask
Ask Low, the lowest price of an ask
Ask Close, the closing price of an ask
Ask Volume, the total volume of asks
Class, representing the classification of the data

Keywords: Foreign Exchange, Currency Trading, Market Data, Bid, Ask, Volume, Classification
