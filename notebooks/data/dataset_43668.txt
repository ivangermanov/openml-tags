Computer Systems, Machine Learning Context 

Thousands of digital currencies have emerged in recent years. Can you forecast which one will be the next prominent digital currency, similar to Bitcoin?

The dataset contains daily opening prices, highest prices, lowest prices, closing prices, and trading volumes for more than a thousand digital currencies, excluding Bitcoin.

Acknowledgments 
https://timescaledata.blob.core.windows.net/datasets/cryptocurrency data archive

Inspiration 
Speculative market forces are continually influencing digital currency exchanges, but do these market forces possess any statistically significant characteristics?

Features 
Date and Time, Opening Price, Highest Price, Lowest Price, Closing Price, Trading Volume, Cryptocurrency Symbol

Keywords: Digital Currencies, Forecasting, Market Forces, Statistical Significance, Trading Volumes
