Cryptocurrencies

Thousands of digital currencies have emerged in recent years. Can you predict which one will be the next big thing, similar to Bitcoin?

The dataset contains daily opening prices, highest prices, lowest prices, closing prices, and trading volumes for over a thousand digital currencies, excluding Bitcoin.

Acknowledgements: The dataset is available at https://timescaledata.blob.core.windows.net/datasets/crypto_data.tar.gz

Inspiration: Speculative forces are always at work on digital currency exchanges, but do they contain any statistically significant patterns or features?

Features: Date and Time, Opening Price, Highest Price, Lowest Price, Closing Price, Trading Volume, Trading Volume in Bitcoin, Currency Symbol

Keywords: Digital Currencies, Bitcoin, Trading Volumes, Speculative Forces, Statistically Significant Patterns
