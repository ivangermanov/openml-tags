Stack Overflow Polarity Dataset 

Tags: Machine Learning, Sentiment Analysis, Statistics 

A gold standard dataset consisting of approximately four thousand four hundred questions, answers, and comments from Stack Overflow, which have been manually annotated for their respective polarities. This dataset has been utilized in the development of the Emotion Mining Toolkit for detecting polarity from technical texts. It comprises the following attributes: identification, sentiment orientation, and textual content.

Keywords: sentiment analysis, machine learning, statistics, polarity detection, technical texts, emotion mining
