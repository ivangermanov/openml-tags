**Meta-Album Omni Print MD Six Dataset (Micro)** 

The Omni Print MD Six dataset consists of images synthesized with Omni Print, without any further processing. The Omni Print synthesis parameters include font size, image size, strength of random perspective transformation, margins, strength of pre-rasterization elastic transformation, random translation, image blending method, rotation, and horizontal shear. Both foreground and background are images taken from a personal mobile phone.
