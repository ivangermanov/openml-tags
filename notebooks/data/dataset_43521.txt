Dril Tweets Tags

Context Scraped from Twitter January. Content Dataset contains the date of the tweet, the text of the tweet, and other statistics such as likes and retweets.

Acknowledgements Many thanks are due to Get Old Tweets for providing an excellent scraper.

Inspiration: As we grow from infancy to adulthood, our desires change. As an infant, I want my father. As a young adult, I want my information. Do you see how rough this is?

Features: identification, link, content, date, retweets, favorites, mentions, geographic location

Keywords: tweets, dataset, scraper, inspiration, growth, desires
