Drink Dataset 
Tags: Fake Dataset Without Any Value 

This dataset consists of various attributes related to beverages and their availability. 
The product feature provides information about the type of drink being referred to. 
Stock indicates whether the product is available for purchase. 
Price represents the cost of the item at a particular location. 
Place refers to the geographic location where the product can be found.
