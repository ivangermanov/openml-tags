The Bread Basket

Tags: Context

The dataset belongs to The Bread Basket, a bakery located in Edinburgh. This dataset contains a large number of entries, comprising numerous transactions, and is organized into four columns. 

Content

The dataset consists of transactions of customers who ordered various items from this bakery online. The time period of the data spans from a specific date in January to a specific date in December. 

Features

The dataset includes information about each transaction, the item purchased, the date and time of the transaction, and whether the transaction occurred on a weekday or weekend.

Keywords: bakery, Edinburgh, transactions, online orders, date range
