Slashdot Tags: 2019 Multi-Output Paper

The Slashdot dataset is a multi-label dataset for text classification, consisting of article titles and partial blurbs. The blurbs can be assigned to several categories based on word predictors, such as Science, News, and Games. This dataset features a wide range of topics, including Entertainment, Interviews, Main, Developers, Apache, News, Search, Mobile, Science, IT, BSD, Idle, Games, Your Rights Online, Ask Slashdot, Apple, Book Reviews, Hardware, Meta, Linux, Politics, Technology, and more.

The dataset is designed to classify article blurbs into multiple categories, allowing for a more nuanced understanding of the topics and themes present in the text. With a diverse range of categories, this dataset provides a comprehensive platform for text classification and analysis.

Keywords: Text Classification, Multi-Label Dataset, Article Blurbs, Word Predictors, Science, News, Games
