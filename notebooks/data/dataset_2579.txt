Diabetes Dataset

The Diabetes dataset consists of ten baseline variables and a quantitative measure of disease progression one year after baseline, obtained for four hundred forty-two diabetes patients. The dataset includes age, sex, body mass index, average blood pressure, and six blood serum measurements.

**Attribute Information:**
The attributes include:

* Age
* Sex
* Body Mass Index
* Average Blood Pressure
* Six blood serum measurements, which are:
    * Serum One
    * Serum Two
    * Serum Three
    * Serum Four
    * Serum Five
    * Serum Six

Each of these ten feature variables has been mean-centered and scaled by the standard deviation.
