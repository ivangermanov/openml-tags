This dataset combines information from airlines and adult demographics. It includes various flight-related features such as the airline, flight details, departure and arrival airports, day of the week, time, and flight length. Additionally, it incorporates adult demographic data including age, work class, education level, marital status, occupation, relationship status, race, sex, financial information, work hours, and country of origin. The dataset also includes a delay indicator for flights.
