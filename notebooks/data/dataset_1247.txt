Aloi Dataset: This dataset originates from the LIBSVM multiclass data repository. It belongs to the categories of Chemistry and Life Science, and is also classified under OpenML-Reg19.

The Aloi dataset contains a multitude of features, including attributes one through ninety-eight, which are essential for conducting experiments and analyzing results within the realms of chemistry and life science.

This dataset is part of the LIBSVM multiclass data repository, a comprehensive collection of datasets used for various research purposes.
