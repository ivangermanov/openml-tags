Name: Delays in Zurich Transport

Tags: Zurich Public Transport Delay Data

This dataset contains information on delays in Zurich's public transport system, specifically from October 30, 2016, to November 27, 2016. The data was cleaned and prepared on Open Data Day in 2017.

The dataset features a range of variables, including the delay itself, the type of vehicle involved, the line number, direction of travel, and the stop identification number. Additionally, the data includes information on the day of the week, time of day, and various weather conditions such as temperature, wind speed, precipitation, dew point, and humidity. The data is also categorized by hour and minute of the day.

Keywords: Zurich, public transport, delay data, weather conditions, transportation
