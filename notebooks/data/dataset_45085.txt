Breast Cancer Dataset

Tags: Demographics, Education, Microarray

This dataset is focused on breast cancer and its relation to demographics and education. It explores the use of microarray technology to analyze gene expression profiles and predict clinical outcomes.

Features:

* Various gene expressions (multiple contigs, accessions, and barcodes)

The dataset aims to provide insights into the molecular mechanisms underlying breast cancer and to identify potential biomarkers for diagnosis and treatment.

Keywords: breast cancer, demographics, education, microarray, gene expression, clinical outcomes
