Features: Show, Type, Revival, New York Times rating, Daily News rating, Tony awards, Tony nominations, Week one attendance, Show run, Binary Class

This dataset focuses on Broadway shows and their various attributes. It includes information about the type of show, whether it is a revival, ratings from major publications, awards and nominations received, initial attendance figures, and the overall run of the show. The data has been processed to create a binary classification, simplifying the original multi-class structure. This modification allows for easier analysis and comparison of Broadway productions based on their success metrics.

Keywords: Broadway, Theater, Ratings, Awards, Attendance, Binary Classification, Show Performance
