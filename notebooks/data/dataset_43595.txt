Among all industries, the insurance domain has the largest use of analytics and data science methods. This dataset provides a taste of working with datasets from insurance companies, including the challenges faced, strategies used, and variables that influence the outcome. This is a classification problem.

The company wants to automate the loan eligibility process in real-time based on customer details provided while filling out an online application form. These details include gender, marital status, education, number of dependents, income, loan amount, credit history, and others. To automate this process, the company has given the problem of identifying customer segments that are eligible for loan amounts so that they can specifically target these customers. They have provided a partial dataset.

The dataset features include loan identification, gender, marital status, number of dependents, education, self-employment status, applicant income, co-applicant income, loan amount, loan term, credit history, property area, and loan status.

Keywords: insurance, analytics, data science, loan eligibility, customer segmentation, credit history, loan automation.
