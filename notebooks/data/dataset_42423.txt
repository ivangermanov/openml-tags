SXDCDE Dataset Tags: Sexism and Discrimination

This dataset focuses on hate and transgender issues, exploring the complexities of discrimination and prejudice. It delves into the harmful effects of hate speech and its impact on marginalized communities, particularly those who identify as transgender. The dataset aims to raise awareness about the importance of promoting inclusivity, respect, and understanding towards all individuals, regardless of their gender identity or expression.

Keywords: Sexism, Discrimination, Hate Speech, Transgender Issues, Inclusivity, Respect.
