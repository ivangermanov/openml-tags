The New York Times Best Sellers dataset comprises a comprehensive collection of best seller lists published by The New York Times every Sunday, spanning an entire decade from January third two thousand ten to December twenty-ninth two thousand nineteen. Each week, five books are nominated as top performers for each category.

This dataset acknowledges the image URL courtesy of Sincerely Media on Unsplash.

The key characteristics of this dataset include the publication date, list name, title, description, Amazon product URL, and price, along with the number of weeks on the list.
