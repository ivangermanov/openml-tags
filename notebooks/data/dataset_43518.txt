The New York Times Best Sellers dataset contains the weekly Best Sellers List published by The New York Times every Sunday, spanning an entire decade from January three, two thousand and ten to December twenty-nine, two thousand and nineteen. Each week, five books are named as best sellers for each category. 

The dataset acknowledges the image URL from Sincerely Media on Unsplash. 

The dataset features include the publication date, list name, title, description, Amazon product URL, and price, as well as the number of weeks the book has been on the list.
