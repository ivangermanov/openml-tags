**Data Description**
This dataset contains house sale prices for King County, which includes Seattle. It includes homes sold between May 2014 and May 2015.

**Attribute Description**
The dataset includes twenty-two attributes that describe the characteristics of each house. 

The target feature is the price of the house. 

The number of bedrooms and bathrooms in each house is also included. 

The size of the living area and the lot are measured in square feet. 

The number of floors in each house is also recorded. 

A waterfront feature is binary, indicating whether the property has a waterfront or not. 

The view of the property is ranked on an index from zero to four, with four being the best view. 

The condition of the house is ranked from one to five, with five being the best condition. 

The grade of the house refers to the construction quality, with higher grades indicating better quality. 

The square feet above and below ground are also included. 

The year the house was built and renovated, if applicable, are recorded. 

The zip code, latitude, and longitude of each house are also included. 

The average size of interior housing living space and land lots for the closest fifteen houses are also included. 

The date sold is broken down into year, month, and day.

Features: price, number of bedrooms, number of bathrooms, size of living area, size of lot, number of floors, waterfront, view, condition, grade, square feet above ground, square feet below ground, year built, year renovated, zip code, latitude, longitude, average size of interior housing living space, average size of land lots, date sold year, date sold month, date sold day

Keywords: house sale prices, King County, Seattle, real estate, property characteristics
