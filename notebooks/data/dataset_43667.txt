Medium Articles Tags: Context

Medium is one of the most renowned platforms for disseminating knowledge on a wide range of subjects. It is extensively utilized to publish articles on Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science. This dataset is a compilation of approximately three hundred and fifty articles in these fields.

Content

The dataset comprises articles, their titles, the number of claps they have received, their links, and their reading time.

Acknowledgements

This dataset was scraped from Medium. A Python script was created to scrape all the required articles using their tags from Medium.

Inspiration

The dataset aims to provide insights into the art of writing a captivating article. It seeks to answer questions such as how to inform readers in an engaging manner, what type of title attracts a larger audience, and what is the ideal length of an article.

Features: Claps, Reading Time, Link, Title, Text
