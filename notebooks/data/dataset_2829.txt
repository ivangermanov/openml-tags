The Indian Liver Patient Dataset is a comprehensive collection of data categorized under the fields of Economics and Geography. This dataset originated from the OpenCC eighteen and has been modified to encode all categorical variables with labels.

The dataset comprises various features including several variables, ultimately leading to a classification outcome. These features have been carefully selected to provide valuable insights into the Indian liver patient demographics.

The Indian Liver Patient Dataset is an essential resource for researchers and analysts seeking to understand the complexities of liver disease in the Indian population.

Keywords: Indian Liver Patient Dataset, Economics, Geography, Categorical Variables, Label Encoding, Classification Outcome.
