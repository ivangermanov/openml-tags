**Chronic Kidney Disease Data Set**

This dataset can be used to predict chronic kidney disease and can be collected from a hospital over a period of nearly two months.

### Attribute Information

The dataset consists of twenty-four attributes plus a class attribute, totaling twenty-five attributes. These attributes can be categorized into eleven numeric and fourteen nominal attributes.

1. Age: The patient's age in years.
2. Blood Pressure: The patient's blood pressure in millimeters of mercury (mmHg).
3. Specific Gravity: A nominal attribute with values ranging from 1.005 to 1.025.
4. Albumin: A nominal attribute with values ranging from 0 to 5.
5. Sugar: A nominal attribute with values ranging from 0 to 5.
6. Red Blood Cells: A nominal attribute indicating whether the red blood cells are normal or abnormal.
7. Pus Cell: A nominal attribute indicating whether the pus cell is normal or abnormal.
8. Pus Cell Clumps: A nominal attribute indicating whether pus cell clumps are present or not.
9. Bacteria: A nominal attribute indicating whether bacteria are present or not.
10. Blood Glucose Random: The patient's blood glucose level in milligrams per deciliter (mg/dL).
11. Blood Urea: The patient's blood urea level in milligrams per deciliter (mg/dL).
12. Serum Creatinine: The patient's serum creatinine level in milligrams per deciliter (mg/dL).
13. Sodium: The patient's sodium level in milliequivalents per liter (mEq/L).
14. Potassium: The patient's potassium level in milliequivalents per liter (mEq/L).
15. Hemoglobin: The patient's hemoglobin level in grams.
16. Packed Cell Volume: The patient's packed cell volume.
17. White Blood Cell Count: The patient's white blood cell count in cells per cubic millimeter (cells/cumm).
18. Red Blood Cell Count: The patient's red blood cell count in millions per cubic millimeter (millions/cmm).
19. Hypertension: A nominal attribute indicating whether the patient has hypertension or not.
20. Diabetes Mellitus: A nominal attribute indicating whether the patient has diabetes mellitus or not.
21. Coronary Artery Disease: A nominal attribute indicating whether the patient has coronary artery disease or not.
22. Appetite: A nominal attribute indicating whether the patient's appetite is good or poor.
23. Pedal Edema: A nominal attribute indicating whether the patient has pedal edema or not.
24. Anemia: A nominal attribute indicating whether the patient has anemia or not.
25. Class: A nominal attribute indicating whether the patient has chronic kidney disease (ckd) or not (notckd).

**Keywords:** Chronic Kidney Disease, Blood Pressure, Blood Glucose, Serum Creatinine, Hemoglobin, Hypertension, Diabetes Mellitus, Coronary Artery Disease.
