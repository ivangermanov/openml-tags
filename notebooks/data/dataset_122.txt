BNG Colic Dataset 

Tags: Artificial

Features:

This dataset includes various characteristics related to equine colic, including the need for surgery, age of the horse, rectal temperature, pulse rate, respiratory rate, temperature of extremities, presence of peripheral pulse, condition of mucous membranes, capillary refill time, level of pain, presence of peristalsis, degree of abdominal distension, use of nasogastric tube, amount of nasogastric reflux, pH level of nasogastric reflux, results of rectal examination, abdominal appearance, packed cell volume, total protein levels, appearance of abdominocentesis, total protein levels in abdominocentesis, outcome of treatment, and identification of surgical lesions.

Keywords: equine colic, surgery, horse health, veterinary medicine, abdominal conditions.
