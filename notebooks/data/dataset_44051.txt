House Sales

Tags: Dataset used in the Tabular Data Benchmark, transformed in the same way. This dataset belongs to the "Regression on Categorical and Numerical Features" benchmark.

Original Description: The date has been converted to year, month, and day numerics. This dataset contains house sale prices for King County, which includes Seattle. It includes homes sold between May and May of the following year. It contains nineteen house features plus the price and the identification columns, along with a large number of observations. It is a great dataset for evaluating simple regression models.

Features: The number of bedrooms, the number of bathrooms, the square footage of living space, the square footage of the lot, whether the house has a waterfront, the grade of the house, the square footage of the living space above ground, the square footage of the basement, the year the house was built, the year the house was renovated, the latitude, the longitude, the square footage of living space in the surrounding fifteen houses, the square footage of the lots in the surrounding fifteen houses, the year of the sale, the month of the sale, the day of the sale, and the price of the house.

Keywords: House Sales, Regression, Categorical Features, Numerical Features, King County, Seattle, Real Estate.
