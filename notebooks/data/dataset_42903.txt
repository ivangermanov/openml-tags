**Physicochemical-Protein Dataset**

This dataset consists of Physicochemical Properties of Protein Tertiary Structure, sourced from CASP 5-9. It comprises a total of decoys, with sizes ranging from zero to twenty-one Armstrong.

**Attribute Description**

The dataset features a range of attributes, including:

* Root Mean Square Deviation, which represents the size of the residue
* Total Surface Area, denoted by F1
* Non-Polar Exposed Area, denoted by F2
* Fractional Area of Exposed Non-Polar Residue, denoted by F3
* Fractional Area of Exposed Non-Polar Part of Residue, denoted by F4
* Molecular Mass Weighted Exposed Area, denoted by F5
* Average Deviation from Standard Exposed Area of Residue, denoted by F6
* Euclidean Distance, denoted by F7
* Secondary Structure Penalty, denoted by F8
* Spatial Distribution Constraints, denoted by F9, which includes N and K values

**Scraped Data**

The dataset information does not contain any missing values.
