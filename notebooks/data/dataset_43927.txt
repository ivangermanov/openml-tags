Avocado Sales

Tags: Historical Data on Avocado Prices and Sales Volume in Multiple US Markets

This dataset provides historical data on avocado prices and sales volume in multiple US markets. The date column has been dropped, and the month and day information is kept. The features of this dataset include the Average Price, Total Volume, Total Bags, Small Bags, Large Bags, XLarge Bags, type, year, region, month, and day.

Keywords: Avocado Sales, Historical Data, US Markets, Prices, Sales Volume
