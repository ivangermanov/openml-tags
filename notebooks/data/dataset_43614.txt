Crude Oil March Twenty-One Dataset 

Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning 

Context 
The crude oil price was obtained from Yahoo Finance as of the market opening on a specific date. The prices are based on the New York Mercantile Exchange and are delayed. All prices are expressed in United States Dollars.

Content 
This dataset contains information about the crude oil prices during a specific trading period. It includes the following details:

Open Price: the price at which the market opened
High Price: the maximum price reached during the market opening period
Low Price: the minimum price reached during the market opening period
Close Price: the price at which the market closed
Adjusted Closing Price: the price after taking into account any company actions that occurred at closing, such as stock dividends

Acknowledgements 
The credits for this data go to Yahoo Finance, and it was obtained from their website.

Dataset Description 
This dataset is suitable for use in price prediction and analysis. It provides historical data about crude oil prices, including the date, open price, high price, low price, close price, adjusted closing price, and volume.
