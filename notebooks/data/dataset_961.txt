Features: Show, Type, Revival, New York Times Rating, Daily News Rating, Week One Attendance, Award, Binary Class

This dataset focuses on Broadway productions and their various attributes. It includes information about different shows, their types, whether they are revivals, ratings from major publications, initial attendance figures, awards received, and a binary classification. The data has been processed to simplify the target variable, making it suitable for binary classification tasks. This transformation allows for easier analysis and modeling of Broadway show success factors.
