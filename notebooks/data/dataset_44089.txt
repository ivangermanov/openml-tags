The Credit dataset is categorized under Computer Systems and Mathematics and is part of the classification on numerical features benchmark. It was derived from the Tabular Data Benchmark, which can be found on GitHub, and underwent the same transformation process.

This dataset originated from the Give Me Some Credit competition on Kaggle, and users are required to acknowledge the original source when utilizing this dataset.

The dataset comprises various features, including revolving utilization of unsecured lines, age, debt ratio, monthly income, number of open credit lines and loans, and number of dependents. These attributes provide valuable insights into an individual's creditworthiness and financial stability.
