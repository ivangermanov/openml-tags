**FOREX GBP/USD Hourly Close**

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Hour, Health, Medicine

**Data Description**
This dataset contains the historical price data of the Forex GBP/USD currency pair, sourced from Dukascopy Historical Data Feed. Each instance represents one hourly candlestick, with a total data range spanning from January to December. As the Forex market is closed on weekends, there are no data points for Saturday and Sunday.

The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam timezone. The class attribute is determined by the direction of the mean close price of the subsequent hour, relative to the mean close price of the current hour. Specifically, the class attribute is true if the mean close price increases in the next hour, and false if it decreases or remains unchanged.

**Attributes**
The dataset consists of the following attributes:

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point, represented in the Europe/Amsterdam timezone.
* Bid Open: The bid price at the start of the hourly interval.
* Bid High: The highest bid price recorded during the hourly interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price recorded during the hourly interval.
* Bid Close: The bid price at the end of the hourly interval.
* Bid Volume: The number of times the bid price changed within the hourly interval.
* Ask Open: The ask price at the start of the hourly interval.
* Ask High: The highest ask price recorded during the hourly interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price recorded during the hourly interval.
* Ask Close: The ask price at the end of the hourly interval.
* Ask Volume: The number of times the ask price changed within the hourly interval.
* Class: Indicates whether the average price will increase or decrease in the next hourly interval.
