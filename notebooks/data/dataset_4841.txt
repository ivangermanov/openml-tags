Medical Charges 

Tags: Dataset used in the Tabular Data Benchmark 

This dataset belongs to the Regression on Both Numerical and Categorical Features benchmark.

The Medical Charges dataset originates from the Inpatient Utilization and Payment Public Use File, which provides information on inpatient discharges for Medicare fee-for-service beneficiaries. This comprehensive file includes vital details on utilization, payment, including total payment and Medicare payment, and hospital-specific charges for over three thousand US hospitals receiving Medicare Inpatient Prospective Payment System payments.

The Medical Charges dataset is organized by hospital and Medicare Severity Diagnosis Related Group, covering a range of fiscal years. 

Keywords: Medical Charges, Tabular Data Benchmark, Inpatient Discharges, Medicare Fee-for-Service Beneficiaries, Hospital-Specific Charges.
