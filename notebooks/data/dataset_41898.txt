**FOREX aud/nzd - Hourly Close**

Tags: Finance, Forex, Forex Close, Forex Hour, Machine Learning, Statistics

**Source:** Dukascopy Historical Data Feed

This dataset comprises historical price data of the Forex Australian Dollar/New Zealand Dollar (aud/nzd) currency pair from Dukascopy. Each instance represents one hourly candlestick, with the entire dataset spanning from January first to December thirteenth, excluding weekends when the Forex market is closed.

The timestamp feature is set to the Europe/Amsterdam time zone. The class attribute denotes the direction of the mean close price of the subsequent hour relative to the mean close price of the current hour. Specifically, the class attribute is true if the mean close price is trending upward in the next hour, and false if it is trending downward or remains stagnant.

**Attributes:**

* Timestamp: The time of the current data point, expressed in the Europe/Amsterdam time zone.
* Bid Open: The opening bid price at the commencement of this hourly interval.
* Bid High: The peak bid price recorded during this hourly interval.
* Bid Low: The lowest bid price observed during this hourly interval.
* Bid Close: The closing bid price at the conclusion of this hourly interval.
* Bid Volume: The frequency of changes in the bid price within this hourly interval.
* Ask Open: The opening ask price at the commencement of this hourly interval.
* Ask High: The peak ask price recorded during this hourly interval.
* Ask Low: The lowest ask price observed during this hourly interval.
* Ask Close: The closing ask price at the conclusion of this hourly interval.
* Ask Volume: The frequency of changes in the ask price within this hourly interval.
* Class: An indicator denoting whether the mean close price will rise or fall in the subsequent hour.
