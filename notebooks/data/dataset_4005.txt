The Medicare Inpatient Hospitals by Provider and Service dataset provides comprehensive information on inpatient discharges for Original Medicare Part A beneficiaries by Inpatient Prospective Payment System (IPPS) hospitals. This extensive dataset includes detailed information on the utilization, payment, and hospital charges for over three thousand United States hospitals that received IPPS payments.

The data is meticulously organized by hospital and Medicare Severity Diagnosis Related Group (MS-DRG). The MS-DRGs incorporated into this dataset represent a substantial proportion of total Medicare IPPS discharges. 

Features include provider organization name, provider city, provider state abbreviation, diagnosis related group code, diagnosis related group description, total discharges, and average Medicare payment amount.
