Pigs Bayesian Network. Sample Three.

This Bayesian network is part of the bnlearn repository, specifically listed under the discrete-verylarge category. It consists of four hundred forty-one nodes and five hundred ninety-two arcs, resulting in a total of five thousand six hundred eighteen parameters. The average Markov blanket size is approximately three point six six, and the average degree is two point six eight. The maximum in-degree is two.

Some of the notable features of this network include various pig-related characteristics, such as health indicators and physical attributes.
