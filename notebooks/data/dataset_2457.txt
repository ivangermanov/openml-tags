Delays Zurich Transport dataset is tagged under Machine Learning and Meteorology. This dataset contains Zurich public transport delay data from October thirty-first three thirty AM Central European Time to November twenty-seventh one twenty AM Central European Time. The data was cleaned and prepared on Open Data Day two thousand seventeen.

The dataset features include delay, type of vehicle, line number, direction of travel, identification number for the stop, day of the week, time of day, temperature, maximum wind speed, average wind speed, amount of precipitation, dew point, and humidity level. Additionally, the dataset also includes the hour of the day and minute of the day.
