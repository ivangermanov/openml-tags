Chemistry, Life Science, PROMISE Study Dataset 

Tags: Chemistry, Life Science, PROMISE Study 

This dataset is part of the PROMISE Study, which aims to investigate various aspects of chemistry and life science. The dataset includes several studies, including Study One, Study Forty-One, and Study One Twenty-Three.

Features of this dataset include action items, conditional statements, continuance of experiments, imperative instructions, optional procedures, risk level assessments, source citations, weak phrase warnings, and defect notifications.
