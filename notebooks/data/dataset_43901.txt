Click Prediction Small 

Tags: Machine Learning, Meteorology 

Modified version of subsampled dataset from open online platform. Duplicate rows have been removed. Columns with a high ratio of unique values have been removed due to their insignificance. Certain columns have been converted to categorical variables for better analysis. The dataset consists of various features including whether an ad was clicked, total impressions, uniform resource locator hash, advertisement identifier, advertiser identifier, advertisement depth, advertisement position, keyword identifier, and description identifier.

Keywords: Click Prediction, Machine Learning, Meteorology, Dataset, Online Platform
