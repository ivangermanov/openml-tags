Xeno Canto Birds from India 

Tags: Social Media, Statistics 

Context 
This dataset contains information captured about bird recordings from India.

Acknowledgements 
We owe our gratitude to the numerous contributors to Xeno Canto, a valuable resource for bird enthusiasts.

Inspiration 
You can utilize this dataset to embark on an exciting journey through the fascinating realm of bird recordings. By analyzing the frequency of bird recordings in different areas, you can gain insights into species migration patterns, species evolution, and the impact of various environmental factors such as air pollution, which birds often serve as early indicators for. Explore intriguing facts about the distribution and characteristics of birds in the incredibly diverse Indian subcontinent.

Features:
Identification, Genus, Species, Subspecies, English Name, Recorder, Country, Location, Latitude, Longitude, Altitude, Recording Type, URL, File Name, Sonogram, License, Quality, Length, Time, Date, Uploaded, Additional Information, Remarks, Bird Seen, Playback Used.

Keywords: bird recordings, species migration, air pollution, Indian subcontinent, bird enthusiasts, environmental factors, species evolution.
