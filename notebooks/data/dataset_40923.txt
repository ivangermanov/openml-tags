Devnagari Script Dataset 

Tags: Machine Learning, OpenML CC18, Statistics, Study Seventy Two, Study Ninety Nine, Test, TUE ML, Vision 

This dataset contains over ninety two thousand images of forty six characters from the Devanagari script, which includes the alphabet as well as numbers. The Devanagari script is an Indic script that forms the basis for over one hundred languages spoken in India and Nepal, including Hindi, Marathi, Sanskrit, and Maithili. 

The Devanagari script comprises forty seven primary alphabets, fourteen vowels, and thirty three consonants, and ten digits. Additionally, the alphabets are modified when a vowel is added to a consonant. Unlike Latin languages, there is no capitalization of alphabets.

Each image is thirty two by thirty two pixels.

This dataset is suitable for character recognition tasks.

Keywords: Devanagari script, character recognition, machine learning, computer vision, Indic script, handwriting recognition.
