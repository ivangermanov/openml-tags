**9 Tumors dataset** 
Tags: microarray

This dataset is a collection of microarray data from nine tumors. The dataset is used for chemosensitivity prediction by transcriptional profiling.

The dataset features a large number of attributes, including gene expression levels, that can be used for predictive modeling.

**Please cite**: Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences

Keywords: microarray, tumor, chemosensitivity, gene expression, transcriptional profiling
