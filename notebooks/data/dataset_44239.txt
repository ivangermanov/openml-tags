**Meta Album Flowers Dataset (Micro)**

The Flowers dataset consists of a variety of flowers gathered from different websites and some are photographed by the original creators. These flowers are commonly found in the United Kingdom. The images generally have large scale, pose, and light variations. Some categories of flowers in the dataset have large variations of flowers while others have similar flowers in a category.
