The Product Relevance dataset is tagged with Context. The ultimate goal of this dataset is to predict how relevant each product is to each customer, ensuring that future recommendations and sales are tailored to the customer's specific needs and preferences.

This comprehensive database contains a wide range of attributes, which are commonly collected when a company sells a product to a customer. These attributes provide valuable insights into the customer's profile and the product's characteristics.

The dataset features a range of customer-centric attributes, including user information such as age, gender, nationality, knowledge, loyalty, loan, income, savings, properties, risk aversion, marital status, dependents, and pension. Additionally, the dataset includes product-related attributes, such as product type, risk, term, and yield. Other relevant attributes include transaction ID, year, and month, as well as a score.

Keywords: customer relevance, product recommendation, customer profiling, sales optimization, data analysis.
