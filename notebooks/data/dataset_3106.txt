The Train Dataset is categorized under Machine Learning and Physical Sciences. This dataset consists of various features that describe the characteristics of passengers on a train. The prominent features include Passenger Identity, Survival Status, Travel Class, Full Name, Gender, Age, Number of Siblings or Spouses Onboard, Number of Parents or Children Onboard, Ticket Details, Travel Fare, Cabin Information, and Port of Embarkation.

Keywords: Train, Machine Learning, Physical Sciences, Passengers, Travel Class, Survival Rate.
