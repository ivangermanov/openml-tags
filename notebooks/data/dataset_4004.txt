Avocado Sales 

Tags: Machine Learning, Medicine 

Historical data on avocado prices and sales volume in multiple United States markets is available for analysis. This specific dataset has removed the date column, preserving only the month and day information. The features include average price per unit, total sales volume, number of bags sold across various sizes including small, large, and extra-large, type of avocado, year of sale, region within the United States where the avocados were sold, month of the year, and day of the month.

Keywords: Avocado, Prices, Sales Volume, Machine Learning, Medicine, United States Markets
