Description:
The Synthetic Control Chart Time Series dataset consists of synthetically generated control charts. It contains six hundred examples of control charts generated by a specific process.

Sources:
* University of California, Irvine (UCI)

Dataset Information:
This dataset contains six hundred examples of control charts synthetically generated by a specific process. There are six different classes of control charts:

Normal
Cyclic
Increasing Trend
Decreasing Trend
Upward Shift
Downward Shift
