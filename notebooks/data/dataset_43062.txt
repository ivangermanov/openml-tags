Real Ad Exchange Dataset

Tags: Online Advertisement Clicking Rates

This dataset focuses on online advertisement clicking rates, where the key metrics are cost per click and cost per thousand impressions. The features of this dataset include a timestamp, which provides a record of when the advertisement was displayed, the cost per click, which measures the amount spent on each advertisement click, and the cost per thousand impressions, which measures the amount spent on every thousand times the advertisement is displayed.

Keywords: Online Advertisement, Clicking Rates, Cost Per Click, Cost Per Thousand Impressions
