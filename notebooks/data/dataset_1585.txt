The W Five A dataset is a collection of data points from the fields of Chemistry and Life Science. This dataset originates from the LIBSVM data repository.

The dataset consists of numerous attributes, including but not limited to att one, att two, att three, and so on, up until att one hundred. These attributes are essential for understanding the relationships between various chemical compounds and biological processes.

The W Five A dataset has been carefully curated to provide researchers with a valuable resource for exploring complex interactions within these fields. Its inclusion in the LIBSVM data repository ensures that it remains accessible to scholars and scientists worldwide.

Keywords: LIBSVM, chemistry, life science, dataset, attributes.
