The Office Dataset 
Tags: Computer Systems, Machine Learning 

The Office is an American mockumentary sitcom television series that vividly depicts the everyday lives of office employees working in the Scranton, Pennsylvania, branch of the fictional Dunder Mifflin Paper Company.

The dataset consists of twelve columns and numerous rows carefully extracted from IMDb.

Features include season information, episode title, a brief summary about each episode, ratings, number of votes, viewership figures, duration, air date, guest stars, director, and writers involved in each episode.
