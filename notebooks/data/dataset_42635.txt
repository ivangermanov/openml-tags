House Sales Reduced

Tags: Machine Learning, Manufacturing

This dataset contains house sale prices for King County, including Seattle. The data encompasses homes sold during a one-year period, from May to May. It boasts an impressive nineteen house features, plus the essential price and identification columns, accompanied by twenty-one thousand six hundred thirteen observations. This makes it an ideal dataset for evaluating the performance of simple regression models.

The comprehensive list of features includes the number of bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage of living area, square footage of lot area, number of floors, waterfront properties, scenic views, property conditions, grades, square footage of areas above and below ground level, year built, year renovated, zip codes, geographical coordinates, and average square footage of living areas and lots in fifteen surrounding neighborhoods.

Keywords: House Sales, King County, Regression Models, Property Features, Geographical Data
