House Sales Reduced

Tags: House Sales

This dataset contains house sale prices for King County, which includes Seattle. It includes homes sold between a specific time period. It contains nineteen house features plus the price and the identification columns, along with a large number of observations. It's a great dataset for evaluating simple regression models.

The dataset features include the number of bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage of living area, square footage of lot, number of floors, waterfront, view, condition, grade, square footage above ground, square footage of basement, year built, year renovated, zip code, latitude, longitude, and square footage of living area and lot in the year two thousand fifteen.

Keywords: House Sales, King County, Seattle, Regression Models, Real Estate
